  Multi-vendor: HPCToolkit

  Deep analysis: Wireshark (free, comprehensive)
  Lightweight: tcpdump (free, CLI)
  Flow monitoring: ManageEngine NetFlow (commercial)
  Web debugging: Fiddler (commercial)

Distributed System Profilers:
  Self-hosted: Jaeger (free, easy deployment)
  Commercial: DataDog APM, New Relic
  Framework: OpenTelemetry (vendor-neutral)

Real-time Profilers:
  Python: py-spy (<5% overhead, production-safe)
  Linux: Linux perf (<1% overhead, system-wide)
  Distributed: Parca, Pixie (eBPF-based)
