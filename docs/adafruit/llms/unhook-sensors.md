# Furby 2012 Teardown

## Unplug wires

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/002/368/medium800/projects_PA102029.jpg?1396782365)

The belly sensor is attached with a tiny plug. Unplug it.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/002/369/medium800/projects_PA102030.jpg?1396782390)

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/002/483/medium800/projects_PA102044.jpg?1396783603)

The speaker (brown wires) can be unplugged from its circuit board.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/002/484/medium800/projects_PA102045.jpg?1396783629)

The microphone (inside the pink circle) can be removed from its nesting spot in the front of Furby's body.
