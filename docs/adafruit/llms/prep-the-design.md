# EL Wire Sign

## Prep the Design

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/961/medium800/el_wire_tape_panel_lining_up_board_for_projection.jpg?1396777853)

Start by projecting your design onto the black poster board. Once you get it lined up, tape it in place and trace the design by poking holes&nbsp;every inch or so... We used an awl but even a ballpoint pen would work.&nbsp;Don't worry if you poke too many or mess up- you won't be able to see these when it's all done.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/002/126/medium800/el_wire_tape_panel_alienpoke_holes.jpg?1396779615)
