# Update Billing Details

1. To update a billing details, go to your workspace's [Plan & Billing page](https://app.roboflow.com/the-analytics-zone/settings/plan) and select "Go to Portal".

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-83bc01e96fd9204dc26f359be6f6fb1b0f84d019%2Fimage.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. In the billing portal, select "Update Information"
3. Update all of the fields you would like to change and click "Save".
