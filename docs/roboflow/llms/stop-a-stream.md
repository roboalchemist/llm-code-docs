# Pause and Resume a Stream

You can pause a running Workflow on a video stream from the Deployment Manager dashboard.

To stop a Stream, click on a Device in Deployment Manager, then click on the three dots next to the Stream you want to stop in your Stream list.

To pause a running stream, click "Pause":

To resume a paused stream, click "Resume":
