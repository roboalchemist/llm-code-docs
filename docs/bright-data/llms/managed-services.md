# Managed Services

Managed Services is a fully-managed data collection solution for organizations that require custom web data extraction. If you can't find a pre-built scraper in the Scraper Library that meets your specific requirements, or if you need a tailored data collection workflow for your unique use case, Managed Services provides end-to-end support. Our team handles scraper development, data collection, quality assurance, and delivery, allowing you to obtain the exact data you need without building or maintaining the infrastructure yourself.

## What are Managed Services?

Managed Services is Bright Data's premium offering for organizations that need enterprise-grade data collection without dedicating internal resources to web scraping operations. This fully managed solution combines our cutting-edge technology, extensive proxy network, and expert team to deliver clean, structured, and validated data at any scale.

Unlike self-service tools or DIY scraping solutions, Managed Services provides:

* Dedicated project management from experienced data professionals
* Custom scraper development tailored to your exact requirements
* Complete infrastructure management including proxies, anti-bot handling, and performance optimization
* Quality assurance and validation to ensure data accuracy and completeness
* Ongoing maintenance and support to adapt to website changes and evolving needs
* Flexible control options - go fully hands-off or trigger collections on-demand via API

## Get Started with Managed Services

Ready to unlock enterprise-grade data collection without the operational burden? Requesting a Managed Services scraper is simple and takes just a few minutes.

### How to Request a Fully Managed Scraper

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Control Panel">
    Log in to your Bright Data account and navigate to the My Scrapers section from the left sidebar menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request a Fully Managed Scraper">
    Click the "New" button in the top right corner, then select "Request a fully managed scraper" from the dropdown menu.

    <Frame>
            <img src="https://mintcdn.com/brightdata/7H2NC5jfr-NiKDmZ/images/datasets/scrapers/managed-services/new-button.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=7H2NC5jfr-NiKDmZ&q=85&s=1d484655481b0a2f979ecd2cdd866ea1" alt="New Button" width="624" height="303" data-path="images/datasets/scrapers/managed-services/new-button.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Your Requirements">
    In the request form, enter your target website URL and provide detailed information about your data collection needs. The more details you share, the faster our team can assist you.

    Include information such as:

    * Target website(s)
    * Specific data fields you need
    * Delivery frequency and format
    * Any special requirements or challenges

    <Frame>
            <img src="https://mintcdn.com/brightdata/7H2NC5jfr-NiKDmZ/images/datasets/scrapers/managed-services/managed-services-request-form.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=7H2NC5jfr-NiKDmZ&q=85&s=50b60d6ae6099a9c00ac79aa91cdf5f8" alt="Managed Services Request Form" width="624" height="303" data-path="images/datasets/scrapers/managed-services/managed-services-request-form.png" />

  <Step title="Submit and Connect with Our Team">
    Click "Submit" to send your request. Our Managed Services team will review your requirements and contact you within 24 hours to discuss your project and provide a custom proposal.

Start your Managed Services journey today and let our experts handle the complexity while you focus on the insights that drive your business forward.
