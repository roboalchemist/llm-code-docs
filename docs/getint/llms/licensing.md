# Understanding Getint Licensing and Pricing

Welcome to our comprehensive guide on Getint’s licensing and payment structures. Our model is designed to be **straightforward, flexible**, and tailored to meet your integration and migration needs.

### **Integrations**

#### **Jira-Related Integrations**

We primarily distribute our apps through the Atlassian Marketplace for integrations involving Jira. We also offer a direct purchase option for customers who cannot access the Marketplace.

#### **License Per Integration**

* Each Jira integration requires a single license.
* For example:
  * Integrating **1x Jira with 1x ServiceNow** requires **one license**.
  * A **Jira-to-Jira integration** needs **two licenses**: one for each Jira instance. Alternatively, you can use a **regular license** for one Jira and a **remote license** for the other. Learn more [here](https://docs.getint.io/getting-started-with-the-platform/integration-synchronization/jira-jira-integration/jira-jira-licensing).

### **Why Choose Getint for Jira Integrations?**

* **Single App, Single Payment:**

Set everything up in one centralized place. Unlike Exalate and Backbone, Getint eliminates the need for additional installations or payments at both ends.

* **No Additional Synchronizers:**

Avoid the hassle of standalone synchronizers like TFS4JIRA.

* **Unlimited Synchronization:**

One license allows you to synchronize an unlimited number of projects, fields, and tasks between two instances.

*(Compare with Unito’s usage-based pricing, where costs scale with the number of integrations.)*

### **Pricing Structure**

* Pricing for Jira integrations is based on:
* **Jira version** (Cloud, Server, or Data Center)
* **User count** on the instance

Pricing adheres to Atlassian Marketplace guidelines. View our apps and pricing [here](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/vendors/1218845).

### **Non-Jira Integrations**

For tools like Asana, Azure DevOps, ServiceNow, Zendesk, Wrike, GitLab, GitHub, and Trello (without Jira involvement), we offer a **simple, fixed-fee structure**:

* **One license covers integration** between 1x Instance A (e.g., Asana) and 1x Instance B (e.g., Azure DevOps).
* No hidden costs—just seamless connectivity. Learn more about pricing [here](https://www.getint.io/pricing).

### **Special Licensing Options**

#### **Network License**

Ideal for organizations managing **multiple integrations**, such as:

* **3x Jira with 2x ServiceNow**
* **4x Asana instances**

Benefit from **special network pricing** for cost-effective scalability. [Learn more about network licenses](https://docs.getint.io/support-legal-and-others/licensing/network-license).

#### **Migrations**

Migrations are often **short-term, high-intensity projects** designed to transfer large volumes of data efficiently.

* **Pricing:** Based on the data volume being migrated, ensure value-based pricing.
* Once migration is complete, you can discontinue using the tool.

Learn more about migration pricing [here](https://www.getint.io/pricing).

### **Getting Started with Getint**

To begin, you only need:

* A license for the dedicated Jira app from the Atlassian Marketplace, or
* A direct license from Getint.

Our simple licensing approach eliminates the need for multiple app purchases or installations across different tools. With just **one platform license**, you’re ready to streamline your integrations and migrations.
