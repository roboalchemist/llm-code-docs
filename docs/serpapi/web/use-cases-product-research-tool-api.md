Product Research Tool
=====================

Discover and analyze products across e-commerce platforms in real time. Scrape structured data such as prices, ratings, reviews, and availability from Google Shopping, Amazon, and more.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Get started for free](https://serpapi.com/users/sign_up?plan=free)

### Google Shopping

Get structured product data directly from Google Shopping — including live prices, availability, ratings, and reviews. Perfect for tracking competitors, comparing offers, and identifying trending products in any market.

**Documentation:**[](https://serpapi.com/google-shopping-api)[](https://serpapi.com/google-immersive-product-api)

![Image 42: Google Shopping API response sample](https://serpapi.com/assets/use_cases/google_shopping-baa2c288740e8eef93b59824aed7a5d4706620558bb91eed1fb29315e1e12698.jpg)

### Amazon Search Results

Extract product insights from Amazon search results — access detailed data such as prices, stock status, ratings, and customer reviews to analyze competition, monitor trends, or build product research dashboards.

**Documentation:**[](https://serpapi.com/amazon-search-api)

![Image 43: Amazon search API response sample](https://serpapi.com/assets/use_cases/amazon_search-4f84f03fed9dbb59bddc580fd70fae90feacc4985f9aea32f1896de565f2ce6d.jpg)

### Full Market Overview

Collect comprehensive product information from various e-commerce platforms such as eBay, Walmart, Home Depot, and Bing Shopping to stay updated on the latest market trends.

**Documentation:**[![Image 44](https://serpapi.com/demo-redesign/icons/static/ebay-gray.svg?version=1.2)](https://serpapi.com/ebay-search-api)[![Image 45](https://serpapi.com/demo-redesign/icons/static/walmart-gray.svg)](https://serpapi.com/walmart-search-api)[![Image 46](https://serpapi.com/demo-redesign/icons/static/bing-gray.svg)](https://serpapi.com/bing-shopping-api)[![Image 47](https://serpapi.com/demo-redesign/icons/static/home_depot-gray.svg?version=1.3)](https://serpapi.com/home-depot-search-api)

### Get reviews from YouTube

Find video reviews of products on YouTube to gain insights into customer opinions and experiences. Analyze video content to enhance your product research and stay ahead of market trends.

**Documentation:**[](https://serpapi.com/youtube-search-api)[](https://serpapi.com/youtube-video-transcript)
