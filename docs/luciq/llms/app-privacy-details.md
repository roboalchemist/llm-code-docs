# App Privacy Details

As of December 8th, apps submitted to the App Store are required to submit privacy details about their app as mentioned [here](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/app-privacy-details/). This page contains more details about the data collected by Luciq in relation to the aforementioned privacy details.
