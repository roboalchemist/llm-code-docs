# General

The following fields are general to this job entry:

* **Name**: Specify the unique name of the job entry on the canvas. You can customize the name or leave it as the default.
* **Advanced Options**: Click the **Advanced Options** link to switch to the **Advanced Options** mode. You can click the **Quick Setup** link in the **Advanced Options** mode to switch to the **Quick Setup** mode.
