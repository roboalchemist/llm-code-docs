# Delete a Schedule

After you have scheduled a report, you can easily delete the schedule without deleting the report from the Pentaho User Console.

1. Click the **Home** drop-down menu on the upper-left and click the link to **Schedules**.

   The list of schedules appears on the Schedules page.
2. Click the schedule that you want to delete in the list.

   The schedule is highlighted.
3. Click **Remove** in the scheduler toolbar.

   The schedule is deleted, leaving the report intact.

The previously scheduled report no longer runs at the specified interval.
