# Source: https://github.com/clerk/clerk-docs/blob/main/docs/_tooltips/publishable-key.mdx

Your Clerk **Publishable Key** tells your app what your FAPI URL is, enabling your app to locate and communicate with your dedicated FAPI instance. You can find it on the [**API keys**](https://dashboard.clerk.com/~/api-keys) page in the Clerk Dashboard.
