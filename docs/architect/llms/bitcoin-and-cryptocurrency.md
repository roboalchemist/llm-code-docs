# Bitcoin & Cryptocurrency

Digital assets or cryptocurrencies, like Bitcoin (BTC) and Ethereum (ETH), are the underlying asset for some US futures. It's worth noting that the underlying spot cryptocurrency markets are not generally regulated in the same way like US futures exchanges are, and this asset class carries higher risks and volatility. \
