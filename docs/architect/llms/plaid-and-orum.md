# Plaid & Orum

We're partnering with [Plaid ](https://plaid.com/)and [Orum](https://www.orum.io/) to streamline transfers between your bank and your trading account. Both are industry leaders in facilitating safe and secure transactions.&#x20;

Linking your bank account with Architect is the easiest way to Deposit and Withdraw money.&#x20;
