Symfony Releases
================

Symfony releases follow a time-based model: minor versions come out every six months (in May and November); major versions come out every two years. Check out the [release process details](https://symfony.com/doc/current/contributing/community/releases.html).

[Stable Release](https://symfony.com/releases#stable-release "Permanent link to this headline")
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[8.0.7](https://symfony.com/releases/8.0 "Symfony 8.0 release details")

* Requires **PHP 8.4.0** or higher
* First released in November 2025
* **Recommended for most users**
* Includes the latest features
* It's easier to upgrade to newer versions

TYPO3 has been certified as a digital public good by the Digital Public Goods Alliance, bringing a trusted CMS platform to the broader PHP and Symfony ecosystem.

Mailtrap is a platform for testing and delivering emails, designed to support modern development workflows and production-grade sending. It offers secure sandboxes, email APIs, and monitoring tools for reliable email delivery.
