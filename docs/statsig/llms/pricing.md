# WHN Usage-Based Pricing Model

<Info>
  Applies to Warehouse Native customers on the new usage-based pricing model only.

  The instructions below describe how to view credit usage under Statsig's new pricing.
</Info>

*Note: Images below are from a sample account. Your view may differ based on your rate and usage. Credit usage may take up to 2 days to reflect in your usage dashboard.*

You can view credit usage in two places:

* The **Statsig Console**
* The **Orb billing portal**

Both show the same view of your total credit balance and usage by SKU.

## Statsig Console

Use the Statsig Console to view credit usage.

1. Log in to the [Statsig Console](https://console.statsig.com/)
2. Open **Settings**
3. Go to **Manage Account → Usage & Billing**
4. At the top of the page, you'll see your total credit balance
5. Below the total, you can view credit usage by SKU, along with current and past invoice and usage details (similar view to Orb)

## Orb Portal

Use Orb to view credit usage and automated alerts.

<Info>
  Orb is used for visibility and alerts only. You will not be auto-charged or
  invoiced through Orb. Any credit top-ups are handled directly by the Statsig
  team.
</Info>

1. **Log into your Orb account**

2. **View your total credit balance** - Go to **Account Information** to see your total credit balance.

   <img src="https://mintcdn.com/statsig-4b2ff144/PId4A4dnRzOQR82w/images/credit_balance_image_2.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=PId4A4dnRzOQR82w&q=85&s=26d7e7d5f2735fd5c359570a02ce21b9" alt="Credit balance in Orb" width="567" height="66" data-path="images/credit_balance_image_2.png" />

   Click **"Credit block detail"** to view:

   * Original credit balance
   * Remaining balance
   * Credit expiration date

3. **View usage by SKU** - Under upcoming invoices, you can view how credits are being consumed across different metrics/SKUs.

   Click **"Show usage details"** to view:

   * Total credit usage by SKU
   * Daily credit drawdown over time

   <img src="https://mintcdn.com/statsig-4b2ff144/EecbNM_i7Vd9Qgy3/images/daily_credit_drawdown_2.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=EecbNM_i7Vd9Qgy3&q=85&s=95446e57ff365394ea8398c9bd8164ba" alt="Daily credit drawdown" width="1101" height="429" data-path="images/daily_credit_drawdown_2.png" />

4. **Experiment Analysis Unit (EAU) usage**

   EAUs are billed when an experiment ends. As a result, EAU usage may appear as a single spike rather than daily usage.
