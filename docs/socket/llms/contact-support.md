# Contact support

Customers can contact us from any page on Socket where the support widget button is seen, making it as easy as possible to get into contact with us, without ever leaving the page. We use [HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com/) to handle all these support inquiries.

Simply click on the bubble at the bottom right of any site on the [main socket.dev](https://socket.dev/) site and the widget will launch.

In the widget you'll be able to see previous conversations with our Support Engineers or send us a new message.

If a Support Engineer is unavailable, or if you're not on Socket when our team replies, we'll send a copy of your conversation to the email address used to log into your Socket account. Feel free to respond using the HubSpot widget or reply via email -- whichever method is more convenient for you!

## 🗑️ GDPR & Data Deletion Requests

If you’d like your data deleted from Socket—whether for GDPR or other privacy reasons—just email [support@socket.dev](mailto:support@socket.dev) with:
• Your Socket organization name
• The email address you used to sign up for Socket

We’ll delete the associated organization and all related data from our systems.
