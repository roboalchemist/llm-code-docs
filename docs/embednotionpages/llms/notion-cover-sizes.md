# Notion Cover Size & Dimensions

Choosing the right size makes your Notion page clean and sharp.

![Recommended Notion cover dimensions](https://framerusercontent.com/images/TDB946QfLCfDsPcpm3Ix1Kmrn1k.png)

## Recommended Notion cover image sizes by device

- **Desktop**: 1500×600 px (5:2 aspect ratio) – Best for desktops and laptops.
- **Tablet**: 1170×290 px (~4:1 aspect ratio) – Good for tablets like iPads.
- **Mobile**: 1170×445 px (~2.6:1 aspect ratio) – Fits smartphones.

Use a 1500×600 px cover for all devices. Keep key parts within 1170×230 px.

## How to add and customize a cover image in Notion

![Adding a cover in Notion interface](https://framerusercontent.com/images/WcJZPYjz8ggypsrTWnQfboqRSYo.png)

Adding a cover makes your page look polished and lively.

1. Open your Notion page.
2. Click "Add Cover."
3. Hover and click "Change Cover."
4. Pick an image from:
   - Notion's gallery
   - Your own uploads
   - A pasted link
   - [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/)
5. Click "Reposition" to move the image.
6. Click "Save Position" when done.

## Tips for creating custom Notion covers

Custom covers let you match your style and needs.

- Use [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) to design covers.
- Or try [Notion Cover Generator](https://notioncovergenerator.com/).
- Match canvas size to device.
- Keep files under 150 KB.
- Use 72 DPI for screens.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the ideal aspect ratio?

Use 5:2, e.g., 6000x2400 px, 3000x1200 px, or 1500×600 px.

### Why is my cover blurry?

It is likely too small. Use at least 1500 px wide.

### Can I resize a cover in Notion?

No. Resize before uploading.
