# Manage Your Wallet

To use the platform, Cloud Hosts must connect a crypto wallet and complete KYC verification.

  * Wallet connection is required _before_ onboarding any GPUs.

  * KYC verification is required _before_ claiming or withdrawing any earnings.

![](https://docs.aethir.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3028335560-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FlJdZs7NyMJ6Ewm4U1eRP%252Fuploads%252FQ8cYrKnznFb2yFMfvSyT%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D465329e4-0ab1-442a-a2c3-9dc20cfbfb51&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=b9f52997&sv=2)

### 

hashtag

Connect Wallet

Cloud Hosts must connect a crypto wallet before staking GPUs. 

circle-exclamation

**Note** : Once connected to the platform, the wallet address **cannot** be changed.

### 

hashtag

KYC Verification

To comply with AML (Anti-Money Laundering) regulations, all users must complete KYC verification before they can claim or withdraw ATH earned through our products.

#### 

hashtag

Steps to Complete KYC

  1. Click the **Complete KYC Now** button on the banner.

  2. Follow the instructions in the **KYC panel**.

  3. To review KYC status, visit the **Settings** page or click the **avatar** in the top-right corner of any page.

For more details, refer to the [Complete KYC Verification](/aethir-tokenomics/complete-kyc-verfication) page.

### 

hashtag

Manage Earnings

The top two rows of the Wallet page show ATH balances from compute activities:

  * [Service Fees](/aethir-cloud/aethir-cloud-host/rewards-and-service-fees-for-cloud-hosts/service-fees-for-cloud-host) – Paid by tenants for using your computational resources.

  * [Rewards](/aethir-cloud/aethir-cloud-host/rewards-and-service-fees-for-cloud-hosts/rewards-for-cloud-host) – Incentives based on:

    * Proof of Capacity (PoC): Servers online with sufficient uptime.

    * Proof of Delivery (PoD): Successful task completion.

#### 

hashtag

Earning Lifecycle

**Vesting Claim**

Earnings are locked during vesting.

**Claimable**

Vesting ends; ready to be claimed.

**Vesting Withdraw**

Claimed, pending 1-day vesting before withdrawal.

**Withdrawable**

Can be withdrawn to wallet.

**Cash Out**

Funds have been transferred to your wallet.

#### 

hashtag

Claim Schedule

  * **Service Fees** : Claimable after 45 days.

  * **Rewards** :

    * 30% claimable immediately

    * 30% after 90 days

    * 40% after 180 days

  * Earnings update daily at 00:00 UTC. Portal data may slightly lag.

#### 

hashtag

How to Claim

  1. Go to **Wallet**.

  2. Click **Claim All** under **Service Fees** /**Rewards**.

  3. Confirm the action on the popup panel.

  4. Approve the transaction in your wallet.

circle-info

**Limits**

  * Max 1 claim per UTC day per type.

  * Min 30 ATH per claim per type.

#### 

hashtag

How to Withdraw

  1. Go to **Wallet**.

  2. Click the **Arrow** icon under **Withdrawable**.

  3. In the list, withdraw oldest transactions first.

  4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

### 

hashtag

Manage Stakings

The bottom row on the Wallet page shows ATH balances related to [Staking](/staking-and-rewards-for-cloud-host-compute-providers/staking-as-a-cloud-host).

  * **Staked** – ATH locked for GPU earnings.

  * **Unstaking Vesting** – Unstaking initiated; in the 180-day unlock period.

  * **Unstaking Withdrawable** – Unlock period completed; ready to withdraw.

  * **Unstaked** – Funds withdrawn to your wallet.

You can stake/unstake on the [Aethir Earth](/aethir-cloud/aethir-cloud-host/cloud-host-portal-guide/how-to-provide-aethir-earth-ai#how-to-stake) or [Aethir Atmosphere](/aethir-cloud/aethir-cloud-host/cloud-host-portal-guide/how-to-provide-aethir-atmosphere-cloud-gaming) page.

#### 

hashtag

How to Withdraw Unstaked ATH

  1. Go to **Wallet**.

  2. Click the **Arrow** next to **Unstaking (Vesting / Withdrawable)**.

  3. In the list, click **Withdraw All**.

  4. Confirm in your wallet.
