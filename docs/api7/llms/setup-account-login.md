# Setup Account and Login

info

**API7 Cloud is accepting beta applicants for early access. Thank you for filling out the form. We will be in touch with you soon.**

Once you have your API7 Cloud account, you can access the [console](https://console.api7.cloud), and our system will direct you to the login page.

![login](https://static.api7.ai/2022/12/30/login.png)

After you log in successfully, API7 Cloud will redirect you to the console. You may see "Please wait for a while. Your service is being deployed" when you're on the overview page. Kindly wait for a while, and API7 Cloud will complete the provision soon if this happens.

Note if this is your first time signing in to the console, API7 Cloud will ask you to input the organization name.

![Initialize the Organization](https://static.api7.ai/2022/12/30/init-organization.png)
