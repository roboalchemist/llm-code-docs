Vectara trial
=============

The Vectara trial offers access to all features for 30 days, allowing you to explore how our Agent OS for the enterprise can transform your AI and search capabilities.

Getting started with your trial[​](https://docs.vectara.com/docs/vectara-trial#getting-started-with-your-trial "Direct link to Getting started with your trial")
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Your Vectara trial begins upon account registration and lasts for 30 days. During this trial period, you can use Vectara’s powerful agent and query capabilities, generative models, and rerankers to see how our platform can meet your business needs.

note

If you signed up through the AWS Marketplace, your trial follows the same process.

Trial quotas and usage limits[​](https://docs.vectara.com/docs/vectara-trial#trial-quotas-and-usage-limits "Direct link to Trial quotas and usage limits")
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Vectara trial includes 10,000 credits.

For more details on our offerings, see [**Pricing**](https://vectara.com/pricing/).
