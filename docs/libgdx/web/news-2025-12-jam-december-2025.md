libGDX Jam December 2025
========================

December 14, 2025[![Image 3: raeleus](https://avatars2.githubusercontent.com/raeleus?v=3&s=20)](https://github.com/raeleus) raeleus

With our 35th collaboration, the [libGDX Jam](https://libgdx.com/community/jams/) continues the time-honoured tradition of making awesome games using the best framework out there. We encourage camaraderie, teamwork, and good sportsmanship with a side of well-intentioned buffoonery. The general jam rules can as always be found [here](https://libgdx.com/community/jams/#rules). **To participate take a look at the official [itch.io page of the jam](https://itch.io/jam/libgdx-jam-35).** The humorous jam trailer can be found on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=flOSpVhL0pI).

Be sure to get involved with the community by joining the libGDX Discord server!

Schedule
--------

The theme is **BRIDGES**. Good luck everybody!

*   **Theme Submission:** 30th of November - 2nd of December. Everyone will be allowed to submit a theme for the jam.
*   **Voting:** 4th - 6th of December. Everyone will vote on their favourite theme. May the best win!
*   **Jam:** 7th - 13th of December. Participants will create their games and rejoice!

**Updated:**December 14, 2025
