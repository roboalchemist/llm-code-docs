# Account Preferences

Use the account menu to access personal settings, connected services, and account-level actions.

### Locate the account menu

The account menu is available from anywhere in the portal.

To open it:

1. Locate your username in the bottom-left corner of the screen.
2. Click your username to open the account menu.

From this menu, you can access account settings, documentation, and sign-out options.

### View your account type

1. Select **Account settings**.
2. View your account type, shown as either **Free plan, Team,** or **Enterprise.**

### Manage data utilization

{% hint style="info" %}
This option is available only to Free plan users.\
Team and Enterprise members cannot change this setting.
{% endhint %}

1. Select **Account settings**.
2. Locate the **data utilization** (Help us improve!) toggle.
3. Turn the toggle on or off to control whether usage data is shared.

### Connect a Git provider account

{% hint style="info" %}
Applies to Team and Enterprise users working with GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket Cloud.
{% endhint %}

To receive consistent feedback from Qodo directly in Git, you need to connect your Git account. This links your Git identity to your Qodo account so feedback is correctly attributed to you.

#### How to connect

1. Select **Link your Git user account**.
2. Select a Git provider:
   * GitHub
   * GitLab
   * Bitbucket
3. Follow the authorization steps for the selected provider.

Once connected, your Git account is linked to Qodo.\
To enable Qodo to analyze code, you must also [install](https://app.gitbook.com/s/yXEFCLH7CkXrROa2gOqv/get-started/install) Qodo on your repositories.

### Access documentation

Select **Documentation** to open the Qodo documentation site.

### Sign out or delete your account

#### Sign out

Select **Sign out** to sign out of your account.

#### Delete your account

1. Select **Account settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Delete my account** section.
3. Click **Delete my account** and confirm.

Deleting your account removes all configurations and subscriptions.
