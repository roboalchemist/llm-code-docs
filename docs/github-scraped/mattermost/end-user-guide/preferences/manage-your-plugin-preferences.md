# Manage your plugin preferences

Using Mattermost in a web browser or the desktop app, you can customize
Mattermost plugin preferences for Microsoft Teams and Calls by selecting
**Settings** [\|gear\|](##SUBST##|gear|) next to your profile picture.

## Microsoft Teams plugin preferences

.. If used with include::, note the paths for images

<div class="mm-badge mm-badge--note">

[\|plans-img-yellow\|](##SUBST##|plans-img-yellow|) Available on [Entry,
Enterprise, and Enterprise Advanced
plans](https://mattermost.com/pricing/) (not available on Professional)

</div>

Select **MS Teams** to connect your Mattermost and Microsoft Teams
accounts, and manage notification preferences for Microsoft Teams chats
and group chats. See the
`connect your account <end-user-guide/collaborate/collaborate-within-connected-microsoft-teams:connect your mattermost account to your microsoft teams account>`{.interpreted-text
role="ref"}.

:::: tip
::: title
Tip
:::

Download our [Mattermost for Microsoft Teams
datasheet](https://mattermost.com/mattermost-for-microsoft-teams-datasheet/)
to learn how Mattermost helps your organization get more from your
Microsoft tools.
::::
