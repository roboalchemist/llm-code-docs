PayPal Checkout: Custom Checkout Integration
============================================

Boost sales with a checkout built to grow
-----------------------------------------

Offer millions of customers in 200+ markets the checkout stack that features their favorite ways to pay.

### Accept PayPal payments and major cards

Accept PayPal payments (PayPal, Venmo,^4^ Pay Later options, Checkout with Crypto, and more), as well as credit and debit cards through a PayPal-hosted checkout flow.

### Simple customization

Less coding for fast and simple setup. Customize the color and shape of our payment buttons to fit your brand.

### Built-in risk management

PayPal simplifies your backend by managing payments. Included Seller Protection helps your business avoid chargebacks, reversals, and fees on eligible transactions.^5^

### Control your risk management

Opt for Chargeback Protection and Fraud Protection Advanced to help reduce fraudulent costs and more.^6^ We'll provide security insights to help you meet global compliance standards.

### Fraud Protection Advanced

Fraud Protection Advanced gives you the insight and control you need to better balance chargebacks and declines on eligible transactions.

### No-code, faster setup

Easily create a link, button, or QR code for a product or service. Then, just copy, paste, and get paid.

### Pricing with no surprises

No monthly or setup fees. Only pay when you get paid.
