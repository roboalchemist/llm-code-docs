Apache ECharts TM

About Mailing List
------------------

Mailing list is where we discuss in public and keep everything tracked. You are welcomed to subscribe it if you wish:

* To be informed about bug reports or feature requests;
* To discuss about developing plans or specific issues;
* To offer helps to those who ask questions by email;
* And etc.

If you have a specific bug to report or feature request, we'd suggest you opening an issue on our [GitHub repo](https://github.com/apache/echarts/issues/new/choose), which is a more efficient way to report the details.

[commits@echarts.apache.org](mailto:commits@echarts.apache.org) focuses on the commit logs, while [dev@echarts.apache.org](mailto:dev@echarts.apache.org) holds other general discussions.

These two are public mailing list, and you can get access to them on Website [https://lists.apache.org/list.html?commits@echarts.apache.org](https://lists.apache.org/list.html?commits@echarts.apache.org) and [https://lists.apache.org/list.html?dev@echarts.apache.org](https://lists.apache.org/list.html?dev@echarts.apache.org) without subscribing.

How To Subscribe
----------------

Email [commits-subscribe@echarts.apache.org](mailto:commits-subscribe@echarts.apache.org) or [dev-subscribe@echarts.apache.org](mailto:dev-subscribe@echarts.apache.org) to subscribe commits@echarts.apache.org and dev@echarts.apache.org accordingly.

You should receive an email and please follow the instructions in that.
