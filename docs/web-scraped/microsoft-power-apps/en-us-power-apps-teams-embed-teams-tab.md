======================================

You can customize the Teams experience by adding Power Apps canvas apps to your channels in Teams using the **Power Apps** tab. In this topic, you learn how to add the Product Showcase sample app template to a Teams channel, and then open the app from that channel.

![Image 14: App embedded in Microsoft Teams.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/embedded-app.png)

If you're not signed up for Power Apps, [sign up for free](https://make.powerapps.com/signup?redirect=marketing&email=) before you begin.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/embed-teams-tab#prerequisites)
Prerequisites
-------------

To follow this procedure, you need an [Office 365 subscription](https://signup.microsoft.com/Signup?OfferId=467eab54-127b-42d3-b046-3844b860bebf&dl=O365_BUSINESS_PREMIUM&ali=1) and a [channel in Teams](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=he2f1quaR7M).

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/embed-teams-tab#sign-in-to-power-apps)
Sign in to Power Apps
---------------------

Sign into Power Apps at [https://make.powerapps.com](https://make.powerapps.com/?utm_source=padocs&utm_medium=linkinadoc&utm_campaign=referralsfromdoc).

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/embed-teams-tab#add-an-app)
Add an app
----------

1.   In Microsoft Teams, select a team, and a channel under that team. In this example, it's the **General** channel under the **Business Development** team.

![Image 15: Select General in Teams under your team.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/teams-select-channel.png)

2.   Choose **+** to add a tab.

![Image 16: Select +.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/teams-add-tab.png)

3.   In the **Add a tab** dialog box, choose **Power Apps**.

![Image 17: Select Add a tab.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/add-a-tab.png)

4.   Choose **Sample app templates**>**Product Showcase**>**Save**.

![Image 18: Select Product Showcase and Save.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/select-an-app.png)

The app is now available to use in the channel.

![Image 19: App opens.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/app-in-channel.png)

Note

You must [share](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/maker/canvas-apps/share-app) your own apps before you add them to Teams (sample app templates are shared by default).

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/embed-teams-tab#open-an-app)
Open an app
-----------

1.   In Microsoft Teams, choose the team and the channel that contains the app.

![Image 20: Select General in Teams under your team.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/teams-select-channel.png)

2.   Choose the **Product Showcase** tab.

![Image 21: Select Product Showcase tab.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/open-tab.png)

The app opens in the channel.

![Image 22: App opens.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/media/open-app-embedded-in-teams/app-in-channel.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/embed-teams-tab#known-issues)
Known issues
------------

In the desktop app for Microsoft Teams:

*   Apps must load content such as images and .pdf files over a secure (https) connection.
*   Not all sensors, such as **Acceleration**, **Compass**, and **Location**, are supported.
*   Only these audio formats are supported: AAC, H264, OGG Vorbis, and WAV.

In the mobile app for Microsoft Teams:

*   End users can't download attachments from canvas apps. As a workaround, use Microsoft Teams desktop to access the canvas app and download the attachment.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/power-apps/teams/embed-teams-tab#clean-up-resources)
Clean up resources
------------------

To remove the app from the channel, choose the **Product Showcase** tab >**Remove**.
