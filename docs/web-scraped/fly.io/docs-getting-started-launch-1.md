Quickstart: Launch your app
===========================

Welcome to Fly Launch. Fly Launch works with a Dockerfile or scans and configures apps for most [common languages and frameworks](https://fly.io/docs/getting-started/get-started-by-framework/).

To deploy your app on Fly.io for the first time:

1.   [Install flyctl](https://fly.io/docs/flyctl/install/) – the [open-source](https://github.com/superfly/flyctl) Fly.io CLI.

2.   Create an account with `fly auth signup` or log in with `fly auth login`.

3.   Run `fly launch` from inside your project source directory to create, configure, and (for most apps) deploy a new application.

4.   If prompted, run `fly deploy` to deploy your new app (or to redeploy after changes!).

![Image 2: Illustration by Annie Ruygt of a phoenix jumping with a motor bike](https://fly.io/static/images/moto-jump.png)
[](https://fly.io/docs/getting-started/launch/#next-steps)Next steps
--------------------------------------------------------------------

1.   Run `fly status` to show the status of your app and Machines. 
2.   Run `fly apps open` to open your app in your browser. 
3.   Learn some [Fly.io essentials](https://fly.io/docs/getting-started/essentials/) and continue to [explore what you can do with Fly Launch](https://fly.io/docs/launch/).
