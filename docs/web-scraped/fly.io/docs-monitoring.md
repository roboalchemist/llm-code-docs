Monitoring on Fly.io
====================

![Image 2: Illustration by Annie Ruygt of a smirking ghost sitting infront of a laptop](https://fly.io/static/images/spooky.webp)
[](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/#metrics)Metrics
--------------------------------------------------

_Fully-managed metrics solutions to help you monitor your apps._

*   **[Metrics](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/metrics):** Learn how to configure and use the Fly.io metrics for Prometheus and Grafana, and built-in and custom metrics for your apps. 

* * *

[](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/#error-monitoring-by-sentry)Error monitoring by Sentry
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

_Error monitoring for your apps from our extension providers._

*   **[Sentry](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/sentry/):** Use Sentry on Fly.io and get actionable insights to resolve the most important code-related issues. Fly.io organizations that use Sentry can claim a year’s worth of Team Plan credits. 

* * *

[](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/#logging)Logging
--------------------------------------------------

_Live tail and search your app’s logs, access them via API, or ship them where you want them._

*   **[Logging overview](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/logging-overview/):** How logs work on Fly.io. 
*   **[Logs API options for programmatic access](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/logs-api-options/)::** How to fetch, stream or export logs programmatically without touching the CLI. 
*   **[Live tail logs](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/live-tail-logs/):** Where to access live tail logs. 
*   **[Search logs](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/search-logs/):** Searchable app logs in Grafana (beta). 
*   **[Export logs](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/exporting-logs/):** Aggregate your logs to a service of your choice. 
*   **[Error codes and troubleshooting](https://fly.io/docs/monitoring/error-codes/):** Look up error codes and get tips for troubleshooting.
