Note

*   Currently, Clarity doesn't support segments in GA4.
*   Clarity doesn't send playback URL to GA4 as it cannot accept High Cardinality rows.

When you manually integrate GA4, enter the different IDs **What do you want to connect to?** dialog box:

*   [**Account ID**](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#how-to-find-your-account-id-on-ga4): Your site's Google Analytics Account ID.
*   **Friendly name**: Your site's name.
*   [**Measurement ID**](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#how-to-find-your-ga4-measurement-id): Your site's Google Analytics Measurement ID.
*   [**Property ID**](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#how-to-find-your-ga4-property-id): Your site's GA4 Property ID.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#how-to-find-your-account-id-on-ga4)
How to find your Account ID on GA4?
-----------------------------------

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#step-1)
### Step 1

Connect to your Google Analytics account. Select **Admin** from the bottom-left corner in the left panel.

![Image 13: Select settings icon in GA4.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-select-settings.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#step-2)
### Step 2

Select **Account Settings**. Copy the **Account ID** to paste it later.

![Image 14: Find GA4 account ID.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-account-id.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#how-to-find-your-ga4-measurement-id)
How to find your GA4 Measurement ID?
------------------------------------

Note

Measurement ID is only available for GA4 sites. Ensure that you selected the desired account and property ID.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#step-1-1)
### Step 1

Connect to your Google Analytics account. Select **Admin** from the bottom-left corner in the left panel.

![Image 15: Select settings icon in GA4.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-select-settings.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#step-2-1)
### Step 2

In the **Property** column, select **Data Streams**. Select your **Web data stream**.

![Image 16: Select web data stream in GA4.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-select-web-data-stream.png)

Your "G-" Measurement ID appears in the upper right portion of the panel. Copy the ID to paste later.

![Image 17: Google Analytics 4 measurement ID in web data streams.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-measurement-id.png)

Check [how to find G-ID](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9539598#find-G-ID) for more details.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#how-to-find-your-ga4-property-id)
How to find your GA4 Property ID?
---------------------------------

Note

Property ID is only available for GA4 sites.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#step-1-2)
### Step 1

Connect to your Google Analytics account. Select **Admin** from the bottom-left corner in the left panel.

![Image 18: Select settings icon in GA4.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-select-settings.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#step-2-2)
### Step 2

In the **Property** column, select **Property Settings**.

![Image 19: Select property settings in GA4.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-select-property-settings.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#step-3)
### Step 3

Copy the **Property ID** from the right corner to paste it later.

![Image 20: Google Analytics 4 property ID.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/media/ga4-property-id.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/ga-integration/ga4-integration#faq)
