What is Brand agents?
---------------------

Brand agents is a conversational AI that lives on your site and represents your brand. It engages customers to assist with product discovery, exploration, and checkout - acting like your best sales associate. Brand agents captures rich behavioral signals and intent patterns with the power of Microsoft Clarity to guide smarter merchandising, marketing, and product decisions.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#key-capabilities)
Key capabilities
----------------

*   **Real-time engagement**: Interacts with visitors as they browse your site.
*   **AI-powered responses**: Understands and responds to customer queries intelligently.
*   **Product guidance**: Helps customers find the right products.
*   **Purchase assistance**: Guides users through the checkout process.
*   **Behavioral insights**: Gathers valuable data on customer interactions.

Note

*   Brand agents is currently in beta. Join the waitlist to be informed when you're eligible to test them.
*   Currently, Brand agents is only available for Shopify sites.
*   Currently, Brand agents is only available for websites and not for mobile apps.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#getting-started)
Getting started
---------------

There are two ways to install Brand agents, depending on your setup:

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#option-1-install-from-clarity)
### Option 1: Install from Clarity

If you already have a Clarity project connected to your Shopify store, you can enable Brand agents directly from the Clarity platform.

Follow the [Clarity installation guide](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/installation/clarity-install-guide) for more steps.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#option-2-install-from-shopify)
### Option 2: Install from Shopify

If you're setting up Clarity and Brand agents for the first time, installing from the Shopify App Store streamlines the process.

Follow the [Shopify installation guide](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/installation/shopify-install-guide) for detailed steps.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#configuration)
### Configuration

After installation, you can configure your agent to reflect your brand voice and product catalog.

Learn how to [configure Brand agents](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/installation/configure-clarity-brand-agents).

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#performance-metrics)
Performance metrics
-------------------

Once your agent is live:

*   **Access the dashboard**: The [agent dashboard](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/dashboard/agent-dashboard-overview) provides a comprehensive view of how your agent is performing on your site.
*   **Monitor key metrics**: Track engagement, conversions, and sales impact. Learn more about [performance metrics](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/dashboard/agent-performance-metrics).
*   **Analyze conversations**: Review engagement depth and conversation quality.
*   **Optimize strategy**: Use insights to improve agent performance.
*   **Compare performance**: See how agent-assisted sessions compare to organic traffic.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#use-cases)
Use cases
---------

Brand agents excels in various scenarios:

*   **Product discovery**: Helps customers find the right products by understanding their needs and preferences through natural conversation.
*   **Purchase support**: Guides hesitant buyers through the decision-making process, addressing concerns and highlighting benefits.
*   **Checkout assistance**: Reduces cart abandonment by helping customers through the checkout process and answering last-minute questions.
*   **Customer service**: Provides instant answers to common questions about shipping, returns, sizing, and product details.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#language-support)
Language support
----------------

Brand agents is designed to support conversations in all languages, enabling users to interact seamlessly in their preferred language. During any chat session, users can switch languages at any time, and the agent continues the conversation in the selected language.

Additionally, the installation and configuration interfaces for Brand agents is available in all languages supported by Clarity, making setup and management accessible to users worldwide.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/brand-agents/brand-agents-overview#privacy)

What's next?
------------

*   **Join the waitlist**: Install the Clarity Shopify app and enable Brand agents. Clarity sends an email notification as soon as access is available. A specific date for access availability can't be provided. However, access continues to expand to more users over time. In the meantime, explore the rest of Clarity to help you understand user behavior on your site.
*   **Set up your agent**: Configure your agent settings.
*   **Go live**: Publish your agent to start engaging with customers.
*   **Monitor performance**: Track metrics and optimize your agent's effectiveness.
