Important

Recordings and heat maps are only displayed on the site's pages where the Clarity tracking code is installed. Check the [Setup](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/clarity-setup) process to know how to install it.

As you modify filters and view recordings or heat maps, you can share them either internally with your team or externally outside your team.

You can share in two ways:

*   [Share link](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/share-clarity#share-link)

*   [Share in email](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/share-clarity#share-in-email)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/share-clarity#where-do-i-find-the-share-icon)
Where do I find the share icon?
-------------------------------

With any of your projects open, you can find the share icon in **Recordings**, or **Heatmaps** pages. Select the ![Image 13](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-icon.png) icon to start sharing.

**Recordings**:

![Image 14: Share button in recordings.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-recording.png)

**Heatmaps**:

![Image 15: Share button in heatmaps.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-heatmap.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/share-clarity#share-link)
Share link
----------

Share the link either within the team or with anyone.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/share-clarity#project-team)
### Project team

Important

Only members of the current team can use this link to view the heat map or recording. This link will never expire.

1.   Go to Share link and select **With project team** in Share settings.

2.   Select **Copy** to copy the link onto the clipboard, and then select **Done**.

![Image 16: Share link with project team.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-with-project-team.png)

3.   For a recording, you can also choose the timing from which a recording can be shared.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/share-clarity#anyone)
### Anyone

Important

Anyone with this link can view this recording or heatmap.

1.   Go to Share link and select **With anyone** in Share settings.

![Image 17: Share link with anyone.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-with-anyone.png)

2.   Choose the link expiration time frame in days. This indicates that the link will expire after the selected time frame. Select **Copy** to copy the link onto the clipboard and select **Done**.

![Image 18: Set expiration days to share with anyone.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-expire-days.png)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/share-clarity#share-in-email)
Share in email
--------------

1.   Go to Share in email. Enter the name or email address of team members. You can enter multiple team member IDs and also share with people outside your team.

Note

Links shared to team members don't expire. 
![Image 19: Share by email with team members.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-email-1.png)

2.   If you're sharing with someone outside your project, they receive a public link. You can choose how long you want this link to be active in Link expiration.

![Image 20: Share by email with nonteam members.](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/media/share-email-2.png)

3.   Enter an optional message and select **Send**.
