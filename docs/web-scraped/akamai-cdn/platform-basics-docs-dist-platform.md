Akamai's distributed cloud platform
===================================

_Akamai Cloud_ is a distributed edge and cloud platform that delivers cloud computing, security, and content delivery that:

*   Boosts performance, speed, and application development with [cloud computing](https://techdocs.akamai.com/platform-basics/docs/cloud-compute) services.
*   Protects your data, workforce, systems, and digital experiences in every corner of your business with [security](https://techdocs.akamai.com/platform-basics/docs/security) services.
*   Delivers applications and user experiences closer to your customers, wherever they connect with our [content delivery network (CDN)](https://techdocs.akamai.com/platform-basics/docs/content-delivery) services.
*   Provides [developer tools and resources](https://techdocs.akamai.com/platform-basics/docs/for-devs) to integrate Akamai services.
