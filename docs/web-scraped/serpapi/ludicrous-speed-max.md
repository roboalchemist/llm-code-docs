FAQs
----

Is Ludicrous Speed Max available for all the subscription plans?

Ludicrous Speed Max can be switched on or off for all paid plans, including self-served, high-volume, and enterprise plans.

How does Ludicrous Speed Max work?

With Ludicrous Speed Max enabled, we use **4x the server resources** to automatically create numerous parallel requests compared to our already aggressive Ludicrous Speed SerpApi. Our advanced validators reject bad HTMLs, CAPTCHA and error pages, and other abnormalities, ensuring you receive only successful results quickly and efficiently.

Can Ludicrous Speed Max be used with all of SerpApi's search APIs?

Yes, Ludicrous Speed Max is fully supported for all SerpApi's search APIs.

How much does Ludicrous Speed Max cost?

Ludicrous Speed Max is priced at 4x the price of Best Effort.

How to enable Ludicrous Speed Max?

To enable Ludicrous Speed Max, first, create an account on our platform. Once logged in, go to your Dashboard >[Change Plan](https://serpapi.com/change-plan), choose the plan that suits your needs, and enable the “Ludicrous Speed Max” option. If you already have a subscription, you can upgrade your plan on the same page by enabling the “Ludicrous Speed Max” option.

Can Ludicrous Speed Max be used for live use cases?

Yes, Ludicrous Speed Max is a no-compromise solution for live use cases, offering even better performance compared to our Ludicrous Speed option, ensuring seamless real-time results.
