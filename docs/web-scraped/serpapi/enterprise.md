### Searches are billed in addition according to your usage.

You can either opt for **on-demand searches**. You don't have to commit to a specific number of searches per month. We have the most flexible payment options for these. You can also prepay or postpay your invoice. We charge **$7.50** (**$15.00 with Ludicrous Speed**, or **$30.00 with Ludicrous Speed Max**) per 1,000 on-demand searches.

Or, you can opt for **reserved searches**. You have to commit to a specific number of searches per month. Unused search credits don’t roll over from month to month. We charge **$2.75** (**$5.50 with Ludicrous Speed**, or **$11.00 with Ludicrous Speed Max**) per 1,000 reserved searches.

In both cases, only fully successful searches are counted. Searches blocked, errored, or receiving CAPTCHAs are not counted. Note that legitimately empty search queries are counted. Pagination queries count as separate searches.

We recommend starting with a base **$3,750** per month Enterprise plan. It comes with 100,000 complimentary searches per month. This way, you can test out things and figure out your actual usage.
