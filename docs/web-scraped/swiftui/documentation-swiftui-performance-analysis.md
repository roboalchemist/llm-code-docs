Performance analysis
====================

Measure and improve your app’s responsiveness.

[Overview](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/performance-analysis#Overview)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Use Instruments to detect hangs and hitches in your app, and to analyze long view body updates and frequently occurring SwiftUI updates that can contribute to hangs and hitches.

[Topics](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/performance-analysis#topics)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Essentials](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/performance-analysis#Essentials)

[Understanding user interface responsiveness](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/Xcode/understanding-user-interface-responsiveness)

Make your app more responsive by examining the event-handling and rendering loop.

[Understanding hangs in your app](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/Xcode/understanding-hangs-in-your-app)

Determine the cause for delays in user interactions by examining the main thread and the main run loop.

[Understanding hitches in your app](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/Xcode/understanding-hitches-in-your-app)

Determine the cause of interruptions in motion by examining the render loop.

### [Analyzing SwiftUI performance](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/performance-analysis#Analyzing-SwiftUI-performance)

[Understanding and improving Swift UI performance](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/Xcode/understanding-and-improving-swiftui-performance)

Identify and address long-running view updates, and reduce the frequency of updates.

[See Also](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/performance-analysis#see-also)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### [Tool support](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/performance-analysis#Tool-support)

[Previews in Xcode](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/previews-in-xcode)

Generate dynamic, interactive previews of your custom views.

[Xcode library customization](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/xcode-library-customization)

Expose custom views and modifiers in the Xcode library.
