Enterprise-grade security and governance
----------------------------------------

Protect your data with our built-in and customizable security features.

Sync data without it leaving your secure cloud network.

[Hybrid Deployment→](https://fivetran.com/docs/deployment-models/hybrid-deployment)

Allow Fivetran to sync your data without exposing traffic to the public internet.

[Private Networking→](https://fivetran.com/docs/using-fivetran/features#privatenetworking)

Use Fivetran-generated logs and metadata to monitor connectors and track usage in your destination.

[Fivetran Platform Connector→](https://fivetran.com/docs/logs/fivetran-platform)

Use a proxy agent to push data to Fivetran without giving us access to your network.

[Fivetran Proxy→](https://fivetran.com/docs/connectors/databases/connection-options/proxy-agent)

Limit access to your Fivetran account and delegate control to your teams to prevent data security incidents.

[Role-Based Access Control→](https://fivetran.com/docs/using-fivetran/fivetran-dashboard/account-settings/role-based-access-control)

Learn how Fivetran protects the security and confidentiality of your data.

[Security→](https://fivetran.com/docs/security)
