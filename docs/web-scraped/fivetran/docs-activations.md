Activations[](https://fivetran.com/docs/activations#activations)
================================================================

Activations is a cloud-based product that enables you to configure managed, automated reverse ETL pipelines without writing code. It activates data from a centralized source of truth, such as a data warehouse, and delivers insights directly into the business tools where your teams make decisions. When paired with Fivetran’s ELT capabilities, Activations enables you to have a bidirectional, end-to-end data pipeline.
