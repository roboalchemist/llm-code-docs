[Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io/) is an easy to use, open source, lightweight and privacy-friendly alternative to Google Analytics. Plausible is trusted by thousands of paying subscribers to deliver their website and business insights. We're completely independent, self-funded and bootstrapped. Read more [about us](https://plausible.io/about).

New to Plausible? Start here[​](https://plausible.io/docs/#new-to-plausible-start-here "Direct link to New to Plausible? Start here")
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.   [Register for an account](https://plausible.io/docs/register-account)
2.   [Add your website details](https://plausible.io/docs/add-website)
3.   [Add the snippet to your website](https://plausible.io/docs/plausible-script)
4.   [Check if Plausible is installed correctly](https://plausible.io/docs/troubleshoot-integration)

Once you're set up, explore the [stats dashboard](https://plausible.io/docs/guided-tour) to see your data.
