Max Basics
----------

With Max, you can instantly see and hear the results with every change you make. Build your own world of sound using sample-manipulation, synthesis tools, high-quality filters, spectral processing, real-time recording and playback. Control visuals with audio, MIDI, or any other data source.

Integrate vector graphics, compositing effects, color manipulations, and videos.Create your own 3D worlds, complete with lighting, materials, and multiple cameras, and use the advanced physics engine for animation control. With its ability to communicate with other applications and hardware, Max is the glue that holds your project together.

Max connects music, video, control, and lighting systems with MIDI or OSC controller devices, or custom hardware.
