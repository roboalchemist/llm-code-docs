### Overview [¶](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit/constant#pkg-overview "Go to Overview")

Package constant provides fundamental constants satisfying unit.Uniter.

Constant values reflect the values published at [https://physics.nist.gov/cuu/index.html](https://physics.nist.gov/cuu/index.html) and are subject to change when the published values are updated.

### Constants [¶](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit/constant#pkg-constants "Go to Constants")

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/atomicmass.go#L13)const AtomicMass = [unit](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit).[Mass](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit#Mass)(1.6605390666e-27)

AtomicMass is the atomic mass constant (mᵤ), one twelfth of the mass of an unbound atom of carbon-12 at rest and in its ground state. The dimension of AtomicMass is kg. The standard uncertainty of the constant is 5e-37 kg.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/avogadro.go#L17)const Avogadro = avogadroUnits(6.02214076e+23)

Avogadro is the Avogadro constant (A), the number of constituent particles contained in one mole of a substance. The dimension of Avogadro is mol^-1. The constant is exact.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/boltzmann.go#L17)const Boltzmann = boltzmannUnits(1.380649e-23)

Boltzmann is the Boltzmann constant (k), it relates the average relative kinetic energy of particles in a gas with the temperature of the gas. The dimensions of Boltzmann are kg m^2 K^-1 s^-2. The constant is exact.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/electricconstant.go#L17)const ElectricConstant = electricConstantUnits(8.854187817620389e-12)

ElectricConstant is the electric constant (ε₀), the value of the absolute dielectric permittivity of classical vacuum. The dimensions of ElectricConstant are A^2 s^4 kg^-1 m^-3. The standard uncertainty of the constant is 1.3e-21 A^2 s^4 kg^-1 m^-3.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/elementarycharge.go#L13)const ElementaryCharge = [unit](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit).[Charge](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit#Charge)(1.602176634e-19)

ElementaryCharge, is the elementary charge constant (e), the magnitude of electric charge carried by a single proton or electron. The dimensions of ElementaryCharge are A s. The constant is exact.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/faraday.go#L17)const Faraday = faradayUnits(96485.33212)

Faraday is the Faraday constant, the magnitude of electric charge per mole of electrons. The dimensions of Faraday are A s mol^-1. The constant is exact.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/finestructure.go#L12)const FineStructure = [unit](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit).[Dimless](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit#Dimless)(0.0072973525693)

FineStructure is the fine structure constant (α), it describes the strength of the electromagnetic interaction between elementary charged particles. The standard uncertainty of the constant is 1.1e-12 .

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/gravitational.go#L17)const Gravitational = gravitationalUnits(6.6743e-11)

Gravitational is the universal gravitational constant (G), the proportionality constant connecting the gravitational force between two bodies. The dimensions of Gravitational are m^3 kg^-1 s^-2. The standard uncertainty of the constant is 1.5e-15 m^3 kg^-1 s^-2.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/lightspeedinvacuum.go#L13)const LightSpeedInVacuum = [unit](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit).[Velocity](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit#Velocity)(2.99792458e+08)

LightSpeedInVacuum is the c constant, the speed of light in a vacuum. The dimensions of LightSpeedInVacuum are m s^-1. The constant is exact.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/magneticconstant.go#L17)const MagneticConstant = magneticConstantUnits(1.2566370621238374e-06)

MagneticConstant is the magnetic constant (μ₀), the magnetic permeability in a classical vacuum. The dimensions of MagneticConstant are A^2 s^4 kg^-1 m^-3. The standard uncertainty of the constant is 1.9e-16 A^2 s^4 kg^-1 m^-3.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/planck.go#L17)const Planck = planckUnits(6.62607015e-34)

Planck is the Planck constant (h), it relates the energy carried by a photon to its frequency. The dimensions of Planck are kg m^2 s^-1. The constant is exact.

[View Source](https://github.com/gonum/gonum/blob/v0.17.0/unit/constant/standardgravity.go#L13)const StandardGravity = [unit](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit).[Acceleration](https://pkg.go.dev/gonum.org/v1/gonum@v0.17.0/unit#Acceleration)(9.80665)

StandardGravity is the standard gravity constant (g₀), the nominal gravitational acceleration of an object in a vacuum near the surface of the Earth The dimensions of StandardGravity are m s^-2. The constant is exact.
