Private Deployment
==================

A private deployment of Vectara brings the full power of our agentic AI platform directly into your controlled environment. This deployment option enables organizations to leverage cutting-edge AI technology while maintaining complete control over their data, infrastructure, and security posture.

[Overview Learn why organizations choose private deployment and explore the key benefits of running Vectara in your controlled environment. Understand how private deployment provides data sovereignty, enhanced security, and full platform capabilities.](https://docs.vectara.com/docs/deployments/private-deployment-overview)[Deployment options Explore flexible deployment models including on-premises, private cloud (VPC), and hybrid configurations. Learn about infrastructure flexibility with support for various Kubernetes environments and storage solutions.](https://docs.vectara.com/docs/deployments/deployment-options)[Use cases Discover how financial services, healthcare, government, and other regulated industries leverage private deployment. See common use cases including enterprise knowledge management, customer support, and research analysis.](https://docs.vectara.com/docs/deployments/use-cases)[Evaluation process Understand the structured process from initial assessment through deployment and ongoing partnership. Learn what to expect when working with our enterprise team to implement your private deployment.](https://docs.vectara.com/docs/deployments/onprem-evaluation-process)[Guides Step-by-step configuration guides for integrating your private deployment with enterprise systems. Includes SSO setup with Okta OIDC and other enterprise authentication and integration patterns.](https://docs.vectara.com/docs/deployments/guides)
