Your Scrapfly account is the entry point to use our service. It's the place where you can manage your subscription, projects, and API keys. Account resources can be shared with collaborators using the [Teams](https://scrapfly.io/docs/workspace-and-team) feature.

[Updating Details](https://scrapfly.io/docs/account#dashboard)
--------------------------------------------------------------

You can update your account details using the [Account Dashboard](https://scrapfly.io/dashboard/settings). This is where you can manage:

*   Your personal details and email address
*   Update your password
*   Update your billing information
*   Manage your scrapfly email subscriptions
*   Review logged in sessions for security

[See your Account Details](https://scrapfly.io/dashboard/settings)
