# Source: https://support.anydesk.com/docs/gdpr-compliance

[GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) is a comprehensive data privacy regulation in the European Union, designed to protect personal data of EU citizens and give them more control over their personal information. It replaces the previously fragmented data protection laws in EU Member States and applies to organizations operating within the EU and handling personal data. ]

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## [What AnyDesk does for GDPR compliance?] 

[We have implemented technical and organizational measures to ensure that data processing complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). ]

[AnyDesk complies with the following GDPR regulations:]

-   [Obligation to process personal data in a lawful, fair, and transparent manner.]

-   [Limitation of purpose for collecting and processing personal data.]

-   [Data minimization (only collecting the data necessary for the specified purpose).]

-   [Data accuracy and storage limitations.]

-   [Implementation of appropriate technical and organizational measures for data protection.]

-   [Obligation to notify data breaches to the supervisory authority and affected individuals.]

-   [Respect for individuals' rights (such as right to access, right to be forgotten, etc.).]

[For more information on GDPR compliance, check our ][[Privacy Policy]](https://anydesk.com/en/privacy)[.]

## [How is my personal data protected at AnyDesk? ] 

[We take measures to ensure that the personal data we collect is necessary and relevant for the purposes it is collected and used for. We also take steps to protect personal data and implement technical and organizational measures to secure personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.]

[AnyDesk protects personal data through the following measures:]

-   [**Encryption:** Personal data is encrypted both in transit (when being transmitted) and at rest (when stored).]

-   [**Secure data centers:** AnyDesk\'s data centers are equipped with industry-standard security measures, such as fire suppression systems and multi-factor authentication.]

-   [**Access controls:** Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who need it to perform their job responsibilities.]

-   [**Regular security audits:** AnyDesk regularly performs security audits to identify potential vulnerabilities and address them promptly.]

-   [**Incident response plan:** AnyDesk has a plan in place to respond to security incidents, such as data breaches, to minimize harm and ensure quick resolution.]

-   [**Compliance with privacy and data protection laws:** AnyDesk complies with applicable privacy and data protection laws, including the GDPR.]

[AnyDesk has various data protection policies in place to ensure that personal data is collected, processed, and stored in accordance with the GDPR. These policies are regularly reviewed and updated to ensure they remain in compliance with GDPR regulations. ]

[It\'s important to note that users also have a role to play in protecting their personal data by keeping their passwords secure and being cautious of phishing attempts and other security threats.]
