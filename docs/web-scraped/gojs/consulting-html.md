Northwoods Consulting and Training
----------------------------------

Our team at Northwoods may be able to assist in the development of your project. Northwoods offers training, help with proofs of concept, and some consulting within the diagram-specific scope of projects. Our proof-of-concepts include sample building and feature demonstrations.

[Contact us](https://nwoods.com/contact) for details.

Consulting for Large Projects: Synergy Codes
--------------------------------------------

[Synergy Codes](https://synergycodes.com/gojs/) has over a decade of GoJS experience and cooperation with Northwoods Software. They have leveraged GoJS in data visualization and process modeling projects for customers worldwide, and have built projects for clients in several industries including banking, energy, and automotive, serving startups, SaaS businesses, and Fortune 500 companies.

Synergy Codes offers a fully staffed team, including UX and UI designers, and software engineers with extensive expertise in GoJS tool and layout customizations, data importation from external sources, and solutions involving real-time data integration and collaboration. You can expect expert performance optimization and improved diagram readability with modern layouts and UX/UI. Because they have completed so many GoJS projects, they have built up a library of components and knowledge that allow for rapid proof-of-concept delivery.
