[Automatic layouts Built-in layouts, and many samples of custom layouts to use or adapt. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/layouts.html)[Node and link templates Quickly set the look for your diagram parts while keeping appearance separate from data. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/nodes.html)[Data binding Automatically keep your data in sync with your display, and vice versa. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/dataBinding.html)[Undo & redo Users can easily undo and redo, and cancelled tool operations are rolled back. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/transactions.html)[Keyboard shortcuts Use common keyboard commands and gestures, which can be customized. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/commands.html)[Subgraphs Groups provide subgraphs to apply different rules or layouts to their members. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/subgraphs.html)[Extensible tools Customize and extend Tools to give your users the interaction they need. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/tools.html)[Customizable events and permissions Execute custom logic or notifications when users perform certain actions or key presses, or disable different interactions altogether. Learn more](https://gojs.net/intro/events.html)[Context menus and tooltips Context menus and tooltips are built-in, and can be extended in-canvas or with HTML.
