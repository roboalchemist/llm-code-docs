# Business Continuity Event Response Software

## Overview

LogicManager's Event Response Software enables organizations to effectively manage business continuity events and cybersecurity incidents by connecting them to associated people, policies, procedures, and controls. It provides real-time incident coordination and visibility during critical events.

## Event Management & Incident Coordination

### Connecting People and Processes

The platform enables you to:

- **Manage Any Business Continuity Event**: Track and coordinate response to disruptions
- **Link to Associated Controls**: Connect events to relevant policies, procedures, and controls
- **Monitor Affected Areas**: Track relationships between events and impacted departments
- **Improve Visibility**: Gain clarity into which critical business functions are affected

## Custom Data Collection

### Dynamic Information Gathering

The system offers custom profile and visibility rules that dynamically collect additional information based on event specifics:

- **Incident Type**: Gather different information based on whether outage is IT, facility, personnel, or other
- **Location Impact**: Track how many facilities or geographic regions are affected
- **Department Impact**: Identify which departments or business units are impacted
- **System Impact**: Document which servers, applications, or critical systems are affected
- **Customizable Questions**: Tailor data collection to your organization's needs

## Workflow Automation

### Intelligent Incident Routing

Workflow automation routes incidents based on business logic:

- **Auto-Assignment**: Automatically assign response tasks to appropriate personnel
- **Escalation**: Route critical incidents to senior management
- **Impact-Based Routing**: Different workflows for different impact levels
- **Department Routing**: Route to relevant department heads and teams
- **Task Tracking**: Monitor completion of assigned response tasks

## Emergency Response Capabilities

### Real-Time Response Coordination

Key emergency readiness features include:

- **Rapid Task Assignment**: Quickly assign response activities
- **Issue Escalation**: Escalate critical issues to leadership
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor recovery efforts in real-time
- **Accountability**: Ensure transparency and accountability during crisis

### Resource & Dependency Management

- **Integrated References**: Connect processes to vendors, applications, and dependencies
- **Upstream/Downstream Impact**: Account for cascade effects across systems
- **Vendor Contact Information**: Quick access to critical vendor relationships
- **Recovery Interdependencies**: Understand recovery sequencing requirements

### Customizable Response Resources

- **Home Screens**: Customize rapid access to critical resources during emergencies
- **Dashboard Views**: Role-specific views for different response teams
- **Quick Navigation**: Fast access to assigned tasks and procedures
- **Incident Templates**: Pre-built response procedures by incident type

## Recovery Monitoring & Reporting

### Performance Tracking

Robust reporting and dashboards track key recovery metrics:

- **RTO Tracking**: Monitor Recovery Time Objectives vs. actual recovery time
- **RPO Tracking**: Track Recovery Point Objectives and actual data recovery
- **Personnel Assignment**: Verify key personnel are in place and executing
- **Status Updates**: Real-time status of recovery operations
- **Impact Assessment**: Measure actual impact vs. projected impact

### Post-Incident Analysis

- **Event Documentation**: Complete audit trail of incident response
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Identify causes and contributing factors
- **Lessons Learned**: Document what worked and what needs improvement
- **Trend Analysis**: Identify patterns in incident types and impacts
- **Continuous Improvement**: Use insights to improve future response
