# LogicManager Business Continuity Management (BCM)

## Overview

LogicManager is a comprehensive Business Continuity Management (BCM) platform designed for resiliency managers, IT operations, and risk leaders. It provides enterprise-class tools for business continuity planning, disaster recovery planning (DRP), business continuity testing, and event response coordination.

The platform takes a risk-based approach to business continuity, helping organizations identify critical resources, prioritize recovery efforts, and coordinate stakeholder engagement before, during, and after disruptive events.

## Core Capabilities

### Business Continuity Planning

- **Business Impact Analysis (BIA)**: Identify critical areas and their recovery requirements
- **Centralized Planning Tools**: Document critical resources, processes, and continuity strategies
- **Risk-Based Framework**: Ground continuity planning in enterprise risk governance using the Risk Maturity Model (RMM)
- **Scenario Planning**: Develop scenario-specific response plans and playbooks
- **Automated Documentation**: Centralize storage for all continuity documentation

### Risk Intelligence

**Risk Ripple Intelligence** shows how disruptions in one area cascade across processes, systems, and teams, transforming complex interdependencies into actionable insights for coordinated response planning.

### Testing & Validation

- **Automated Testing**: Ensure BCPs are operational and effective with automated testing
- **Tabletop Exercises**: Facilitate drills and simulations to validate plans
- **Gap Analysis**: Reveal plan gaps through structured testing
- **Testing Documentation**: Document outcomes for continuous improvement

### Event Response & Incident Management

- **Event Coordination**: Manage business continuity events or cybersecurity incidents
- **Workflow Automation**: Route incidents based on business logic
- **Task Assignment**: Automatically assign response tasks to appropriate personnel
- **Visibility Dashboard**: Track recovery metrics including RTO, RPO, and key personnel
- **Post-Incident Analysis**: Analyze and learn from events
