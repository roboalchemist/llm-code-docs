Support When You Need It
========================

### Getting Help from the Community

![Image 8](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-documentation.svg)![Image 9](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-documentation-dark.svg)
#### Documentation

We have a lot of documentation. Be sure to check our [Getting started page](https://quarkus.io/get-started/), and all our [guides](https://quarkus.io/guides/). Also check out our [FAQ section](https://quarkus.io/faq/) and [Quarkus Tips Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLsM3ZE5tGAVbMz1LJqc8L5LpnfxPPKloO).

![Image 10](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-stackoverflow.svg)![Image 11](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-stackoverflow-dark.svg)
#### Stack Overflow

Ask your questions on [Stack Overflow](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/quarkus). After the documentation, it’s probably the best place to look for answers. We actively monitor the [Quarkus tag](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/quarkus).

![Image 12](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-discussion.svg)![Image 13](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-discussion-dark.svg)
#### Discussions and Collaboration

Check out our [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/quarkusio/quarkus/discussions) collaboration area to interact with other Quarkus users and developers.

![Image 14](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-callcalendar.svg)![Image 15](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-callcalendar-dark.svg)
#### Community Call

We host a Quarkus Community Call on the first and third Tuesday of each month at 2PM (Paris time) to discuss technical topics and project updates. To attend, [view the calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/embed?src=935090aaae38be35eda437d018782b7827f3770f7a0344013677acd861570b20%40group.calendar.google.com&ctz=Europe%2FParis) or [subscribe (iCal) to the calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/935090aaae38be35eda437d018782b7827f3770f7a0344013677acd861570b20%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics) so you don't miss one.

![Image 16](https://quarkus.io/assets/images/icons/icon-development.svg)
#### Quarkus Development

Discuss the development of Quarkus with the team in the [development mailing list](mailto:quarkus-dev+subscribe@googlegroups.com) or by [Zulip Chat](https://quarkusio.zulipchat.com/).
