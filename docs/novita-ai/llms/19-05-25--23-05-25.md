# May 23, 2025 Product Updates

## **Model APIs Updates**

### **New Features**

* **Kling V1.6: Now Supports 10-Second Video Generation**

  Kling V1.6 now supports generating videos up to 10 seconds long, enabling more complete and coherent visual storytelling. Ideal for short narratives, ad snippets, and dynamic showcases. Get started now:  [Novita AI KLING V1.6 Text to Video API | Generate videos from text](https://novita.ai/docs/api-reference/model-apis-kling-v1.6-t2v) and [Novita AI KLING V1.6 Image to Video API | Generate videos from images](https://novita.ai/docs/api-reference/model-apis-kling-v1.6-i2v).

## **GPUs Updates**

### **New Features**

* **New GPU Type L40S Now Available**

  The **L40S** is a **high-performance data center GPU** from NVIDIA, built on the Ada Lovelace architecture. It is optimized for **multi-tasking scenarios** such as AI inference, large model training, graphics rendering, and virtualization. The L40S is well-suited for **inference and training of medium to large language models** like LLaMA, GPT, and BERT, as well as for **Stable Diffusion and image/video generation**. It supports concurrent operation of multiple hardware engines and offers excellent **multi-user VM deployment** and **GPU virtualization capabilities**. Get started now: [Novita AI](https://novita.ai/gpus-console).

  Updated time: May 23, 2025
