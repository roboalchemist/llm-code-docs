# Automatic Top-Up

When you enable **"Automatic Top-Up"**, your account will automatically add credit if the balance falls below a specified threshold. Please follow the steps below to set up this feature:

### 1. Enable Automatic Top-Up

Go to <a href="https://novita.ai/billing/payment-methods" target="_blank">Payment Methods</a> in the console.

  <img height="300" src="https://mintcdn.com/novitaai/pBY37YC9HPtF1YcS/guides/images/automatic-top-up-switch.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=pBY37YC9HPtF1YcS&q=85&s=2d9d485c491ec64f8328c06285528913" data-path="guides/images/automatic-top-up-switch.png" />

If you don't have a payment method, click **"Add payment method"**. You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure page to add one, and we do not store any payment information.

### 2. Automatic Top-Up Settings

After you turn on the **"Automatic Payments"** switch, a configuration popup will appear automatically.

Set valid values for **"When credit goes below"** and **"Bring credit back up to"**, then click **"Save settings"** to complete the process.
