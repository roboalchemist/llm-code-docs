# I accidentally uploaded a file with confidential or sensitive information to VirusTotal, can you please delete it?

We are very concerned about the privacy of our users and will do everything that is in our hands in order to ensure that privacy is preserved, please use our [contact form](https://www.virustotal.com/gui/contact-us/private-upload) to inform us about the issue.

We also recommend to read our [privacy policy](https://docs.virustotal.com/docs/privacy-policy) and [terms of service](https://docs.virustotal.com/docs/terms-of-service).
