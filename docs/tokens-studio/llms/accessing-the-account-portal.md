# Accessing the Account Portal

You can access the Account Portal at any time to review your plan or manage seats.

#### How to log in

1. Go to [account.tokens.studio.](https://account.tokens.studio/)
2. Enter the email that was used to subscribe to Starter Plus plan.

#### Who can access

* **Organization Owners/Admins**: Full access to subscription, billing, and seat management.

{% hint style="info" %}
An organization owner or admin for a Tokens Studio Starter Plus plan is the person who carried out the initial subscription. Their email is linked to the Account Portal
{% endhint %}

#### Changing the Admin Email

You can transfer admin rights by updating the admin email address in the Account Portal.

1. Go to **Edit billing** in the portal.
2. Scroll down to the **Email** field.
3. Update it with the new admin’s email address.
4. Save your changes.

***

#### Important notes

* Once the current admin logs out, only the new admin will be able to log in.
* No automatic notification is sent to the new admin — make sure to inform them directly about the change.
* The new admin will now have full control over billing, seats, and organization settings.
