Dataedo is an intuitive and lightweight tool for data governance, observability, and data discovery. It simplifies cataloging, publishing data structure definitions, and assessing data quality across various data sources. Built to support team collaboration, Dataedo unlocks the value of your data assets with end-to-end metadata management tools. With automated data quality checks, Dataedo also promises to bring accuracy, consistency, and reliability to your organization's data.

How Dataedo works[​](https://dataedo.com/docs#how-dataedo-works "Direct link to How Dataedo works")
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Dataedo repository](https://docs.dataedo.com/get-started/repository/ "Dataedo Repository") is a centralized database designed to keep all your metadata organized in one place. With the [Dataedo Scheduler](https://docs.dataedo.com/configure-and-manage/scheduler/ "Dataedo Scheduler"), you can automate metadata imports, ensuring your repository stays current with minimal effort. The [Dataedo portal](https://docs.dataedo.com/intro/portal-and-desktop#dataedo-portal) then offers an intuitive interface for viewing, searching, and managing your data documentation, allowing teams to collaborate effectively and maintain accurate, up-to-date information about your data.
