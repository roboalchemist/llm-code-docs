# Slack App

The SMTP2GO Slack App allows you to get real-time notifications directly into your Slack environment for events from your account’s “Reports > Activity” page as well as system status updates.

The following event notifications are available via the app:

* Bounce.
* Unsubscribe and resubscribe.
* Spam complaint.
* Open and click tracking.
* System maintenance/status updates.

Events can be enabled or disabled at any time via the "Settings > Webhooks" section of your account in the SMTP2GO App.

For detailed setup instructions, view our [Slack App knowledgebase article](https://support.smtp2go.com/hc/en-gb/articles/900006072346-Slack-App) or view the app in the [Slack app directory](https://slack.com/apps/A0211H2QXB5-smtp2go?tab=more_info).

If you have questions or need assistance with setting up or using the Slack App, please [contact](https://www.smtp2go.com/contact/) our support team.
