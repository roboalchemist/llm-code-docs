What is Clarity?
----------------

[Clarity](https://clarity.microsoft.com/) is a cutting-edge behavioral analytics tool that helps you understand user interaction with your website or app. By using Clarity, you can enhance the user experience and outcomes for your clients and business.

[Try Demo](https://clarity.microsoft.com/demo/projects/view/3t0wlogvdz/dashboard?date_d=Last%203%20days)

[Sign Up](https://clarity.microsoft.com/)

[Watch a quick video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cCfPxElfAz0)

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/setup-and-installation/about-clarity/#supported-features)
### Supported Features

*   Session recordings
*   Heatmaps
*   Event and funnel tracking
*   Chat and summarize with Copilot

Visit the [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@msftclarity) to learn more about our features.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/setup-and-installation/about-clarity/#why-should-i-use-clarity)
Why should I use Clarity?
-------------------------

A few ways where Clarity can be helpful:

*   Understanding how people use your site or app and where they get stuck to make data-driven decisions.

*   Identify problem areas that are causing user frustration.

*   Copilot powered insights quickly summarize recordings and heatmaps to help you analyze user behavior efficiently.

*   Create and analyze segments to customize the data to your specific needs.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/setup-and-installation/about-clarity/#where-can-i-use-clarity)
Where can I use Clarity?
------------------------

A few scenarios where Clarity can be best-used include but aren't limited to:

*   Enable your clients or management in making data-driven decisions on what works and what doesn't.
*   Test what content works best and where to place it on your page.
*   Understand confusing elements of your webpage.
*   Identify user behavior and add required content to your page.
*   Test and publish new ideas on your webpage.
*   Study user clicks and scrutinize the requirements.

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/setup-and-installation/about-clarity/#what-is-the-pricing-model-for-clarity)
What is the pricing model for Clarity?
--------------------------------------

Clarity is a free service forever. There's no obligation to upgrade to a paid version. [Learn more](https://clarity.microsoft.com/pricing).

[](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/setup-and-installation/about-clarity/#is-my-data-secure)
Is my data secure?
------------------

Yes, your data is secure. Visit Clarity's [Terms of Use](https://clarity.microsoft.com/terms) to learn more.

For all answers related to Data Privacy, refer to the [Privacy FAQ](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/clarity/setup-and-installation/faq#privacy).

Important

Clarity shouldn't be used on any websites/apps targeting users under the age of 18.
