# Deleting Account

Deleting your Apidog account is a permanent action that removes all your data, including API projects, documentation, and stored information. This guide walks you through the deletion process for both individual accounts and team-based accounts.

:::warning
Account deletion is irreversible. Ensure you have backed up all essential data before proceeding. Additionally, handle any credits or active subscriptions, and exit or disband any teams you're part of.
:::

## How to Delete Your Apidog Account

To permanently remove your Apidog account, follow these steps:

1. Log into your Apidog account.

2. Navigate to **Account Settings** by clicking on your avatar in the top right corner.

<Background>
![](https://assets.apidog.com/uploads/help/2023/07/18/90e93ff5139031843bdf2874a6ff2d99.png)
</Background>

3. In the left sidebar, select **My Account** and choose **Delete Account**.

<Background>
![](https://assets.apidog.com/uploads/help/2023/07/18/8aecfd7c1a8eb98697a72f591ae6dcb4.png)
</Background>

4. Click **Delete** and confirm your decision by selecting **Go to quit** in the warning message that appears.

<Background>
![](https://assets.apidog.com/uploads/help/2023/07/18/feaa0f4a9c51ad41d093a743f773e162.png)
</Background>

Following these steps will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

## How to Delete Your Apidog Account in a Team

If you are part of a team, you must exit or manage team-related settings before deleting your personal account.

1. Go to your team's section and open the **Settings** tab.

2. Look for the **Danger Zone**, where you have options to transfer ownership or dismiss the team.

<details>
<summary>Team Management Options</summary>

- **Transfer the Team:** Select a member to assign as the new owner and confirm the transfer.

- **Dismiss the Team:** Choose dismiss, confirm by entering the team's name, and proceed.

</details>

3. After managing team affairs, revisit **Account Settings** and select **My Account**.

4. Press the **Delete** button and confirm your decision by clicking **Go to quit**.

:::caution
Make sure all necessary preparations are made, as deleting your account removes your access to all Apidog services and data. For further assistance or concerns, please reach out to support@apidog.com.
:::
