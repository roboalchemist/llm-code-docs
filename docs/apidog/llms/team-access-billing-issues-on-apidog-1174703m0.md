# Team Access & Billing Issues on Apidog

#### Q. Why might some teams face access issues even after payment?
A: Sometimes, teams within an organization may not be properly linked to the main paid subscription. This can happen if a team was previously subscribed separately or not transferred into the paid organization. As a result, those teams might be on a free plan despite the overall payment, causing limited access or error messages.

#### Q. How can I identify the Team ID to troubleshoot access issues?
A: You can find the Team ID by going to the **Settings** tab of the specific team within Apidog. The Organization ID is available in your account or billing information.

#### Q. What causes double billing for teams in Apidog?
A: Double billing can happen if a team is billed separately from your main paid organization. For instance, if a team is not transferred into your paid organization, it may be charged independently.

#### Q. How can I fix the access issue for my team?
A: To restore full access for any affected team, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your Apidog account.
2. Select your paid organization (e.g., Org ID: 121212).

3. Go to the “**Teams**” section.

4. Click “**Transfer in**”.
5. Select the affected team (e.g., ID: 343434) from the dropdown list.
