# Account Settings

Apidog provides comprehensive account management features that allow you to personalize your profile, secure your account, and manage integrations. This guide covers password settings, username changes, email binding, third-party connections, and account deletion.

## Password Settings

1. Click your avatar in the upper right corner and select **Account Settings**.

</details>

## Changing Username

1. Go to the account settings page and click **Edit** in the **Name** section.

<details>
<summary>📷 Visual Reference</summary>

<Background>
![](https://assets.apidog.com/uploads/help/2023/07/12/8285581518df2ed3c43fa2039e23156c.png)
</Background>

</details>

2. Type your desired new username, click **Confirm**, and your username will be updated.

## My Connections

Link your Apidog account with third-party accounts if you signed up using an email.

1. From the Account Settings page, select the **My Connections** section.

<details>
<summary>📷 Visual Reference</summary>

<Background>
![](https://assets.apidog.com/uploads/help/2023/07/12/6d90ec515b437b0754ebf506bef139b8.png)
</Background>

</details>

2. Here, you can link your account with platforms like GitHub and Google.

## Deleting Your Account

For detailed instructions on permanently deleting your Apidog account, including important prerequisites and team management considerations, see [Deleting Account](https://docs.apidog.com/deleting-account-640859m0.md).

:::caution
Before deleting your account, ensure that you back up essential data, exit or disband any teams, and handle any active subscriptions. Account deletion is irreversible.
:::
