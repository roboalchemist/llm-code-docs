# Invited team member cannot logon to Apidog. 

### Can I move a paid team into a paid organization?
No, paid teams cannot be directly moved into a paid organization due to billing conflicts. You’ll need to downgrade the team to the free plan first and then  you will have the opportunity to move your team to organization.

### How do I proceed if I want to move my paid team into an organization?
 **Downgrade the team to the free plan** (Required cancellation of the current subscription)
-   Contact to Apidog support to request downgrade and pro-related refund for unused time.
-   Once downfraded, you can move the team into the organization.

### What information do I need to provide to support?
- **Team ID**(e.g, `123456`)
- **Organization ID** (e.g., `123456`)
- Preferred resolution (Downgrade + Refund / billing consolidation)

### Will I get refund if I downgrade?
Depending on the cycle and other terms, Apidog will issue refund based on unused time.

### Can I do this myself, or do I need support?
This process requires manual handling by the Apidog support team. Contact us here - [Apidog Support Center](https://docs.apidog.com/apidog-support-center-748035m0.md)
