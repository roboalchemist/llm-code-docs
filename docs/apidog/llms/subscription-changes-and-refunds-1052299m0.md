# Subscription Changes and Refunds

#### Q: Can I convert my current (monthly/ annual)subscription to another plan and get a refund for the difference?
A: We don’t offer direct conversions from one active plan to another (such as from annual to monthly or vice versa) with a partial refund. But don’t worry — we’ve got an easy way to help you make the switch!

#### Q: What happens if I want to switch from my current plan to another plan?
A: Simply cancel your current  subscription, and we’ll issue a full refund for that charge. Once that’s done, you’re free to subscribe to the desired plan anytime through your Apidog dashboard.

#### Q: How long does it take to receive a refund?
A: Refunds typically show up in your account within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

#### Q: How do I subscribe to the desired plan after cancellation?
A: After your current plan is cancelled and refunded, just head over to your Apidog dashboard to choose the desired plan that fits you best.

#### Q: Who can I contact if I need help during the subscription change process?
A: We’re here for you! If you get stuck or have questions about your subscription options, just reach out to our [Apidog Support Center](https://docs.apidog.com/apidog-support-center-748035m0.md) anytime.
