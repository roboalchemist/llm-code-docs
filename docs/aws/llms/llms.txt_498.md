## [Actions](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/ivs/latest/chatmsgapireference/actions.html)

- [DeleteMessage (Publish)](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/ivs/latest/chatmsgapireference/actions-deletemessage-publish.html): Instructs other clients to delete a message.
- [DisconnectUser (Publish)](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/ivs/latest/chatmsgapireference/actions-disconnectuser-publish.html): Disconnects another viewer from the Chat room.
- [SendMessage (Publish)](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/ivs/latest/chatmsgapireference/actions-sendmessage-publish.html): Sends a message to participants of the room.
- [Event (Subscribe)](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/ivs/latest/chatmsgapireference/actions-event-subscribe.html): Events are messages generated by client actions or operations; e.g., join or leave notifications.
- [Message (Subscribe)](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/ivs/latest/chatmsgapireference/actions-message-subscribe.html): Messages from other clients that are sent to the room.
