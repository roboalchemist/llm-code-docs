# Source: https://firebase.google.com/docs/crashlytics/ai-assistance.md.txt

This page describes at a high level how you can use AI assistance with
Crashlytics to understand, prioritize, fix, and manage issues in your app.
You can **get guidance from an AI tool in the Firebase console** or you can
**use AI-powered development tools via MCP** that can help you debug and fix
issues directly in your codebase.

## AI insights in the Crashlytics dashboard

- Offers a chat experience to answer questions and offer guidance.
  Available in any page of the Firebase console.

- Provides AI-generated insights for specific Crashlytics issues. Available
  within the Crashlytics dashboard.

- Does ***not*** have access to and cannot make changes to your actual codebase.

[Learn more](https://firebase.google.com/docs/crashlytics/ai-assistance-in-dashboard)

## AI assistance for Crashlytics via MCP

- Offers a conversational debugging experience that can answer questions,
  provide context, and offer guidance from within your choice of MCP-capable
  AI-powered development tools (like Gemini CLI, Claude Code, or Cursor).

- Provides MCP tools and prompts to your AI tool to directly debug and fix
  Crashlytics issues for you in your codebase.

- Lets you view prioritized issues and manage issues like changing state or
  adding notes.

[Learn more](https://firebase.google.com/docs/crashlytics/ai-assistance-mcp)
