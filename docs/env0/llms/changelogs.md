## 2026

<Update label="January 2026" description="Self-service SSO configuration and a refreshed Projects page.">
  ### Self-Service SSO: Speed Up Authentication and Stay in Control

  Configure Single Sign-On directly from Organization Settings. Set up Azure AD or SAML 2.0 authentication on your own schedule with full control over your enterprise authentication setup.

  **Key benefits:**

* **Immediate setup** - configure SSO during onboarding or anytime after
* **Full control** - manage domain mappings, role assignments, and settings on your schedule
* **Enterprise ready** - centralize user access and enforce your org's authentication policies

  [Read more](/changelogs/2026/01/self-service-sso)

</Update>
