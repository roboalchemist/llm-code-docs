# Setting Up Your Organization

> Create and configure your env zero organization during initial onboarding

Everything in env zero happens under the scope of an [Organization](/guides/admin-guide/organizations) . When you first login, you must first create an env zero organization. That happens as a part of the initial onboarding process.

### Creating another organization

1. In the bottom left corner, click on the organization drawer. When the drawer opens, click on the the **Create Organization** button.

<Frame caption="Create Organization Button">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/envzero-b61043c8/A22trGA9OCusm4S6/images/guides/getting-started/getting-started/218.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=A22trGA9OCusm4S6&q=85&s=37905a5b5b92227fefa4c28a55b427e3" alt="Organization drawer interface showing Create Organization button in bottom left corner" width="218" height="119" data-path="images/guides/getting-started/getting-started/218.png" />
</Frame>

1. Enter the `Name` for your organization. Organization names are unique across the system, so you may have to modify it if it's already being used.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/envzero-b61043c8/A22trGA9OCusm4S6/images/guides/getting-started/getting-started/2a331eb-create_organization_dialog.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=A22trGA9OCusm4S6&q=85&s=c72ff11aeb1a899f336c8d3f4a2b9978" alt="Create Organization dialog box showing organization name input field" width="1088" height="1132" data-path="images/guides/getting-started/getting-started/2a331eb-create_organization_dialog.png" />
</Frame>

1. You can enter a `description` and `logo` as well, but those are optional, and can be added later.
2. Click **Create Organization**

That's it! You are now logged in to env zero using your newly created organization. You are the organization administrator, so you have full access to everything in it.
