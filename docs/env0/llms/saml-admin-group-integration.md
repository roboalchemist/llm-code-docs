# 👔 SAML Admin Group Integration

> env0 already offers to keep your env0 teams in sync with your SAML IdP groups. Today we're announcing an extension of that - allowing you to set up a Group or Groups in your IdP that follows a specific convention of your choice, and env0 will automatically grant users in those groups Organizational Admin permissions.

env0 already offers to keep your env0 teams in sync with your SAML IdP groups, and Today we're announcing an extension of that - allowing you to set up a Group or Groups in your IdP that follows a specific convention of your choice, and env0 will automatically grant users in those groups Organizational Admin permissions.

This advanced new feature can come in handy if you have (or planning to have) such groups which contain all users that should be Organization Admins on env0.

More about how how to use and enable it can be found [here](/guides/sso-integrations/importing-roles-or-groups-from-your-identity-provider).
