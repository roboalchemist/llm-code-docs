# Launch App

> 📘 Salesforce Step
>
> This is a Salesforce step.

The **Launch App** step opens the selected Salesforce app.

:fa-arrow-right: **To add a Launch App step:**

1. In the editor, add a step by clicking the + button.
2. Under the **Salesforce steps** tab, click **Common operations** and select **Launch app** step.\
   The **Launch app** step is added and the following **Object properties** is displayed.

   <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/818df59-2024-05-05_16-42-34.png" />
3. Under **Select app**, select the desired Salesforce app from the drop-down menu.
4. When finished, click **Save**.
