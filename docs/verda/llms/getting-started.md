# Getting Started

To use the models on Verda, you’ll need to create an account and add funds to your project balance. Follow the steps below to get set up in minutes.

### 1. Create Your Verda Account

1. Visit <https://console.verda.com/signin>.
2. Fill in the registration form and click **Create account**.
3. Check your email and verify your address to activate the account.
4. After verification, log in and go to your console: [https://console.verda.com](https://console.verda.com/signin).

### 2. Add Funds to Your Project

1. Navigate to **Billing & settings** in the dashboard.
2. Under **Add funds to your project balance**, enter the desired amount.
3. Add a payment method and click **Save billing details**.
4. Press **Complete payment** to finish the process.

Once your balance is topped up, you're ready to start using our models.
