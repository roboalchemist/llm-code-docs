# Built-in Reports

Built-in reports provide ready-made views of your security data, pre-designed and immediately available. These reports are created by OX, cannot be modified by users, and are visible to everyone in your organization.

With built-in reports, you can quickly assess your organization's security posture by leveraging standard dashboards, no setup required. They serve as a foundation of insight, letting you spot trends, monitor risk areas, and share key metrics without building a report from scratch.

In the Reports list, you can view both built-in reports and any custom ones you have created. Built-in reports are labeled **Created by OX**, while custom ones display the creator’s name.

The following built-in reports are available:

* [Executive Reports](https://docs.ox.security/generate-reports/built-in-reports/executive-reports)
* [SBOM Reports](https://docs.ox.security/generate-reports/built-in-reports/sbom-reports)
* [SLA Reports](https://docs.ox.security/generate-reports/built-in-reports/sla-reports)
* [Active Issues Reports](https://docs.ox.security/generate-reports/built-in-reports/active-issues-report)
* [VibeSec Usage Reports](https://docs.ox.security/generate-reports/built-in-reports/vibesec-usage-reports)
* [OSC\&R](https://docs.ox.security/generate-reports/built-in-reports/oscar-coverage-reports)
