# Skill Badge Sash

## Sew Together

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/674/medium800/braincrafts_skill-badge-sash-10.jpg?1396775020)

Stitch a seam all the way across one shoulder, and at either end of the other shoulder seam, leaving a space for turning it right side out later. Check out the illustration above.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/675/medium800/braincrafts_skill-badge-sash-11.jpg?1396775038)

Line up the right sides of your sash-length pieces.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/676/medium800/braincrafts_skill-badge-sash-13.jpg?1396775057)

Pin all around the edges, and pin the shoulder seam allowances open.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/677/medium800/braincrafts_skill-badge-sash-15.jpg?1396775074)

Stitch around the pinned perimeter.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/683/medium800/braincrafts_skill-badge-sash-16.jpg?1396775170)
