# Resistor Helper

## Assembly

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/285/medium800/braincrafts_resistor-helper-06.jpg?1396770643)

Carefully cut out the viewing windows in the holder.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/286/medium800/braincrafts_resistor-helper-10.jpg?1396770660)

Cut small slits over the red dots on the holder (both sides = 8 cuts). This is where the paper fasteners will anchor each wheel.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/287/medium800/braincrafts_resistor-helper-09.jpg?1396770673)

Shorten the tabs on four paper fasteners with a pair of clippers. This is optional but we think it looks nicer!

  
![braincrafts_resistor-helper-11.jpg](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/290/medium640/braincrafts_resistor-helper-11.jpg?1396770704)

![braincrafts_resistor-helper-12.jpg](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/291/medium640/braincrafts_resistor-helper-12.jpg?1396770712)

Use a ruler to gently score (but not cut!) the fold line on the holder.

The holder folds easily and neatly after scoring.

  
![braincrafts_resistor-helper-13.jpg](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/292/medium640/braincrafts_resistor-helper-13.jpg?1396770723)

![braincrafts_resistor-helper-14.jpg](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/293/medium640/braincrafts_resistor-helper-14.jpg?1396770736)

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/294/medium800/braincrafts_resistor-helper-15.jpg?1396770749)

Pierce a paper fastener through a tab on one side of the holder and through a wheel.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/295/medium800/braincrafts_resistor-helper-16.jpg?1396770759)

You can assemble the Resistor Helper with either side as the “front,” but we think you’ll use the 4-band side more often.

Pierce through the opposite side of the holder and flare out the tabs on the fastener to secure. Repeat with the other three wheels.

  
![braincrafts_resistor-helper-18.jpg](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/297/medium640/braincrafts_resistor-helper-18.jpg?1396770782)

![braincrafts_resistor-helper-19.jpg](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/298/medium640/braincrafts_resistor-helper-19.jpg?1396770788)

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/299/medium800/braincrafts_resistor-helper-20.jpg?1396770796)

Once you’ve verified you assembled the wheels in the right order, carefully glue together the corners/edges of the holder.

![](https://cdn-learn.adafruit.com/assets/assets/000/001/300/medium800/braincrafts_resistor-helper-21.jpg?1396770805)

That’s it! Use and enjoy!
