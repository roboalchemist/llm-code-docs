# IML variables

IML variables may differ depending on the module or connection type.

Always check available IML variables in the specification of the [module](https://developers.make.com/custom-apps-documentation/app-components/modules) or [connection](https://developers.make.com/custom-apps-documentation/app-components/connections) you are using.
