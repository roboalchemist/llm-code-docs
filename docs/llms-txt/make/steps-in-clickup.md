# Steps in ClickUp

To create OAuth 2.0 credentials for Clickup, follow these steps:

1. Log into ClickUp.
2. Click on your avatar in the lower-left corner and click **Integrations**.
3. Click **ClickUp API**.
4. Click **Create an App**.
5. Enter a name for your app and your [OAauth redirect URI](https://developers.make.com/white-label-documentation/install-and-configure-apps/oauth-2.0-setup).
6. After creating your app, your Client ID and secret appear. Copy and save them in a safe place.
