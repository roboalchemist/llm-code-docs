# Custom domains

Make's custom domains support the following:

* Public and admin interfaces are both available on your custom domain
* Emails from the custom domain to your end customers
* OAuth redirects with the custom domain
* [Base and Primary domains](https://developers.make.com/white-label-documentation/customize-your-instance/custom-domains/base-and-primary-domains) for your instance
* API calls, webhooks, and mailhooks using any configured domain

Existing customers can contact their CSM with their desired custom domain to start the process. Check the requirements before you begin the process of creating your custom domain.
