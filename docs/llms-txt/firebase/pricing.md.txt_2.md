Although Firebase PNV requires a billing account to be attached to your Firebase
project, during the preview phase, you won't be billed for the service. The
following table shows you how the service will be billed when it becomes
generally available. Billing only occurs when a phone number is successfully
returned to your application.

| Region | Tier 1 (0 to 99,999) | Tier 2 (100,000 to 999,999) | Tier 3 (1,000,000+) | Enterprise (Custom) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Germany (DE) | $0.0743 | $0.0715 | $0.0688 | Contact Sales |
| Indonesia (ID) | $0.1350 | $0.1300 | $0.1250 | Contact Sales |
| Malaysia (MY) | $0.0810 | $0.0780 | $0.0750 | Contact Sales |
