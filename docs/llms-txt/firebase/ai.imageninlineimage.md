## ImagenInlineImage.mimeType

The MIME type of the image; either `"image/png"` or `"image/jpeg"`.

To request a different format, set the `imageFormat` property in your [ImagenGenerationConfig](https://firebase.google.com/docs/reference/js/ai.imagengenerationconfig.md#imagengenerationconfig_interface).

**Signature:**  

    mimeType: string;
