# Develop with AI assistance

## Develop with AI assistance


Get help or information about Firebase and your project.


Access agentive AI tools that can make changes to your code
and Firebase project.
[![](http://firebase.google.com/static/docs/ai-assistance/images/firebase-studio-16-9.png)](http://firebase.google.com/docs/studio)

### [Firebase Studio](http://firebase.google.com/docs/studio)

Rapidly prototype, build, and ship full-stack AI-infused apps quickly and efficiently, right from your browser. [![](http://firebase.google.com/static/docs/ai-assistance/images/gif-16-9.png)](http://firebase.google.com/docs/ai-assistance/gemini-in-firebase)

### [Gemini in Firebase](http://firebase.google.com/docs/ai-assistance/gemini-in-firebase)

Streamline development with an AI-powered collaborative assistant available in Firebase services and interfaces, like the console. [![](http://firebase.google.com/static/docs/ai-assistance/images/mcp-logo.png)](http://firebase.google.com/docs/ai-assistance/mcp-server)

### [Firebase MCP server](http://firebase.google.com/docs/ai-assistance/mcp-server)

Install our MCP server to access and use Firebase-specific agentive tools and prompts in your favorite AI-powered development tools. [![](http://firebase.google.com/static/docs/ai-assistance/images/gemini-cli-logo.png)](http://firebase.google.com/docs/ai-assistance/gcli-extension)

### [Gemini CLI extension](http://firebase.google.com/docs/ai-assistance/gcli-extension)

Use our Firebase extension to give the Gemini CLI more Firebase-specific capabilities and expertise, including everything available from the Firebase MCP server.
