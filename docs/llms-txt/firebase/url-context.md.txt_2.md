# Firebase.AI.UrlContext Struct Reference

# Firebase.AI.UrlContext

A tool that allows you to provide additional context to the models in the form of public web URLs.

## Summary

By including URLs in your request, the Gemini model will access the content from those pages to inform and enhance its response.
