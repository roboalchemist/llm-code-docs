The IANA standard MIME type of the image file; either `"image/png"` or `"image/jpeg"`.

Note: To request a different format, set `ImageFormat` in your `https://firebase.google.com/docs/reference/unity/struct/firebase/a-i/imagen-generation-config#struct_firebase_1_1_a_i_1_1_imagen_generation_config`.
