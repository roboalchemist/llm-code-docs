*** ** * ** ***

Thrown on security sensitive operations on a [FirebaseUser](https://firebase.google.com/docs/reference/android/com/google/firebase/auth/FirebaseUser) instance that require the user to have signed in recently, when the requirement isn't met.

Resolve this exception by asking the user for their credentials again, and verifying them by calling [reauthenticate](https://firebase.google.com/docs/reference/android/com/google/firebase/auth/FirebaseUser#reauthenticate(com.google.firebase.auth.AuthCredential)).
