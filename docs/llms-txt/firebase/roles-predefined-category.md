These roles grant full read/write or read-only access to groups of products. They are structured aroundGoogle Analyticsand general product categories.

Assign these product-category roles to project members using the[Firebaseconsole](https://console.firebase.google.com/project/_/settings/iam)or the[Google Cloudconsole](https://console.cloud.google.com/iam-admin/iam).
| **Note:** If you want more granular predefined roles for your project members, you can assign[product-level roles](https://firebase.google.com/docs/projects/iam/roles-predefined-product).
