# Team Setup and Integrations

<figure><img src="https://1990502200-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FtAIPUIiSH7MWC0IHLJuD%2Fuploads%2Fu6dqbhVOhLZzpildW5i0%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=066c5d0a-b87c-435f-b37e-915bdeabcb15" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Key Benefits

* **All Jams in one place:** Avoid duplicate bug reporting with centralized team workspace
* **Access control:** Set domain-based permissions or per-Jam sharing
* **Cost-effective scaling:** Free viewer seats for stakeholders, paid seats for creators
* **Easy onboarding:** Domain-based workspace discovery helps teammates find and join your team

### Invite your team

1. Log in to [jam.dev](https://jam.dev/login)
2. Click **Invite** in the dashboard header
3. Invite team members via email or team invite link&#x20;
4. Optional: modify the team member roles, here's how: [members-and-roles](https://jam.dev/docs/administration/members-and-roles "mention")

#### Setup Process

1. Go to **Settings** in your workspace
2. Select **Integrations**
3. Connect your preferred tools
4. Choose an integration when creating Jams

### Send Jams to Integrations

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="New Jams" %}

<figure><img src="https://1990502200-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FtAIPUIiSH7MWC0IHLJuD%2Fuploads%2FAmak2XI9jVnLLGGXhW8M%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a131b64b-555d-44d1-b881-1b27af86a190" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

\
You can send any new created Jams to integrated tools:

1. Capture a Jam
2. Click the "`link to share`" select menu
3. Select your destination integration
4. Click create to send your Jam to your selected integration
   {% endtab %}

You can send previously created Jams to integrated tools:

1. Open any existing Jam
2. Click the `send to app` button
3. Select your destination integration
4. Click create to send your Jam to your integration
