<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_Vzrq" id="join-the-weaviate-community">Join the Weaviate Community<a href="#join-the-weaviate-community" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Join the Weaviate Community" title="Direct link to Join the Weaviate Community" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>A lot of community chat happens on the <a href="https://weaviate.io/slack" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Weaviate Community Slack</a>, with longer-form discussions taking place on the <a href="https://forum.weaviate.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">forum</a></p>
<p>Many members of our community help us by giving feedback, asking questions, or proposing ideas. To get involved in our community, please make sure to familiarize yourself with the project&#x27;s <a href="https://weaviate.io/service/code-of-conduct" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Code of Conduct</a>.</p>
<p>Please set your forum or Slack workspace display name to your name. This will make it easier to connect with other community members. Then reach out to members of the community, introduce yourself, and share your ideas/questions. Tell us about your areas of interest and what technologies you are using to build your projects. The more we know about you, the better we will be able to match project requirements to your interests and abilities.</p>
<p>If at any time you face any difficulties, don&#x27;t hesitate to reach out in the <code>#general</code> channel on our Slack for quick help, or in the <a href="https://forum.weaviate.io/c/general/4" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">General category of the forum</a> for more complex issues. Our team and the community will help you solve your problem.</p>

<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_Vzrq" id="further-resources">Further resources<a href="#further-resources" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Further resources" title="Direct link to Further resources" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>Navigating a new project can be difficult, and it takes time to become acquainted with the codebase. If you haven&#x27;t yet, we suggest going through the <a class="" href="/weaviate/quickstart">Weaviate Quickstart guide</a>.</p>
<p>Here are additional resources that will help you familiarize with Weaviate and its applications:</p>
<ul>
<li class=""><a href="https://weaviate.io/blog" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Weaviate Blog</a> – a series of blog around Weaviate and the overall vector search space.</li>
<li class=""><a href="https://newsletter.weaviate.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Weaviate Newsletter</a> – bi-weekly newsletter with updates on the latest and greatest announcements.</li>
<li class=""><a href="https://github.com/weaviate/awesome-weaviate" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Awesome Weaviate</a> – a list of curated examples and tutorials on how to use Weaviate.</li>
<li class=""><a href="https://github.com/weaviate/weaviate-examples" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Weaviate Examples</a> – a repository of various example projects created by the community. Each example shows a different Weaviate use case. You can add your own examples too!</li>
<li class=""><a href="https://www.youtube.com/c/SeMI-and-Weaviate/featured" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Weaviate&#x27;s YouTube Channel</a> – podcasts and live demos showcasing Weaviate, and providing insight into the vector search space in general.</li>
</ul>
