<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_Vzrq" id="chonkie-and-weaviate">Chonkie and Weaviate<a href="#chonkie-and-weaviate" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Chonkie and Weaviate" title="Direct link to Chonkie and Weaviate" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>Chonkie offers <a href="https://docs.chonkie.ai/oss/chefs/overview" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">three techniques</a> for parsing your documents: <code>TableChef</code>, <code>TextChef</code>, and <code>MarkdownChef</code>.</p>
<p>Once the data is preprocessed, you can <a href="https://docs.chonkie.ai/oss/handshakes/weaviate-handshake" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">ingest it into Weaviate</a> using the <code>WeaviateHandshake</code>.</p>
