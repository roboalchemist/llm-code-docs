# Graphs & TradingView

Architect offers three different modes for price graphs, which can be configured on the top right on the Trade tab. In the [Advanced ](https://docs.architect.co/user-guide/tech-support/desktop/simple-and-advanced-layouts)layout, the graphs will take you directly to the TradingView. \
