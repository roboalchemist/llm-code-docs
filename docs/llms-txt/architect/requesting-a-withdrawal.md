# Requesting a Withdrawal

Visit the [Transfers ](https://app.architect.co/user/transfers)page! Architect uses [Plaid & Orum ](https://docs.architect.co/user-guide/my-account/deposits-and-withdrawals/plaid-and-orum)to allow you to safely and securely withdraw funds to your linked bank account.

You can withdraw the Excess Cash in your account, which is the US Dollar balance in excess of the [margin required](https://docs.architect.co/user-guide/futures-101/margin-and-leverage) to support your current futures positions. \
\
Please [contact-us](https://docs.architect.co/user-guide/contact-us "mention") if you need additional assistance, including if you would like to request a wire transfer or non-linked ACH transfer.&#x20;
