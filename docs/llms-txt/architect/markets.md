# Markets

The Markets page is a dashboard of available products to trade.&#x20;

The default contract to trade will be the front month contract (the contract with the highest volume), but advanced users can also select a specific contract expiry in the dropdown.

There are available filters to narrow by asset type (Agriculture, Crypto, FX, etc).&#x20;
