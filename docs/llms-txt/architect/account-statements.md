# Account Statements

Your account information is available on the [Statements ](https://app.architect.co/user/statements)page, including daily and monthly statements.&#x20;

In addition, our [FCM ](https://docs.architect.co/user-guide/futures-101/ibs-and-fcms)partners may be required to directly send you account statements, which will typically arrive by e-mail.&#x20;
