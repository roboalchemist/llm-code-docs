## Frequently asked questions

If you can’t find what you’re looking for, contact us.

### What is Embed Notion Pages' password protection?

It’s a simple way to add a password gate to any Notion page you embed on your website. Once enabled, visitors will need to enter the correct password to view your content—adding an extra layer of privacy and control.

### Why should I password-protect my Notion embeds?

Password protection ensures that sensitive content like client dashboards, internal docs, or exclusive content isn't accessible to just anyone with a link. It's a lightweight but powerful way to control access without needing a full login system.

### Who should use password protection for Notion embeds?

Anyone who shares Notion content publicly but wants to limit access—for freelancers, agencies, educators, or teams. Whether you're managing a client portal, sharing paid resources, or organizing team materials, password protection helps you keep it private and professional.

### How does password protection work?

When enabled, anyone accessing your embedded Notion page will need to enter the correct password before viewing the content.

### Can I change or remove the password later?

Yes! You can update or remove the password anytime from your dashboard.

### Is password protection included in the free plan?

No, password protection is available in the Pro and Unbound plans.
