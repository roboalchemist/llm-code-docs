# Setting up Your Account

This article shows you how to install the Testim extension and sign up for a  Testim account (free trial). Once you have that in place, you’ll be ready to start creating tests.

## Installing the Testim Extension

:fa-arrow-right: **To Install the Testim Extension:**

1. Go to the Chrome Web Store to download the extension [here](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/testim-editor/pebeiooilphfmbohdbhbomomkkoghoia).

> 📘
>
> [Why do I need the Testim extension? ](https://help.testim.io/docs/why-do-you-need-testim-extension)

2. Click **Add to Chrome**, then in the confirmation window click **Add Extension**.\
   Once the installation is completed, the Testim icon appears in your Chrome toolbar.
3. Click on the icon and then click on **Login to start**.

<Image align="center" className="border" width="80%" border={true} src="https://files.readme.io/e45eaec-Testim030.png" />

## Signing Up

If you haven’t set up an account yet, you will need to do so now.

> 👍
>
> It’s free up to 1000 runs a month!  You are eligible for a 7-day free trial, [contact us](https://www.testim.io/root-cause/contact-us/) and we will enable that for you.

> 📘
>
> If you already have an account, just sign in and you can start running tests.

:fa-arrow-right: **To sign up for an account:**

1. At the bottom of the Sign in window, click **Sign up**.\
   The Sign up screen is displayed.

![](https://files.readme.io/fc6e71410d3f85abddbb20da480f39a03de28161b59fc316c49b889e3f9b1d7d-image.png)

<br />

2. Enter your **Work Email**, **Country** and **Password** in the appropriate fields.

> 📘
>
> Password must be 8 characters long and contain at least one digit, one symbol and no spaces.

3. Select the **I'm not a robot** challenge.
4. Click **Start**.
5. Choose your role to help up customize your experience.

   <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/0b34acc112f11d6d7c724a0f247fd2dee76fcd0587e874e40c9303545dccbbe5-2024-09-09_15-40-38.png" />

   <br />
6. Select one or more ways that you would like to use Testim and click **Continue**.

   <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/317a5ffbbfd59f22026d5391671f724b75690c0f09d721aafb1276525fc9212f-2024-09-09_15-41-34.png" />

<br />

Your free trial account is activated and you are directed to install the [Testim Extension](https://help.testim.io/docs/why-do-you-need-testim-extension).
