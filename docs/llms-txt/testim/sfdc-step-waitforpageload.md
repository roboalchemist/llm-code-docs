# Wait for Page Load

> 📘 Salesforce Step
>
> This is a Salesforce step.

The Wait for Page Load step waits until the page is fully loaded before moving on to the next step.

:fa-arrow-right: **To add a Wait for Page Load step:**

1. In the editor, add a step by clicking the + button.
2. Under the **Salesforce steps** tab, click **Common operations** and select **Wait for Page Load** step.
3. When finished, click **Save**.
