# Purchase Prepaid Credits

To purchase prepaid credits, go to your workspace's [Plan & Billing page](https://app.roboflow.com/the-analytics-zone/settings/plan) and navigate to the section for Prepaid Credits.

Specify how many credits you want to add to your account then click Purchase.
