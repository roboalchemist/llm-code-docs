# New Augmentations: Camera Gain & Motion Blur

We have added two new image augmentation options to help your models generalize better in real-world conditions:

* Camera Gain: Simulates sensor ISO noise to help models handle grainy, low-light footage.
* Motion Blur: Mimics the visual effect of movement during image capture.
