# Train and Deploy SmolVLM2 and SmolVLM 256M Models

<figure><img src="https://2667452268-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMR3m936tBXGm5QsAcPwe%2Fuploads%2FiIqjUOnrFKwInDfEOpCO%2FScreenshot%202025-06-09%20at%2010.53.22.png?alt=media&#x26;token=9f494dcd-308b-4f20-a10e-11bbe96bfc7c" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can now train SmolVLM2 and SmolVLM 256M multimodal models on Roboflow. This feature is available by generating a dataset version and selecting the option to train a model. You will then see SmolVLM as an option. From there, you can choose whether you want to train a SmolVLM2 or SmolVLM 256M model.

Both model types can be deployed in a [Roboflow Workflow](https://docs.roboflow.com/workflows/what-is-workflows).
