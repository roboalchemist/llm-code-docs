# Update Payment Method

1. To update your payment method, go to your workspace's [Plan & Billing page](https://app.roboflow.com/the-analytics-zone/settings/plan) and select "Go to Portal".

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-83bc01e96fd9204dc26f359be6f6fb1b0f84d019%2Fimage.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. In the billing portal, select "Add payment method"
3. Enter in all the details from your payment method and click "Add". If you want to use the the payment method as the default method, also check this box.
