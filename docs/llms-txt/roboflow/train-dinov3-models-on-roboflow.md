# Train DINOv3 Models on Roboflow

<figure><img src="https://2667452268-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMR3m936tBXGm5QsAcPwe%2Fuploads%2F40Zv5YQ98paja1HNX1l0%2FScreenshot%202025-11-24%20at%2016.10.12.png?alt=media&#x26;token=68de607e-9105-4062-9382-70e13744fa13" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can now train DINOv3 classification models on Roboflow. DINOv3 models train fast, work well across different image resolutions, and runs at a speed comparable to ViT model.

To train a DINOv3 model, create a new dataset version in a Classification project, then choose the "DINOv3" model architecture.

You can then deploy your DINOv3 model in Roboflow Workflows in the classification Workflow blocks.
