# Cancel a Plan

To cancel a paid plan, go to your workspace's [Plan & Billing page](https://app.roboflow.com/the-analytics-zone/settings/plan) and select "Cancel Plan".

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-569393619e734dedc9f6502dd58481ecc3a8c9ab%2Fimage.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On the next screen, confirm that you're sure you want to cancel your plan.

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-cda9cd9383a027de2efc98722cfaee100f099284%2Fimage.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You'll receive a confirmation that your plan has been downgraded.

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-769af959f33e6dfb792d052cc07d025b3cabf19a%2Fimage.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
When you cancel your plan, you'll continue to have access to Roboflow at your current plan level until the end of your current billing cycle.
{% endhint %}
