# Download an Image

You can download a single image from a dataset.

To do so, first open the image you want to download in Roboflow Annotate:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-369febfe31527d3826ea402cc358fa3bad18e58c%2FScreenshot%202025-05-20%20at%2017.54.48.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Then, click the three dots in the top right corner:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-51bd55cfe890e293ac96e6fec7319af3afa2a50e%2FScreenshot%202025-05-20%20at%2017.56.00.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click the "Download Image" button.

The image will then be downloaded to your computer.
