# Use Instant Models in Label Assist

{% embed url="<https://www.loom.com/share/ac8bfb92591041eba9c854836fe70ed8?sid=db384008-3a0e-4fc5-ac51-3ffa97c797d9>" %}

You can now use [Roboflow Instant](https://docs.roboflow.com/train/roboflow-instant) models in [Label Assist](https://docs.roboflow.com/annotate/ai-labeling/model-assisted-labeling).

Instant models are automatically trained when you approve annotations in your dataset. They train in a few minutes, as opposed to larger models that take longer to train.

By using Instant models in Label Assist, you can use Instant models to annotate data faster than ever.
