# Apply for Research Credits

We offer additional platform credits for students and researchers in academia.

These credits are available for anyone with an academic email address and who is using Roboflow for non-commercial work related to research or academia.

By applying for a research plan, all data in the workspace where you make your application will be made public on [Roboflow Universe](https://universe.roboflow.com/).

### Research Plan

The following plan is available for students and researchers:

| Plan Features                |
| ---------------------------- |
| Enhanced image augmentations |
| 50 credits per month         |
| 15 team members              |
| 20 projects                  |

The Research Plan is ideal if you are training models or managing large datasets.

### Apply for Research Credits

To apply for research credits, go to [**Plan & Billing Settings**](https://app.roboflow.com/settings/plan) (or go there through "Settings" -> "Plan & Billing")

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fm0bFvmDNDhzmhAroXY6P%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=2084ee78-2f1f-4cb6-bc74-2d95a53845a9" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Scroll down to the bottom of the Plan & Billing page to the "Using Roboflow for research or education?" option, then click "Request Access":

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2FgWxJscyout8pVpFhHp1i%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=45890ded-d1f8-4ed7-90cd-ae06614aa31e" alt=""><figcaption><p>Request Access to the Edu/Research plan inside Plan &#x26; Billing Settings</p></figcaption></figure>

A window will then appear from which you can choose your plan, and confirm you want to upgrade your Workspace.

### Roboflow in the Classroom

Students and teachers using Roboflow in the classroom are encouraged to contact us for help developing course materials or ensuring account access meets the needs of your class. Hobbyists working on projects can earn additional image and training credits by sharing work in blog posts, videos, forum discussions, and more.
