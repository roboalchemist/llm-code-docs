# Terminate a Stream

If you don't need to momentarily pause a stream but terminate it's configuration fully and permanently, you can terminate a video stream from the Device Manager dashboard.

To terminate a Stream, click on a Device in Deployment Manager, then click on the three dots next to the Stream you want to stop in your Stream list. Click "Terminate Stream":

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-ed9e4de22d5896f29e933d4c4c9ae3d290797ca6%2FScreenshot%202025-06-27%20at%2011.00.32.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

:information\_source: Note that terminating a stream removes it's configuration entirely and it's configuration may not be recoverable. If you may need to restart a stream with that configuration, we recommend you pause a stream instead.
