# Show and Hide Annotations

You can show and hide annotations on an image in the Roboflow Annotate dashboard.

This is helpful if you want to see the underlying image below annotations.

To show and hide annotations, click the eye icon in the top right corner of the Roboflow Annotate interface, or press the `H` key:
