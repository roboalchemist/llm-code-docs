# Compare SAM 3 with Other Vision Models in Playground

You can now compare Segment Anything 3 with other zero-shot detection models for free using Roboflow Playground, our web-based playground for using, comparing, and evaluating state-of-the-art computer vision models.

[To get started, open Roboflow Playground and select SAM 3 as one of your models to use.](https://playground.roboflow.com/)
