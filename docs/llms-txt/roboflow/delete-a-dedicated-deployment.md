# Delete a Dedicated Deployment

You can delete a Dedicated Deployment from:

1. The Deployments page in your Roboflow dashboard, and;
2. The Dedicated Deployments tab of the "Choose an Inference Server" setting in the Workflow editor.

### Delete a Dedicated Deployment from the Deployments List

First, click "Deployments" from the left sidebar of your Roboflow dashboard, then click the "Dedicated Deployments" tab.

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-cf645a31bf2c3be43abf6c9211fb063761c464c8%2FScreenshot%202025-06-27%20at%2011.59.34.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Then, click the trash icon next to the Dedicated Deployment you want to delete.

You will then be asked to confirm that you want to delete the Dedicated Deployment:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-fe6cc955d2c5d4c6d4baa9698e4c8509e3a6c893%2FScreenshot%202025-06-27%20at%2012.00.05.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "Delete Dedicated Deployment" to delete your deployment. This is permanent and irrevocable.

### Delete a Dedicated Deployment from the Workflows Editor

You can also delete a Dedicated Deployment from the "Choose an Inference Server" page in the Workflows editor. To delete the Deployment, first open a Workflow, then click the "Running on" option:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2FK7OSh7RVSlBiDEARtE95%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=d0467b5c-3e45-42b6-bd84-eda8e7ec4625" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

A window will appear with several deployment options. Click "Dedicated Deployments", then click the trash icon next to the deployment you want to delete:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2FTCtpZgYObzLXW5syJv3o%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e3d1b391-28bf-4f23-a447-e0f9afd1b4a4" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will then be asked to confirm that you want to delete the deployment:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-f4a2e23930341fca9fb080df5499719eb0de77c4%2FScreenshot%202025-06-27%20at%2012.02.21.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "Delete Dedicated Deployment" to delete your deployment. This is permanent and irrevocable.
