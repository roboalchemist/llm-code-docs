# New Workspace Invitation Instructions

<figure><img src="https://2667452268-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FMR3m936tBXGm5QsAcPwe%2Fuploads%2F7nvOnd1RW20ABhE0i2sk%2Fimage%20(2).png?alt=media&#x26;token=91f7348b-ad2e-43e1-8c0a-f609e75aac52" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

When a user accepts an invite to your Workspace, they will now see a pop up that shows:

1. A list of what they can do given the role they have been assigned, and;
2. Links to guides to help them get started.

You will see this window when you accept an invitation to a Workspace from an email or an in-app notification.
