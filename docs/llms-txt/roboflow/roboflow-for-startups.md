# Roboflow for Startups

Roboflow now offers one free year of our paid plans to startups who participate in select partner accelerators or venture capital deal programs.

To check for eligibility, go to the Perks page associated with your accelerator or investor and check for Roboflow. If Roboflow is offered as a perk, you will see information on how to claim your Roboflow for Startups deal.

To learn more, read our [Roboflow for Startups page](https://startups.roboflow.com/).
