# Delete an Image

There are two ways to remove an image from a Project:

1. In Roboflow Annotate: Ideal for deleting a single image.
2. From the Dataset page that lists all the images in your dataset: Ideal for deleting multiple images.

### Delete a Single Image in Roboflow Annotate

To delete an image from a Roboflow project:

1. Click on the image you want to delete on the Dataset tab associated with your project.
2. Click the three dots in the top right corner of the page.
3. Click "Remove From Project".
4. Click "Confirm" in the confirmation pop up.

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-986ead827d5ac440c12de4356577d673b08dd396%2FScreenshot%202023-07-05%20at%2011.59.59.png?alt=media" alt="" width="316"><figcaption><p>Select "Remove From Project" to remove an image from a project.</p></figcaption></figure>

After you click "Confirm", your image and any associated annotations will be permanently deleted.

### Delete Multiple Images from the Dataset Page

To delete multiple images from a dataset, navigate to the Dataset page in your Project:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-7e4d48f3b617422b3ea508841e49059f1157068d%2FScreenshot%202025-05-20%20at%2017.52.04.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Then, hover over an image and click the checkbox in the top right corner of the image:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-b5badbc7e9c0f4e46d66d5f6e8fe1d82d7b4242a%2FScreenshot%202025-05-20%20at%2017.52.39.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can then click on more images if you want.

Once you have selected all of the images you want to remove, click the "Actions" dropdown then click "Remove from Project":

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-4ff98ce4aee4f83a71cf3a59f8483ce0d8571076%2FScreenshot%202025-05-20%20at%2017.53.06.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will then be asked to confirm that you want to delete the image(s) you have selected:

<figure><img src="https://662926385-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-M6S9nPJhEX9FYH6clfW%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-1cb4e19285149fc014f9cc19bfbcaa7a7fa2b40f%2FScreenshot%202025-05-20%20at%2017.53.37.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "Delete" when you are ready to delete the image(s) you have selected. This step is irrevocable.
