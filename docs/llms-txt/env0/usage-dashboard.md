# 📊 Usage Dashboard

> Exciting news! We've just launched the 📊 **Usage Dashboard**, a handy tool to keep tabs on your system's usage without the headache.
Located in **Organization Settings > Billing**.

Exciting news! We've just launched the 📊 **Usage Dashboard**, a handy tool to keep tabs on your system's usage without the headache.

Located in **Organization Settings > Billing**.

## ✨ What to Expect ✨

You can stay on top of things by viewing your total **users**, all your **monthly deployments**, and their **deployment limits**.

You can also view usage going as far back as the past 12 months:
