# Quick Reference

> Get started with env zero - onboarding, setup, and deploying your first cloud environment

Hi and welcome to env zero!

env zero is a powerful, easy to use, automation platform that allows you to quickly deploy and manage your cloud environments, all while maintaining visibility, predictability, and governance over what's happening.

## Onboarding Process

When you first login to env zero, you'll notice the onboarding welcome page. Clicking on *Get Started* will take you to the onboarding process that will guide you through setting up and deploying your first environment in env zero.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/envzero-b61043c8/A22trGA9OCusm4S6/images/guides/getting-started/fd34b85-screen_shot_2022-06-20_at_13.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=A22trGA9OCusm4S6&q=85&s=9695dbcb7fc7152911375279eff7a4b3" alt="" width="980" height="761" data-path="images/guides/getting-started/fd34b85-screen_shot_2022-06-20_at_13.png" />

First, you must create your initial env zero organization. After setting up your organization, you can either go through the onboarding process of deploying your first env zero environment, or decide to skip it and do it by yourself later on. Note that you can head out of the onboarding process anytime by clicking the top-right X button.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/envzero-b61043c8/A22trGA9OCusm4S6/images/guides/getting-started/8b1ddd9-screen_shot_2022-06-20_at_13.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=A22trGA9OCusm4S6&q=85&s=7034db12958bd23b8e1ab43b4b861e11" alt="" width="978" height="755" data-path="images/guides/getting-started/8b1ddd9-screen_shot_2022-06-20_at_13.png" />

The onboarding process will guide you through several aspects that your future env zero environment needs:

1. Select the relevant Infrastructure-as-Code framework.
2. Provide VCS information so env zero can fetch your IaC code.
3. Share the credentials of your relevant cloud.
4. Set up any required variables for your environment.
5. Configure the details of your env zero environment (such as name and etc.)

Eventually, by clicking the ***Start using env zero*** button, env zero will deploy your environment!

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/envzero-b61043c8/A22trGA9OCusm4S6/images/guides/getting-started/bbb94de-screen_shot_2022-06-20_at_13.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=A22trGA9OCusm4S6&q=85&s=45e97e39324472d84a64cc66ae374ead" alt="" width="980" height="760" data-path="images/guides/getting-started/bbb94de-screen_shot_2022-06-20_at_13.png" />

That's it! It's that simple to deploy a new environment with env zero. Feel free to check out your newly created environment and unlock some more additional features!
