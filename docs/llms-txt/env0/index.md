# Overview: What is env zero?

> env zero is the cloud infrastructure governance platform for IaC automation, self-service, and compliance

env zero is the cloud infrastructure governance platform built for the AI era.

As infrastructure scales across teams, tools, and clouds, complexity and risk accelerate—especially in the AI era, where change is constant and velocity is critical. Delivery pipelines that once worked for a few engineers no longer hold up across a growing organization.

env zero changes that. It gives platform teams a control plane to define how infrastructure should be delivered, and ensures those expectations are met everywhere.

The result is infrastructure that moves quickly, adheres to standards, and scales without chaos.

## What env zero makes possible

### **Developer self-service with guardrails**

Engineers use their existing workflows to ship infrastructure without waiting on manual reviews. Platform teams define the standards—guardrails, scopes, and approvals are built in by design.

### **Codify and govern existing infrastructure**

Unmanaged resources are brought under control without rebuilding from scratch. Infrastructure is versioned, governed, and aligned to your delivery model.

### **Consistent delivery across teams**

Infrastructure is shipped using shared standards across every team, tool, and environment. Manual steps and ad-hoc scripts are replaced with repeatable workflows.

### **Govern every stage of the lifecycle**

Standards are applied throughout the entire deployment flow—from planning to deployment to ongoing management. Every change is reviewed, tracked, and aligned with how infrastructure is meant to operate.

### **Cost controls built into delivery**

Cost awareness and accountability are integrated into infrastructure delivery from the start. Teams operate with clear boundaries, and usage is automatically mapped to owners.

### **Operate at enterprise scale**

Growth doesn't compromise control. Teams, environments, and projects remain organized, isolated, and governed—even at enterprise scale.

## Infrastructure control without disruption

env zero serves as a control plane for infrastructure delivery across your existing ecosystem. It works across frameworks, clouds, and workflows—providing structure and visibility without requiring teams to change how they work.
