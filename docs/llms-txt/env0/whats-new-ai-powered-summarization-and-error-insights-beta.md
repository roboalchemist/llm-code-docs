# 🤖 What’s New: AI-Powered Summarization and Error Insights (Beta)

> We’re bringing the power of AI to your deployments with our new AI-Powered Summarization and Error Insights - now in beta!

We’re bringing the power of AI to your deployments with our new AI-Powered Summarization and Error Insights - now in beta!

## 🧠 Understand Errors Faster

Debugging just got easier. When a deployment error occurs, click the new "Ask AI" button next to the error message to get a clear explanation of what went wrong, why, and how you might fix it.

## 📋 Smarter Summaries for Plan & Apply

Reviewing plans may be cumbersome and confusing - you can now make sense of the changes with a quick click of the "Summarize" button. AI-generated summaries highlight key modifications, flag sensitive updates, and offer insights - so you don’t have to dig through logs.
