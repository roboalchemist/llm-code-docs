# 📧 Email Notifications

> We're excited to announce a revamp of email notifications on the env0 platform!  Now we are allowing users to define custom email recipients and configure different events to go out to each email, such as failed deployments or maxed out budgets. 🚧 Current legacy email notifications will be disabled in 2 weeks time.

We're excited to announce a revamp of email notifications on the env0 platform!

Until now, users would receive email notifications which were not configurable.\
Now we are allowing users to define custom email recipients and configure different events to go out to each email, such as failed deployments or maxed out budgets.\
These can be configured separately for different projects so you can stay on top of critical updates sent directly to your inbox!

> 🚧 Legacy Email Notifications
>
> Current legacy email notifications will be disabled in 2 weeks time.

## Configure your project to send notifications

Configure the events you would like to be notified about, as described in our [docs](/guides/integrations/notifications#configure-your-project-to-send-notifications):

1. Navigate to your Project Settings, and open the Notifications tab.
2. Find the Notification Target you've previously added and click on the edit icon (✏️) next to it.
3. Select the events you'd like to get notified of, from this project to this notification target.
4. Click Save.
