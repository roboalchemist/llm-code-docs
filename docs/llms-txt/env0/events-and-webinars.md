# Events and webinars

Connect with the env zero team at industry events, webinars, and conferences to learn about Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) best practices, platform engineering, and how teams are scaling their infrastructure operations.

## Upcoming events

We regularly participate in conferences, meetups, and virtual events where you can:

* Learn about the latest IaC trends and best practices
* Discover how teams are implementing platform engineering
* Get hands-on experience with env zero features
* Connect with other infrastructure professionals
* Meet our team and ask questions

## Terra-to-Tofu Tour

Join us on our Terra-to-Tofu Tour as we visit cities across North America. These events focus on the transition from Terraform to OpenTofu and how teams can maintain their Infrastructure-as-Code workflows while embracing open-source alternatives.
