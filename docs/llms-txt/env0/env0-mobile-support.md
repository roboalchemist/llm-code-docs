# 📱 Mobile support

> Now you have a full view of your cloud environment on the go - managing your environments just got simpler! It's now possible to access the env zero app using a mobile device 💪
We're happy to announce that the env zero app is now available on mobile browsers in any resolution. The mobile view is slimmed down, giving you visibility of the important things and insights into your cloud environments.
