# Purchase Options

env zero is sold exclusively through our sales team. To get started with any purchase option, [contact us](https://www.env0.com/contact-us) and we'll help you find the right package and get you set up.

## Direct Invoicing

The simplest way to get started with env zero is through direct invoicing. After agreeing on a package with our sales team, you'll be billed directly by env zero via Stripe. This option is straightforward - no marketplace account required - and invoices are sent directly to your billing contact. It's a good fit for organizations that prefer to keep software spend separate from their cloud provider billing.

## Cloud Marketplace

Purchasing through a cloud marketplace lets you consolidate env zero spend with your existing cloud billing, which can be useful for organizations with committed cloud spend or procurement processes tied to a specific marketplace.

Marketplace purchases are handled through a **private offer** process. Once you've connected with our sales team and aligned on a package, we'll prepare a private offer through your preferred marketplace. You'll receive and accept the offer directly within your cloud console, and your env zero subscription will be activated and linked to your cloud billing from there.

**Supported marketplaces:**

* [AWS Marketplace](https://aws.amazon.com/marketplace/seller-profile?id=55540b66-733b-4d1f-b4fd-84a376407155)
* [Azure Marketplace](https://azuremarketplace.microsoft.com/en-us/marketplace/apps?search=env0\&page=1)
* [Google Cloud Marketplace](https://console.cloud.google.com/marketplace/product/env0-public/env0)
