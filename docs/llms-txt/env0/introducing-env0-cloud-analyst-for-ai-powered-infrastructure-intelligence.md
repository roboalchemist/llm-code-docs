# 📊Introducing env zero Cloud Analyst: AI-Powered Infrastructure Intelligence

> Meet Cloud Analyst: AI-Powered Infrastructure Intelligence in env zero Cloud Analyst gives you instant access to previously hard-to-reach infrastructure and operations data - uncovering insights into deployments, drift, IaC usage, and other key DevOps metrics. Ask questions in plain language, get structured answers in seconds, and build custom dashboards for continuous visibility.

We're excited to introduce **Cloud Analyst**, a new capability in env zero that gives you access to infrastructure and operations data that was previously hard to retrieve - unlocking insights into deployments, drift, IaC usage, and additional key DevOps metrics.

Built directly into env zero, Cloud Analyst lets you explore that data by asking plain-language questions, without needing to build reports or learn the data model. Just type a question and get a clear, structured answer.

What you can do with Cloud Analyst:

* **Ask questions** in natural language and get structured answers in seconds
* **Track trends** across projects, environments, deployments, and more
* **Create, save, and share customized dashboards** that update with your latest data

To enable Cloud Analyst, contact your env zero Customer Success team.\
