# Launching Your First Environment

> Deploy your first Terraform environment using an env zero template

After you’ve connected your VCS and your cloud account, the only thing left to do is deploy your Terraform code.

1. Go to your **Default Organization Project** and enter the **Project Templates** page
2. Find the template you’ve added, and click **Run Now**
3. You can review the settings this environment will be created with. If it requires any additional variables, you can add them here.
4. Click **Run**
