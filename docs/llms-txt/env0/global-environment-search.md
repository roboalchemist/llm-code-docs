## Searching the environment

Finding environments is now a breeze by accessing our search bar from the 🔍 icon in the top right or by using our [CMD+K](https://docs.env0.com/changelog/easy-search-and-navigation-with-cmdctrl-k) shortcut.

Now starting to type in the search bar will search within your environments enabling you to easily and quickly access the environments you are searching for ✨
