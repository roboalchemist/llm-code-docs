# General Availability of rerank-lite-1

Voyage reranker endpoint is now generally available.  See [here](https://docs.voyageai.com/docs/reranker)  for details.

Our first  general-purpose reranker, `rerank-lite-1`,  consistently outperforms `bge-rerank-large` and Cohere’s `rerank-english-v2.0` and can be seamlessly integrated with other components of RAG and search stack.
