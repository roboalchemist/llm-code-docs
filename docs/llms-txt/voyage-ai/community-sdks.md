# Community SDKs

Here are various SDKs contributed by community members:

* <a href="https://voyageai.ksylvest.com/" target="_blank">Ruby</a> by <a href="https://ksylvest.com/" target="_blank">Kevin Sylvestre</a>
* <a href="https://www.npmjs.com/package/voyage-ai-provider" target="_blank">Vercel</a> by <a href="https://patelvivek.dev" target="_blank">Vivek Patel</a>
* <a href="https://pkg.go.dev/github.com/austinfhunter/voyageai" target="_blank">Go</a> by <a href="https://austinhunter.dev" target="_blank">Austin Hunter</a>
* <a href="https://github.com/lunary-ai/abso" target="_blank">Typescript</a> by <a href="https://lunary.ai/" target="_blank">Lunary</a>

A big thank you to all of the authors above for their contributions. We’ve made our [OpenAPI spec publicly available](https://github.com/voyage-ai/openapi) for anyone who wants to build their own SDKs, libraries, or clients.

If you would like to get your SDK listed here, please reach out to [contact@voyageai.com](mailto:contact@voyageai.com).
