# 3. Index your Codebase

After you've [set up your database](https://docs.qodo.ai/qodo-documentation/qodo-aware/getting-started/enterprise-self-hosted/1.-set-up-database), the second step to set up Qodo Context Engine is to **index your codebase**.

This allows Qodo Context Engine to analyze your repositories, build internal context, and start answering questions intelligently.
