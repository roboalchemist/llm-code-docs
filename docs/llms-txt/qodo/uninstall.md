# Uninstall

### Uninstall Qodo in VSCode

1. Click on the Qodo button in the bottom status bar.
2. Hover over the authentication notification, select the settings icon and choose the `Manage Extension` option.
3. Qodo Extension will open in a new tab. Select Uninstall and reload VSCode.

### Uninstall Qodo in JetBrains IDEs

1. Click on the Settings button on the top right corner of your IDE.
2. From the dropdown menu, choose plugins.
3. Find Qodo from the list of installed plugins.
4. Click on the small arrow next to the Disable button.
5. Choose uninstall.
