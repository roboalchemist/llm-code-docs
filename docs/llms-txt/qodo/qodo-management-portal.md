# Qodo Management Portal

The Qodo management portal gives you the tools and visibility you need to:

* Manage users, teams, and organization settings
* Configure access and collaboration across your organization
* View insights and metrics related to your team’s software development

### Access the management portal

Sign in to the management portal at [**app.qodo.ai**](https://app.qodo.ai) using your Qodo account.

You can also access the portal from [**https://www.qodo.ai**](https://www.qodo.ai/) by selecting **LOG IN** from the top navigation bar.
