# Troubleshooting

If you encounter problems with Qodo IDE Plugin, start by checking the troubleshooting guides below:

* [**Connectivity Issues**](https://docs.qodo.ai/qodo-documentation/qodo-gen/troubleshooting/connectivity-issues)\
  Fix problems related to connecting Qodo IDE Plugin to our servers or your environment, such as failed connections, proxy/firewall restrictions, or timeouts.
* [**General Issues**](https://docs.qodo.ai/qodo-documentation/qodo-gen/troubleshooting/general-issues)\
  Resolve issues with chat responses, login, or unusual behaviors in the IDE. Learn how to copy a Request ID or extract logs to share with support.

For additional help, please [contact support.](mailto:support@qodo.ai)
