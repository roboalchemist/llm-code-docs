# Sign-up and Onboarding

Follow the steps to sign up for Qodo and complete your onboarding.   &#x20;

### Step 1: Access the sign-up page

Open your browser and go to [**https://app.qodo.ai/signin**](https://app.qodo.ai/signin).

### Step 2: Select your sign-up method

On the sign-up page, choose one of the following options to create your account and sign in to Qodo:

#### **Sign up with Google**

1. Click **Continue with Google**.
2. Select your Google account or enter your Google credentials.
3. You’ll be redirected back to Qodo with your Google account linked.

#### **Sign up with GitHub**

1. Click **Continue with GitHub**.
2. Enter your GitHub username and password, then click **Sign in**.
3. If prompted, click **Authorize**.
4. You’ll be redirected back to Qodo with your GitHub account linked.

#### **Sign up with email**

1. Click **Continue with email**.
2. Enter a valid email address.
3. Check your inbox for a verification link.
4. Click the link to activate your account.

### Join Qodo via invitation

If you were invited to join a team:

1. Open the invitation email from Qodo with the subject **“Join your team – enjoy Qodo”**.
2. Click **Accept the invite**.
3. You’ll be redirected to the Qodo sign-in page.
4. Enter the same email address that received the invitation.
5. Click **Continue with email**, then **Log in** to access your Qodo account.

### Onboarding

After signing in for the first time, you are guided through a **setup wizard** in the Qodo portal.\
This wizard helps connect Qodo to your development tools so you can get the most value from the platform.

Completing this setup is **recommended**, but **not required**. You can skip it and start using Qodo immediately.

**1.** [**Link Git user accounts**](https://docs.qodo.ai/qodo-documentation/management-portal/account-preferences#connect-a-git-provider-account)**.**\
Link your Git provider account to connect your Git identity to Qodo and identify you in pull requests and commits.

**2.**[ **Install the Qodo app**](https://app.gitbook.com/s/yXEFCLH7CkXrROa2gOqv/get-started/install)**.**\
Install the Qodo app on your repositories to allow Qodo to analyze code and provide suggestions.

**3. Connect task management systems.**\
Connect task management tools to link code changes to tasks and issues.

Once onboarding is completed, you can explore the Qodo portal, or start using Qodo directly in your pull requests.

#### Completing setup later

You can close the setup wizard at any time and finish the process later.

To exit the wizard, click **Close**.

When you’re ready to continue, click **Finish onboarding** in the top-right corner of the Qodo portal.
