# Administrators Actions

Admin actions can be performed in the **Organization** section.

To get to the Organization section, find the **Platform** header on the left, then choose **Organization**.

### Create a New Team <a href="#creating-a-new-team" id="creating-a-new-team"></a>

1. Open the **Organization** section.
2. Click the **Manage Organization** button on the right, then from the dropdown menu choose **Create a Team**.
3. Enter a team name in the provided field.
4. Click **Submit** to create your team.
5. The new team will be displayed in the dashboard.

### Manage Your Team <a href="#managing-your-team" id="managing-your-team"></a>

Click **Manage Team** in the upper right corner of each team to manage your team.

### **Close Team**

To disband the team, select **Close Team** from the dropdown menu.

**Note**: Removing your team will remove current team members and terminate the existing subscription.

To remove a member from the team, click on the delete icon (trash can) located to the right of the member’s name.
