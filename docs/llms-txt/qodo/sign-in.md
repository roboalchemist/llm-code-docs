# Sign In

To start using Qodo **you must** [**Sign in**](https://app.qodo.ai/signin)**.**

### Visual Studio Code

1. Open Qodo by clicking the Qodo logo in your extensions bar.
2. Click the `sign in to Qodo` button on the top right.

<figure><img src="https://782320861-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FhllduvO2nHKZ2vKzfcEn%2Fuploads%2FjhE1oYeUFXBATFGiUU6C%2FScreenshot%202024-11-25%20at%2018.41.29.png?alt=media&#x26;token=388acf7b-159d-41b2-a96b-902e400cc621" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Sign in to Qodo Gen button</p></figcaption></figure>

3. Follow the instructions on the webpage.

Alternatively, click Qodo button on the bottom right. A popup message with instructions will open.

### JetBrains

1. Open Qodo by clicking the Qodo logo in your tool window bar.
2. Follow the instructions to sign in.

### Choose your sign in method

Choose one log in method and stick to it.

**Qodo won't let you log in using a different method in the future.**

### Log In to Teams Plan <a href="#login-to-teams-plan" id="login-to-teams-plan"></a>

Once you receive an invitation email from your team administrator, log in to Qodo **using the email address to which the invitation was sent**. Select one of the available login methods complete the process.

### Log In to Enterprise Plan <a href="#login-to-enterprise-plan" id="login-to-enterprise-plan"></a>

Sign in using your work email to automatically connect to your organization’s workspace. Select one of the available login methods to finish signing in.

If your organization has configured Single Sign-On (SSO), you can use it for a smoother login process.
