# How can I update my billing information?

If you want to change your account's credit card details, go to the left side panel, click **Account settings** → **Billing** → scroll to **Payment information**. Enter the new credit card details on the respective fields then click on **Update payment information**. Only the *account owner* can make this change.

To change your billing name or location address, send our Support team a message at <support@getdbt.com> with the newly updated information, and we can make that change for you!
