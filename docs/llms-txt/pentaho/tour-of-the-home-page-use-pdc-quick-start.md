# Tour of the Home page

The Home page provides a central location for accessing the business tools available to you based on your permissions, such as data canvas, business glossary, applications, policies, management, workers, and so on. You can also access your user profile from the top menu bar and use the menu bar on the left to navigate to different features in the product.

## Quick access cards

When you navigate to the Home page, you can see quick access cards for various Data Catalog features, depending on your permissions. Based on the options selected in the Landing page options window (when you click **Edit**), it can display the following widgets.

<table><thead><tr><th width="200">Card</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Explore your data</td><td>Click <strong>Open Data Canvas</strong>, and it takes you to the Data Canvas page, where you can explore your data. For more information, see.</td></tr><tr><td>Organize your data</td><td>Click <strong>Open Business Glossary</strong>, and it takes you to the Business Glossary page, where you can create, organize, and curate business terms to help you navigate your data. For more information, see.</td></tr><tr><td>Manage your data</td><td>Click <strong>Open Management</strong>, and it takes you to the Manage Your Environment page, where you can manage your data sources, users, user roles, workers, business rules, schedules, dictionaries, and more.</td></tr></tbody></table>
