# Viewing Golden Records

View the Golden Records for your organization to determine whether the records are up to date or require any modification.

Perform the following steps to view Golden Records:

1. On the left navigation menu, click **Master Data**. The **Master Data** page opens.
2. Click the **Golden Records** tab. The Golden Records are shown in a table.

   The **Golden Record Table Key** column of Golden Records table contains the unique identifier key of each Golden Record. This Golden Record Table Key is generated by the Pentaho Data Mastering application when the Golden Record is created, and the key remains unchanged until the record is removed from the application.
