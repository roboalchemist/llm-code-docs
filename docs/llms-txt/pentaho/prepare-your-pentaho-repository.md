# Prepare your Pentaho Repository

Once you have finished preparing your environment, you can initialize and configure your Pentaho Repository.

Select the database that you are using as your Pentaho Repository:

* [PostgreSQL](https://docs.pentaho.com/install/10.2-install/pentaho-installation-overview-cp/archive-installation/archive-installation-process/use-postgresql-as-your-repository-database-archive-installation)
* [MySQL](https://docs.pentaho.com/install/10.2-install/pentaho-installation-overview-cp/archive-installation/archive-installation-process/use-mysql-or-mariadb-as-your-repository-database-archive-installation)
* [Oracle](https://docs.pentaho.com/install/10.2-install/pentaho-installation-overview-cp/archive-installation/archive-installation-process/use-oracle-as-your-repository-database-archive-installation)
* [MS SQL Server](https://docs.pentaho.com/install/10.2-install/pentaho-installation-overview-cp/archive-installation/archive-installation-process/use-ms-sql-server-as-your-repository-database-archive-installation)
