# Keep or exclude selected data in your visualization

In **Stream** or **Model View**, you can select data points to keep only the selected data, or to exclude that data from your visualization.

* To keep or exclude a single data point, click the data point in your visualization. A filters pop-up menu displays.

  ![Filters Pop-up Menu](https://3411831820-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FAYwCj9fPr1B2pjC11IOQ%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-74d4851d33b09b863cf00f9d715ead724571a462%2FFilter_popup_menu.png?alt=media)

  * Click **Exclude** or **Keep Only**. The visualization updates your view and the **Filters** panel displays the filtered data point.

    ![Excluded Filter Applied Example](https://3411831820-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FAYwCj9fPr1B2pjC11IOQ%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-3a8d84f132a6b5d87028d0f290fd5638bc941f47%2FFilter%20updates%20data.png?alt=media)
* To keep only or exclude multiple data points, press the CTRL key while clicking additional data points, axis labels, or the legend in your visualization. Release the CTRL key to display the filters pop-up menu.

  ![Keep Only Filter Selection Example](https://3411831820-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FAYwCj9fPr1B2pjC11IOQ%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-810a4577e8f95afd200dc18ce3c17f43c36444f2%2FFilter_popup_exclude.png?alt=media)

  * Click **Exclude** or **Keep Only**. The visualization updates your view and the **Filters** panel displays the filtered data points.

    ![Keep Only Filter Applied Example](https://3411831820-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FAYwCj9fPr1B2pjC11IOQ%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-4438504b67c5688af22838024afbace0de6d07d5%2FFiltered_data_displays.png?alt=media)

**Note:** **Exclude** and **Keep Only** are not available for the **Table** or the **Pivot Table**.
