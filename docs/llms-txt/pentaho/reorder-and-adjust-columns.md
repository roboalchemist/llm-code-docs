# Reorder and adjust columns

To change the order of columns in your report, choose the column you want to move, then drag it to the desired location. Alternatively, choose the column you want to move, click the down-arrow, and choose **Move** (**Left** or **Right**) from the menu.

![Move Interactive Reports columns](https://591371677-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FFgwF7oC7Y6b7XWdahykf%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-1f2304e485966dd5cc8eaeed04a001f7f01131af%2FCustomize_PIR_Move_Columns.png?alt=media)

To adjust a column's width, choose the column, then click the vertical line that divides the column headers. Move the line left or right to adjust the column width.

![Change Interactive Reports column widths](https://591371677-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FFgwF7oC7Y6b7XWdahykf%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-9f9b6a4f9bbb0a6c06293cc08d279679f110d731%2FCustomize_PIR_Change_Column_widths.png?alt=media)
