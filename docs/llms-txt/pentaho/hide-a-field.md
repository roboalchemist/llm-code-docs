# Hide a field

This task assumes you are in an Analyzer report.

1. In the **Available fields** list, hover your mouse over the field you want to hide and click the Down Arrow.
2. From the context menu which appears, click **Set Hidden** to hide the field.

   This field is no longer visible in the list.

   ![Hide a field](https://591371677-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FFgwF7oC7Y6b7XWdahykf%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-4464c7cfcc33d878689d79445477b10d9e8b1373%2FAnalyzer_SetHiddenMenu.png?alt=media)

   **Note:** When you hide a field which is currently used in one or more of your reports, the field is automatically removed from all of your reports, including [drill-through links](https://github.com/pentaho/documentation/blob/main/PDIA/10.2/Analytics/Pentaho%20Analyzer/Pentaho%20Analyzer%20cp/Set%20Analyzer%20report%20options/Turn%20on%20drill-through%20links=GUID-29ACF615-2298-4EA9-80A9-473DE845F6B8=3=en=.md). You can click the **Undo** button to restore the field to the report prior to saving your changes. Once saved, you will have to [unhide that field](https://docs.pentaho.com/pba/10.2-analytics/pentaho-analyzer-cp/working-with-analyzer-fields/hide-and-unhide-fields/unhide-a-field) to restore it to the **Available fields** list.
