# Copy and paste formatting

Once you have one column formatted the way that you want, you can use the **Copy formatting** and **Paste formatting** options on the **Formatting** tab to copy the formatting to other columns in your report.

![Copy and Paste Formatting](https://591371677-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FFgwF7oC7Y6b7XWdahykf%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-92a44f16638a5efac4f6decac49e29077b6fdb71%2FCustomize_PIR_Copy_and_Paste_Formatting.png?alt=media)

If you want to remove the formatting that you applied to a column, click to select that column, then **Remove Formatting**.
