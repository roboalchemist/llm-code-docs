# Apply your plugins

If you use plugins with your Pentaho products, after upgrading to version 10.2, you must re-apply your plugins. For instructions with CTools plugins, see the **Pentaho CTools** document. For instructions with PDI plugins, see [Install PDI plugins](https://docs.pentaho.com/install/10.2-install/pentaho-installation-overview-cp/installation-of-the-pentaho-design-tools/install-the-pdi-tools-and-plugins/install-pdi-plugins).
