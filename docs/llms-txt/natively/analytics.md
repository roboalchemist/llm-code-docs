# Analytics

With the Analytics feature in Natively, you can track app installs and optimize your marketing budget.

Natively supports several analytics providers:

* [Facebook](https://docs.buildnatively.com/natively-platform/features/analytics/facebook)
* [AppsFlyer](https://docs.buildnatively.com/natively-platform/features/analytics/appsflyer)
