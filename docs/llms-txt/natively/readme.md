# What is Natively?

Natively is a no-code development tool that helps you create mobile applications in a few minutes.

Natively wraps(converts) your website/web app to the native mobile app by adding it to the WebView (an embeddable browser that a native application can use to display web content while a web app provides additional functionality and interactivity).

**Accessing Native Mobile Features**\
One of the standout advantages of using Natively is the ease with which you can access a wide range of mobile native features. With our comprehensive Software Development Kit (SDK) and an array of plugins, you can integrate functionalities that enhance user experience and interaction. For instance, you can incorporate push notifications to keep your users informed about updates or promotions, use a QR or barcode scanner for convenient transactions or access geolocation services to provide location-based features. Furthermore, you can utilize the camera functionality for features such as photo uploads or augmented reality experiences.

These features are not just add-ons; they are essential tools that can significantly elevate the performance of your mobile application. By tapping into the capabilities of native mobile features, you can create a more engaging and interactive experience for your users, which is critical in a competitive app market.

**Continuous Updates Without Hassle**\
Another compelling aspect of Natively is the flexibility it offers in terms of updates. Traditionally, updating a mobile application can be a cumbersome process that often requires resubmitting the app to app stores like the App Store or Google Play. This can lead to delays and a frustrating experience for both developers and users. However, with Natively, you can continue to update your website without the need to go through the lengthy resubmission process. This means that as you make changes or improvements to your website, those updates are reflected in your mobile app automatically.

This seamless integration not only saves time but also ensures that your app remains current and relevant, thereby enhancing user satisfaction and engagement.
