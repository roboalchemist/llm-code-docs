# Subscription Plans

## Natively plans:

* **Preview** - is an excellent option to get started with Natively. You can walk around, test some features, and see if Natively fits your needs.
* **Business** - is recommended for startups or businesses to get a full package and white label capabilities.
* **Lifetime** - One-time payment, you will receive a license for using Natively on Android & iOS (with 10 re-builds included)&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
A **Special Offer** is a limited-price offer that applies for a lifetime (Until you cancel or switch a plan).
{% endhint %}
