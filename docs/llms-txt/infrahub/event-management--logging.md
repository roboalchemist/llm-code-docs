## [📄️<!-- --> <!-- -->Activity log](/topics/activity-log.md)

[Changes (events) in Infrahub are documented in the Activity log. It helps you see which objects were impacted, when a change was made, and who made it. It can be used to troubleshoot unforeseen changes, audit previous operations, and comprehend the order of updates across various branches.](/topics/activity-log.md)
