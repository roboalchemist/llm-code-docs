## Long-term contract discounts

We understand compute can be expensive, which is why we offer hefty discounts for long-term contracts. You can choose long-term contracts for GPU Instances and Instant Clusters via the console or [contact our sales team for customized GPU clusters](https://docs.verda.com/contact-support#cluster-sales).

Long-term contracts are paid up-front.

***

## Making Payments

### Bare-metal Clusters

Each Bare-metal Cluster contract is unique, so you will receive an invoice based on discussions with our sales and technical teams. Feel free to discuss your payment needs with our sales team by contacting [**support@verda.com**](mailto:support@datacrunch.io).

### Cloud Console

On the cloud console, you can deploy On-Demand instances and clusters with different payment types:

**Long-term**: Pay up-front for a specified contract length

**Pay As You Go**: Pay per 10 minutes of usage, including storage

Make payments by visiting the **Billing & Top-up** tab in the navigation sidebar, or via the **Deploy a new instance** page.

For **Pay As You Go** instances, your account balance is deducted for every 10-minute period of usage.

Because our services are prepaid, you cannot use more than what’s in your account. If your balance is running low, you will receive console and email notifications (check your Low Balance notification settings in **Account Settings**).

To avoid a zero balance, you can set up an [auto top-up](#auto-top-up). This makes a payment to your account once the balance is below a chosen limit.

{% hint style="danger" %}
If your balance reaches zero, your instances will be discontinued and your volumes (data) will be deleted.

*Your volumes can be restored up to 96 hours after deletion.*
{% endhint %}

#### One time top-up

This adds a specific dollar amount to your balance one time. It is a good option if you are unsure how much compute you will be using or just want to do a few tests.

#### Automatic top-up

Auto top-up will automatically charge your chosen payment card when your balance goes below a specified amount. This is a good option if you are using compute consistently and don't want to worry about resources being deleted accidentally.

Auto top-up settings are found in the **Billing & settings** page of each project.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://2529223994-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FYO2RW7i8v8Hs8UzxSAdO%2Fuploads%2Fgit-blob-cd0c7f383532b2987adde7a6fa65a16e5f9d4a0a%2FScreenshot%202025-11-18%20at%2015.16.20.png?alt=media" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

When Auto top-up is properly enabled, a green icon will appear next to your project balance:

{% hint style="success" %}
We recommend setting your top-up trigger to at least 12 hours of your estimated hourly rate so you have time to react if there is a payment issue.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="danger" %}
Check your payment card for expiration date, and sign up for low balance notifications in case your payment card fails for any reason.
{% endhint %}

### Top-up by bank transfer

You can easily top-up your Verda Cloud balance through a bank transfer.

To proceed, please email <sales@verda.com> and indicate you wish to top-up via a bank invoice. Include the following details in your mail:

* **Full Name**
* **Company** (if applicable)
* **Street Address** (including postal code, city, state/province and country)
* **Company Registration Number / Tax Number** (if applicable)
* **Invoicing Contact Information** (name and email)
* **Top-Up Amount** (in EUR or USD)
* **Verda User Account** to receive the top-up

Once we receive your request, we will send you an invoice. After the payment is successfully processed, your cloud account balance will be updated.

The minimum amount that can be topped up via bank transfer is 1,000 EUR or USD.

## Low balance

The low balance icon is visible when your balance has **less than 48 hours of running time** for all your resources combined.
