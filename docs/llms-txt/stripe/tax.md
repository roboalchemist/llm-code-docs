# Stripe Tax

Automate sales tax, VAT, and GST compliance on all your transactions—low or no code integrations available.

Stripe Tax is a global all-in-one tax compliance solution that handles tax obligation monitoring, registrations, calculations, collections, and filings.

> You might be eligible for a 1099 tax form through a platform that pays you with Stripe. Learn more about how to [receive your forms](https://support.stripe.com/express/topics/1099-tax-forms).

## Understand where to register

Stripe Tax proactively monitors your transactions, providing clarity on where you may have tax obligations. You can see how your Stripe transactions compare to filing thresholds, helping you understand where to register, and you can switch on tax collection in relevant locations.

Learn more about [monitoring your tax obligations](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/monitoring.md).

## Manage your registrations

Stripe Tax helps manage your global tax registrations, saving you time with pre-filled application details and reducing errors.

Learn more about [managing tax registrations](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/registering.md).

## Automatically calculate and collect tax

Stripe Tax consistently calculates and collects the correct amount of tax, regardless of what or where you sell. Stripe Tax supports [over 100 countries](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/supported-countries.md) and more than [600 product types](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/tax-codes.md).

Learn more about  [tax calculation](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/calculating.md).

## File and remit with ease

Stripe Tax integrates with partners to automate the filing process, so your global filings are accurate and timely. It also provides reports that meet the formatting requirements of filing locations, so you can file taxes on your own, with your accountant, or with a preferred partner.

Learn more about [tax filing](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/filing.md).

## Enable Stripe Tax

Start collecting taxes globally in minutes by adding a single line of code to your existing integration for a low-code setup, clicking a button in the Dashboard for a no-code setup, or creating a custom integration with our powerful API.

You can integrate Stripe Tax with [Payment Links](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/payment-links.md) or [Checkout Sessions API](https://docs.stripe.com/payments/checkout/taxes.md) and use it for [subscriptions](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/subscriptions.md) and [invoices](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/invoicing.md). You can also create [custom payment flows](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/custom.md) or integrate Stripe Tax as a platform or marketplace with [Connect](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/connect.md).

Learn more how to enable [Stripe Tax](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/set-up.md).

## Features

- [End-to-end compliance solution](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/how-tax-works.md) Helps you manage tax calculations, registrations and filings directly or through integrated partners.

- [Threshold monitoring](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/monitoring.md) Track your sales against local tax registration thresholds and receive alerts when approaching limits, helping you stay compliant as your business grows.

- [Automatic tax calculation](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/how-tax-works.md#calculate-and-collect) Instantly calculate sales tax, VAT, and GST for transactions worldwide, determining tax rates based on customer and business locations.​

- [Product tax codes](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/tax-codes.md) Categorize your products and services for correct tax treatment, ensuring accurate tax calculations across different jurisdictions.

- [Multi-country support](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/supported-countries.md) Handle tax calculations for international sales and cross-border transactions, supporting compliance in multiple countries.

- [Platform and marketplace integration](https://docs.stripe.com/tax/connect.md) Integrate with platforms and marketplaces using Stripe Connect, enabling tax calculation and collection on behalf of your connected accounts.

- [Flexible pricing options](https://stripe.com/tax/pricing) Choose between pay-as-you-go or subscription-based pricing models to suit your business needs, with transparent fees based on usage and integration type.
