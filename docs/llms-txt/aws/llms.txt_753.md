## [Onboarding steps](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/secretsmanager/latest/mes-onboarding/mes-onboarding-steps.html)

- [Step 1: Discuss your integration with Secrets Manager](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/secretsmanager/latest/mes-onboarding/mes-step1.html): Duration: approximately one week
- [Step 2: Provide integration specifications](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/secretsmanager/latest/mes-onboarding/mes-step2.html): Duration: Typically one to two weeks.
- [Step 3: Launch your integration](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/secretsmanager/latest/mes-onboarding/mes-step3.html): Secrets Manager conducts joint testing with you prior to the launch.
