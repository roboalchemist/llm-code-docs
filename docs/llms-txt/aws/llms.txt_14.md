## [Manage Workers](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/ManagingWorkers.html)

- [View Worker statistics](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/ViewingWorkerDetails.html): Walks through how Requesters can view a Worker's statistics to see what the Worker is good at.
- [Block a Worker](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/BlockingaWorker.html): Walks through how Requesters can block Workers who aren't performing to your standards.
- [Unblock a Worker](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/UnblockingaWorker.html): Walks through how Requesters can unblock a Worker.
- [Award a bonus](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/GivingaBonus.html): Walks through how a Requester can award bonuses to Workers to encourage them to keep working for you.
- [Assign a qualification to a Worker](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/AssigningaQualificationTypetoaWorker.html): Walks through how Requesters can assign a qualification type to a Worker.
- [Revoke a Worker's qualification](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/RevokingaWorkersQualification.html): Walks through how Requesters can revoke a Worker's qualification if who aren't performing to your standards.
- [Managing Worker details offline](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/RequesterUI/ManagingWorkerDetailsOffline.html): Walks through how Requesters can download and modify Worker details offline.
