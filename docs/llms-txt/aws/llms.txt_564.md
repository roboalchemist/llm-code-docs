- [Usage pricing](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/marketplace/latest/userguide/usage-pricing.html): A usage pricing model, also known as pay as you go pricing, enables you to offer products to customers who only pay for what they use.
- [Contract pricing](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/marketplace/latest/userguide/contract-pricing.html): Using the contract pricing model, you can offer upfront pricing to customers that enables them to buy a license for 1 month, 12 months, 24 months, or 36 months.
