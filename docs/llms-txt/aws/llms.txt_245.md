### [Things to know before porting](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/things-to-know-before-porting.html)

The topics in this section explain which numbers can be ported, how long it takes, and what fees you might incur.

- [What is phone number porting?](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/what-is-phone-number-porting.html): Porting a phone number is the process of moving a phone number from one telephony service provider, or carrier, to another.
- [How much does it cost?](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/fees-for-number-porting.html): Amazon Connect does not charge fees for porting numbers.
- [Can my number be ported?](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/what-numbers-can-be-ported.html): Port phone numbers to Amazon Connect that meet specific criteria.
- [How long does it take to port numbers?](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/how-long-for-number-porting.html): Provides timelines for porting phone numbers to Amazon Connect.
- [Can I cancel a scheduled porting?](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/cancel-port-request.html): Depending on the country of service, after a mutually agreed date and time has been provided it can be difficult to cancel.
- [When do I cancel my current telecom service?](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/cancel-current-service.html): Do not cancel your existing telecom service until your phone numbers have been ported and confirmed working in Amazon Connect.
- [South Korean porting regulations for your Amazon Connect instance](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/porting-numbers-sk.html): Rules for South Korea differ from those in other countries.
- [Things to know about Thailand number porting](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/porting-numbers-th.html): Learn about the things to know when porting numbers for Thailand.

### [Porting your phone numbers](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/porting-your-phone-numbers.html)

Porting phone numbers from your existing carrier to Amazon Connect is a multi-step process.

- [How to port your numbers to Amazon Connect](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/about-porting.html): Begin this process several months before your go-live date, depending on the country.
- [Documentation requirements for porting numbers to Amazon Connect](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/porting-documentation-requirements.html): A Letter of Authorization (LOA) and country-specific requirements, such as local business address.
- [How to verify flows before numbers are ported](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/verify-flows-before-porting.html): Test your call flows before the mutually agreed date and time of porting with a claimed number.

- [What to do if the ported number isn't receiving calls](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/not-receiving-calls-after-porting.html): In Amazon Connect, update your support ticket to troubleshoot the ported number not receiving calls.
- [Release ported numbers you no longer need](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/release-ported-numbers-you-do-not-need.html): You can release ported numbers that you no longer need.
- [Revert to original carrier after porting](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/revert-porting-to-original-carrier.html): If you want to revert to the original carrier after porting, open a support case telling us who your new carrier is.
- [Port numbers away](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/port-away.html): Open an AWS Support case to let us know.
