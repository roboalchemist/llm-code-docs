## [Monitoring your Savings Plans](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/sp-monitoring.html)

### [Viewing your Savings Plans inventory](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-inventory.html)

Get a detailed overview of the Savings Plans that you own or have queued for future purchase in AWS Cost Management.

- [Renewing a Savings Plan](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/queue-sp-replace.html): Understand how to queue a Savings Plan to start as soon as the active Savings Plan expires.
- [Deleting a queued Savings Plan](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-queued-delete.html): Understand how to delete a queued Savings Plan.
- [Downloading your Savings Plans rates](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-download-pr.html): Understand how to download a CSV file showing your Savings Plans rates.
- [Downloading your Savings Plans inventory](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-download-inventory.html): Understand how to download a CSV file showing your Savings Plans inventory.

### [Using the Savings Plans utilization report](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-usingPR.html)

Use the utilization report in AWS Cost Management to understand the percentage of your Savings Plans commitment that you're using across your On-Demand usage.

- [Understanding utilization metrics and calculations](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-pr-metrics.html): Understand high-level metrics in the Utilization report and how they are calculated.
- [Downloading your Savings Plans utilization report](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-dl-pr.html): Understand how to download your Savings Plans utilization report.

### [Using the Savings Plans coverage report](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-usingCR.html)

Find out how much of your eligible spend was covered by your Savings Plans during a selected time period with the coverage report in AWS Cost Explorer.

- [Understanding coverage metrics and calculations](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-sp-cr-metrics.html): Understand high-level metrics in the Coverage report and how they are calculated.
- [Downloading your Savings Plans coverage report](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/ce-dl-cr.html): Understand how to download your Savings Plans coverage report.

### [Using budgets for Savings Plans](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/sp-usingBudgets.html)

Set budgets for your Savings Plan utilization, coverage, and costs, and track costs to optimize usage in AWS Cost Explorer.

- [Using a template to create a Savings Plans budget](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/sp-create-savingsplans-budget-template.html): Create a coverage budget for your Savings Plans using a template.
- [Customizing a Savings Plans budget](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/savingsplans/latest/userguide/sp-create-savingsplans-budget.html): Create a customized budget for Savings Plans utilization or Savings Plans coverage.
