## [Review Policies](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/AWSMturkAPI/ApiReference_ReviewPoliciesArticle.html)

- [How Review Policies Work](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/AWSMturkAPI/ApiReference_HowDoReviewPoliciesArticle.html): You specify the Review Policy(s) that you want Mechanical Turk to apply when you call the operation.
- [Assignment Review Policies](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/AWSMturkAPI/ApiReference_AssignmentReviewPolicies.html): Assignment-level Review Policies are applied as soon as a Worker submits an assignment.
- [HIT Review Policies](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AWSMechTurk/latest/AWSMturkAPI/ApiReference_HITReviewPolicies.html): A HIT-level Review Policy is applied when a Human Intelligence Task (HIT) becomes reviewable.
