# Amazon WorkMail User Guide

> Amazon WorkMail is a managed email and calendaring service with strong security controls and support for existing desktop and mobile email clients.

- [What is Amazon WorkMail?](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/what_is.html)
- [Troubleshooting](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/troubleshooting.html)
- [Document history](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/DocumentHistory.html)
- [AWS Glossary](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/glossary.html)

## [Setting up email clients](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/clients.html)

- [Password best practices for security](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/password.html): Learn how to secure your password for security purposes.
- [Signing in to the Amazon WorkMail web client](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/web-client.html): Sign in to the Amazon WorkMail web client to access your Amazon WorkMail account using a web browser.
- [Setting up Microsoft Outlook clients](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/outlook-client.html): Set up Microsoft Outlook email clients for use with Amazon WorkMail.
- [Setting up mobile device clients](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/mobile-client.html): Use Exchange ActiveSync to connect your Android or iOS mobile device client to Amazon WorkMail.
- [Setting up the Windows 10 Mail app](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/connect_win10_mail.html): Use Exchange ActiveSync to connect your Amazon WorkMail account to the Windows 10 Mail app client.
- [Setting up the Windows 11 Mail app](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/connect_win11_mail.html): Use Exchange ActiveSync to connect your Amazon WorkMail account to the Windows 11 Mail app client.
- [Setting up macOS apps](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/connect_mac_mail.html): Use Exchange ActiveSync to connect your Amazon WorkMail account to macOS client apps for mail, calendar, contacts, reminders, and notes.
- [Setting up IMAP](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/using_IMAP.html): Connect IMAP-compatible software to Amazon WorkMail.


## [Working with email](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/email_overview.html)

- [Working with email messages](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/email-messages.html): Send, copy, move, print, and delete email messages from Amazon WorkMail.
- [Working with email rules](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/email-rules.html): You use inbox rules to route emails from your mailbox in Amazon WorkMail.
- [Working with shared and full access inboxes](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/shared-inboxes.html): You can open inboxes that other users share with you or that your administrator grants you permission to access.
- [Sending encrypted or signed email](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/workmail/latest/userguide/send_encrypted_email.html): With S/MIME, you can send signed or encrypted emails inside and outside of your organization.
