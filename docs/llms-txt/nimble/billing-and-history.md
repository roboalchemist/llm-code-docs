# Billing and history

The billing and history page is your central resource for viewing and managing your payment details, balance, plan, and invoices.

<figure><img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/ZNy4aqrBN53tR8DpTEuW/blobs/nNyEwkWcCumwdf2kiOHj/Screen%20Shot%202023-06-22%20at%2013.39.11.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

At the top of the page is your available balance and active payment method.

Below is a table containing the full history of payments made to Nimble, including an option to view/download past invoices.

To the right is a summary of your existing plan, including the cost, next renewal date, and whether or not auto-recharge and rollover are available.

* [x] **Auto-recharge** - whether your payment method will be charged automatically on the next renewal date.
* [x] **Rollover** - does unused bandwidth from your plan rollover into the next period (available only for Advanced and Enterprise plans).

If you need to change your plan or payment method, please contact your account manager.
