# Onboarding Guide

## **Introduction**

Welcome to Nimble! We're excited to have you on board.&#x20;

We've created this onboarding guide to help you get started with our products quickly and easily. We'll walk you through logging in and using the User Dashboard, how to work with our modern proxy network, and sending your first real-time data request!

We also include some troubleshooting tips to help resolve common issues and helpful resources to help expand your knowledge and take your projects to the next level.

We’re excited to see what you’ll build with Nimble!
