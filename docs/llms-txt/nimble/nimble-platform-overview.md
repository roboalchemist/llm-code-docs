# Nimble Platform Overview

## Business ingenuity is restricted by technical complexity

Data and AI are widely understood to be the new frontier for business competition. Businesses that succeed in attaining the freshest, broadest, most accurate data will have the capacity to train more effective and accurate AI models.&#x20;

These models will in turn produce better business outcomes across marketing, product, inventory, brand management, and more.

Although this is widely accepted and understood, complicated engineering hurdles have dramatically impacted the ability of businesses to put data and AI into practice. Data acquisition has been a particularly sharp pain, being a complex, error-prone process that limits business potential.

Many businesses have turned to 3rd party vendors and static datasets for lack of a better solution. However, datasets come with wide ranging limitations, including:

* Low update frequency
* Lack of flexibility (limited data fields and pages)
* Limited available data sources
* Minimal or no integration with crucial B2B apps
* Overall lack of freedom to test and experiment data strategies

### **The Nimble Way - a Data Platform for Modern Businesses**

> At Nimble, we envisioned a new way to approach the problem. We built the fundamental infrastructure for data gathering from the ground up, integrating modern data stacks and AI to surpass the limitations encountered by others.

Having built the core infrastructure for collecting AI-ready data from the ground up, we immediately began building a Data Platform that could serve business needs. The Nimble Data Platform helps businesses go from zero public data processes to fully-leveraging data & AI across custom and B2B apps that drive direct business impact.

Some of the popular use cases supported by the Nimble Data Platform include:

* [x] Dynamic product pricing
* [x] Competitor marketing campaign tracking
* [x] Lead generation & enrichment
* [x] Unifying Hospitality, Retail, and Travel source Data
* [x] Social media sentiment analysis

and more.

#### The Nimble Data Platform is made up of two key products

* **Online Pipelines**
  * Extracts & processes AI-ready data from many popular sources in real-time.
  * Delivers data natively into cloud storage and B2B apps.
  * Empowers data teams and business analysts with fresh, diverse data for business impact.
* **Knowledge Cloud**
  * Provides answers to complex business questions by harnessing LLM technology.
  * Empowers anyone within the organization to leverage data with zero technical requirements.
  * Unifies data from across whole regions and industries into trained Cloud AI models.

## Ready for Enterprise Needs

### **Leverage data confidently with state-of-the-art compliance protocols**

Sleep well at night knowing every byte of collected data has gone through unparalleled compliance scrutiny. Nimble’s unique compliance-by-design technology blends expert human oversight with pioneering compliance monitoring technology.

Nimble is fully CCPA, GDPR, and SOC 2 certified. From the way we handle IP addresses to Terms and Regulations crawling, we take compliance incredibly seriously, and invite you to visit our [Trust Center](https://ai.nimbleway.com/trust) to learn more about our commitment to ethical data gathering.

### **Grow as needed with auto-scaling to fit your workload**

Some use cases build up over time - starting small and growing. Others require short bursts of data, with lulls in between. Others still require massive amounts of data 24/7. Regardless of your use case and scalability needs, Nimble’s Infrastructure automatically expands and contracts compute to meet your projects.

### **Observe & control granularly through Nimble Analytics & Management Hub**

Nimble provides comprehensive reporting and visibility tools that give businesses detailed insights into their data operations. The Nimble Analytics Hub allows users to generate custom reports, including month-over-month (MoM) and year-over-year (YoY) comparisons, with filters for pipelines, country of origin, target domains, and more. These reports can be easily exported to CSV or PDF, making it simple to share actionable insights with the whole team.

Additionally, Nimble offers real-time analytics through a user-friendly dashboard, enabling users to monitor data usage, request volumes, and success rates with highly granular filters. Businesses can track spending by pipeline, domain, or service, and set custom budget caps to manage costs effectively. Instant alerts at various thresholds help ensure that no unexpected overages occur, providing full control and transparency over web scraping activities.
