## **What’s New?**

We’re updating how you interact with and pay for the Nimble API. Instead of pricing based on industries endpoint (SERP, Web, eCommerce), we’re introducing **Driver-Based Pricing -** a smarter, more transparent way to align costs with the technology behind your requests.

## **Here’s What’s Changing:**

1. **Driver-Based Pricing:**
   * Pricing now reflects the [**Browserless Driver Technology**](https://www.nimbleway.com/nimble-api/browser) powering your requests:
     * **VX6:** Fast native requests.
     * **VX8:** Enhanced capabilities with rendering.
     * **VX10:** Advanced access for complex websites.
     * **VX12/12-Pro:** Social media platforms.
2. **Simplified Client Tiers:**
   * **SMB Customers:** Flat pricing for all drivers—simple and predictable.
   * **Enterprise Customers:** Dynamic pricing tailored to the complexity of your requests, offering cost efficiency for high volumes.
3. **Enhanced Visibility in the UI:**
   * **Service Rates View:** See driver-specific pricing and performance metrics instead of broad categories like Data API.
   * **Main Dashboard:** Filter graphs by driver to track request usage and costs.
   * **Custom Analytics Reports:** Generate reports segmented by driver, giving you deeper insights into your data collection patterns.
   * **Invoices:** Transparent breakdown by driver, showing request volumes and corresponding rates for each.

## **What’s In It for You?**

1. **Pay Only for What You Need  - Cost Efficiency :**&#x20;

   * Enterprise customers no longer rely on generic pricing.&#x20;
   * Costs align directly with the complexity (driver based) of your data collection tasks.

2. **Better Insights  - UI Enhancements:**&#x20;

   * Driver-level data lets you track, plan, and optimize your usage.
   * Easier tracking and reporting with driver-specific dashboards and reports.

3. **Transparent Billing/Invoicing - Budget Control & Visibility:**&#x20;
   * See exactly what you’re paying for and why with driver-level breakdowns..
   * Better budget planning, visibility and predictability.&#x20;
