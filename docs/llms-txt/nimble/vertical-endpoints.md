# Vertical Endpoints

## Overview&#x20;

Nimble offers a suite of specialized APIs designed to transform how you collect and interact with web data. Each API is tailored to address specific data collection needs, ensuring that you can efficiently gather and process the information most relevant to your goals. Whether you’re looking to scrape data from websites, monitor search engine results, gather social media insights, or extract product details from online marketplaces, Nimble’s vertical APIs have you covered.

These APIs are powered by advanced technology, including AI-driven optimization and specialized [headless browser drivers](https://docs.nimbleway.io/technologies/browserless-drivers), to ensure that your data collection is both efficient and effective. By leveraging Nimble’s vertical APIs, you can streamline your data operations, reduce complexity, and focus on deriving actionable insights from the information you gather.
