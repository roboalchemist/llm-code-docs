# Online Pipelines

## **Online Pipelines: A Flexible Data Delivery Platform**

### **Overview**

**Online Pipelines** is a next-generation data platform designed to simplify and accelerate the way businesses access and integrate the data they need. Our platform allows customers to select from a gallery of pre-configured data pipelines, tailored to their specific industry and use case.&#x20;

Whether you're in real estate, eCommerce, restaurants, or any other sector, Online Pipelines makes it easy to connect your business with the right data at the right time.

{% hint style="info" %}
The Nimble **Online Pipeline** offering will soon be accessible in the [Nimble Dashboard](https://docs.nimbleway.io/management-tools/nimble-dashboard). \
In the meantime, please reach out to your Sales representative to schedule a demo and to get more information.
{% endhint %}

### **Key Features**

1. **Customizable Pipelines:** Choose data pipelines from a wide selection in our gallery, organized by industry verticals. Customize each pipeline based on the data you need and how you want to receive it.
2. **Flexible Data Delivery:** Select how frequently you want to receive the data—whether it's daily, weekly, or monthly. We understand that different use cases require different schedules, and we offer complete flexibility to fit your workflow.
3. **Seamless Output Integration:** Our pipelines are designed to deliver data where you need it. Choose your preferred output destination from a variety of options, including:
   * **Cloud Storage**: AWS S3, Google Cloud Storage (GCS), Azure Blob Storage.
   * **Databases**: Snowflake data sharing, Databricks, MySQL, PostgreSQL, and more.
4. **Industry-Specific Pipelines:** Explore curated pipelines within dedicated verticals, including:
   * **Real Estate:** Property listings, pricing history, rental data, and more.
   * **Restaurants:** Insights on locations, reviews, menus, and customer engagement.
   * **eCommerce:** Product inventories, sales trends, customer reviews, and more.
5. **Detailed Documentation & Data Dictionary:** Each pipeline comes with comprehensive documentation, including metadata about the pipeline and a data dictionary to help you understand the structure and key fields of the data.
6. **On-Demand Scalability:** As your business grows, so can your data needs. Online Pipelines offers scalable solutions, ensuring that you can adjust your data intake to match evolving requirements.

### **How It Works**

1. **Browse the Gallery:** Explore pipelines tailored to your industry from our gallery of verticals. Each pipeline comes with a one-pager that details what data is included, sample records, metadata, and a downloadable data dictionary.
2. **Select Your Pipeline:** Once you've found a pipeline, customize the pipeline based on your preferences, such as delivery frequency and output destination. Our intuitive interface ensures a seamless selection process.
3. **Receive & Integrate Data:** After setting up your pipeline, data is delivered automatically to your specified destination. You'll receive updates as often as you've chosen, whether it's real-time or on a scheduled basis.
4. **Monitor and Adjust:** Our platform provides easy monitoring of your data pipelines, allowing you to track performance and make adjustments as needed to meet your business needs.

With **Online Pipelines**, we make the data delivery process streamlined, scalable, and customizable to ensure that you get the right data, at the right time, in the right format—so you can focus on driving business insights and growth.
{% endhint %}
