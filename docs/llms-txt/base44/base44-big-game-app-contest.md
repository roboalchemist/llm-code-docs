## About the contest

The Base44 Big Game app contest is a limited-time promotion that rewards the best game day apps built on Base44. You use Base44 to generate and polish an app, make it live, then show it off on social for a chance to win.

Build anything that makes the Big Game more fun, more organized, or more chaotic in the best way. For example, you might create:

* A watch party planner or invite tool
* A snack tracker or rating board
* A score predictor or prop tracker
* Halftime bingo, commercial rating, or meme generators

Build something useful or completely ridiculous. As long as it is built on Base44 and clearly tied to the Big Game experience, you are in the right place!

## Entering the contest

Enter by building on Base44, publishing your app, and sharing it on social with the right tags. You do not pay to enter and you do not need a paid Base44 plan.

<Warning>
  **Important:**

  * Each person can submit only **one** entry.
  * Team entries are not allowed. If you build with others, choose one person to submit.
  * Base44 support cannot submit an entry for you, change your post, or confirm your place in the contest.
</Warning>

**To enter the Base44 Big Game app contest:**

1. Create a Base44 account, if you do not already have one.
2. Type your idea into the prompt box on the Base44 Big Game page and build your game day app in Base44.
3. Publish your app so it is live and publicly accessible.
4. On X, Instagram, LinkedIn, or Facebook, create a public post that:
   * Includes a link to your live Base44 app.
   * Tags Base44 (for example, @Base44 or @Base44.app, depending on the platform).
   * Includes the hashtag **#MyBigGameApp**.
   * Stays public and accessible through the end of the Judgment Period.
5. [Go to the submission form](https://base44.com/biggame/form) on the Base44 Big Game page.
6. Paste the link to your social post into the form and submit it. This post link is your proof of entry for the prizes.

<Note>
  **Notes:**

  * Email, chat, or support tickets do not count as entries.
  * If your social post is private, deleted, or edited so that the tag or hashtag is missing, your entry may be disqualified.
</Note>

## Prizes, dates, and eligibility

The contest has four prize categories and a fixed schedule. You must meet all eligibility rules and deadlines to be considered.

### Prizes

The total prize pool is \$50,000 in U.S. dollars across four categories:

* **Boldest idea:** \$15,000 for the most original concept.
* **Best built:** \$15,000 for the most polished execution.
* **Most buzzworthy:** \$15,000 for the app that drives the most genuine traction.
* **Wild card:** \$5,000 for the judges’ pick.

A single entry can win only one prize category.

### Key dates

* Entry Period opens: **January 29, 2026 at 12:00 a.m. PT**
* Entry Period closes: **February 10, 2026 at 11:59 p.m. PT**
* Judgment Period: **February 12, 2026 through February 18, 2026**
* Winners announced: Within 7 days after the end of the Judgment Period (planned around February 25, 2026)

During the Entry Period, your app must stay live and your post must stay public. During the Judgment Period, judges review eligible entries and confirm data, including engagement for the Most Buzzworthy category.

### Who can enter

You can enter if:

* You are at least 18 years old, or the age of majority where you live, whichever is higher.
* You do not live in, and are not physically located in, any of the following: Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Russia, or the Crimea, Donetsk, or Luhansk regions of Ukraine.
* Your local laws allow you to participate in a contest like this.

You cannot enter if:

* You are an employee, director, or officer of the sponsor or its parent, affiliates, or agencies.
* You are an immediate family member of someone who is not eligible, or live in the same household.

## Content and app guidelines

Your app and your entry need to respect Base44’s standards, third party rights, and applicable laws. Sponsor can disqualify any entry that does not follow these rules or the Official Rules.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep the content safe and legal">
    Avoid content that is:

    * Sexually explicit, violent, hateful, or harassing.
    * Defamatory or designed to attack a person, group, or company.
    * Promoting illegal activity, illegal drugs, or weapons.
    * Focused on a specific political agenda.

    If you are not sure whether an idea crosses a line, choose a different angle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Respect trademarks and copyrights">
    Your app and entry must not:

    * Use trademarks, logos, or protected branding you do not own or have permission to use.
    * Use official NFL marks, team logos, Super Bowl names, or similar protected assets.
    * Include copyrighted photos, videos, music, text, or other media that you do not own or have the rights to use.
    * Feature another person’s face, name, or likeness without their informed consent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Follow disclosure rules">
    If your app includes any sponsorships, ads, or endorsements:

    * Make sure you follow local advertising and disclosure laws.
    * Clearly disclose any material connections inside your app or content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep your app and post live">
    Your entry depends on a live Base44 app and an accessible post. During the Entry Period and Judgment Period:

    * Your app must stay up, functional, and substantially the same experience as when you entered.
    * Your social post must stay public and must still include the app link, Base44 tag, and #MyBigGameApp.

    Major changes, downtime, or deleted posts can affect eligibility.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQs

Click a question below to learn more.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to pay or upgrade my Base44 plan to join?">
    No. There is no entry fee and you do not need a paid Base44 plan to participate. You just need a Base44 account and a published app that meets the contest rules.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I submit more than one app or enter as a team?">
    Each person can submit only one entry.

    * If you build multiple apps, choose the one you want to enter.
    * Team submissions are not supported. If you worked as a team, pick one person to submit as the entrant. That person is the one who receives the prize if the entry wins.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which social platforms count for my entry?">
    The contest flow is designed around:

    * X
    * Instagram
    * LinkedIn
    * Facebook
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Base44 confirm that my entry is registered or winning?">
    Base44 Customer Care cannot:

    * Confirm that a specific entry is “locked in”.
    * Check your analytics or traffic for you.
    * Share live judging results or odds.

    If you followed all the steps and your app and post stay live and public, you are entered. You will hear directly from the sponsor only if you are selected as a potential winner.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I update my app after I submit my entry?">
    You can fix bugs, polish design, or make small improvements. The core experience should remain substantially the same as when you entered.

    * If you break the app so it does not work during the Entry Period or Judgment Period, it can affect judging.
    * Major changes that turn it into a different app may impact how judges view your entry.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does judging work?">
    The contest is judged by a panel selected by the sponsor. This panel can include Base44 team members, advisors, and external experts. All judging decisions are final.

    A single entry can win only one category. If an app is in the running for several categories, the judges decide which category it fits best.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I delete my app or social post?">
    Your entry depends on both your app and your social post.

    * If you unpublish or delete your app during the Entry Period or Judgment Period, your entry may be disqualified.
    * If you delete your social post, set it to private, remove the Base44 tag, or remove #MyBigGameApp, judges may not be able to track your entry.

    To stay safe, keep everything live and unchanged until winners are announced.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I keep ownership of my app and idea?">
    Yes. You keep ownership of your app and your idea.

    When you enter, you give the sponsor a license to showcase your app and related content for marketing and promotional purposes, as explained in the Official Rules. For example, Base44 may feature your app in campaigns, on the Base44 site, or on social, with credit to you.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
