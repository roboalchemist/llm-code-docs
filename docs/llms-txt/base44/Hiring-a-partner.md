## Choosing a Base44 partner

Choosing the right partner can make all the difference to your project’s success. Each partner lists their skills, experience, and pricing options, so you can make an informed choice.

You can also filter the partners by services and languages to find the best match for you.

<Card icon="list-check" title="Tips for choosing the right partner">
  * **Check their profile:** Review the partner’s profile, website, and portfolio to see past projects and client recommendations.
  * **Meet with them:** Arrange an intro call (e.g. Zoom, Meet, or Discord) to make sure your expectations match.
  * **Request a clear proposal:** Include scope, pricing (hourly or project), and deliverables.
  * **Check availability:** Make sure the partner can meet your deadlines.
  * **Look for transparency:** Reliable partners will explain what they can deliver, how long it will take, and what you will receive.
</Card>

## Collaborating with your partner

Once you have found the right partner, set your project up for a productive and secure experience.

* **Communicate clearly:** Maintain open and professional communication with your partner at every stage of the project. Define milestones and delivery checkpoints early to ensure everyone is on the same page.
* **Keep records:** Document all agreements in writing, even a short email is enough. Confirm the project scope, pricing, payment schedule, and timeline **before** work begins.
* **Agree on payments:** Each partner sets their own pricing model; some charge an hourly rate, others a fixed project fee, or both. You could also consider using milestone-based payments (for example, paying a deposit followed by installments as work progresses). Decide together which approach works best and use secure payment methods such as bank transfer, PayPal, or Stripe.

<Warning>
  - All payments and agreements are handled directly between you and the partner.
  - Base44 does not charge commissions and does not mediate disputes or payments.
</Warning>

## Becoming a Base44 partner

If you have skills and experience with Base44, you can apply to join the partner network and offer your services to Base44 users. As a partner, you can set your own global project pricing, hourly rate, or both, and you will be featured in the official directory.

**To become a Base44 partner:**

1. Go to [Base44 Partners](https://app.base44.com/partners).
2. Click **Apply here**.
3. Submit your application through the sign-up form. The details you provide will be shown on your partner profile.

After approval, your profile will be published in the directory.

<Tip>
  **Tips for your profile:**

  * You can update your pricing and information any time as your business evolves.
  * As time goes on, you can collect client recommendations to help build your reputation.
</Tip>

## FAQs

Click a question below to learn more about Base44 partners.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to pay to use Base44 Partners?">
    No. You don't have to pay to hire a partner or to become one. All payments are handled directly between partners and clients.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hire more than one partner?">
    Yes, you can hire multiple partners for your project. Many people choose to work with different experts for areas like design, development, and integrations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I’m not satisfied with the service?">
    First, try to resolve issues directly with the partner or client. If this does not work, [contact us](https://docs.base44.com/Community-and-support/Contacting-support) with your issue.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change partners mid-project?">
    Yes, you can switch partners during a project if needed. Keep in mind that changing partners may involve additional costs, but it’s often better to make a change than continue with a partnership that isn’t working.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I receive an invoice or receipt?">
    Some partners, such as legally registered freelancers or agencies, can provide invoices or receipts. Be sure to confirm this with your partner before starting the project.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if a client complains about me as a partner?">
    If a client submits a complaint, Base44 will review the situation by listening to both sides. If there are repeated issues, your partner membership may be suspended.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Base44 help promote partners?">
    Yes, all approved partners are featured in the Base44 Partners directory. Base44 also promotes the program through its community and official channels, and may highlight your services to give you even more opportunities to connect with clients.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I build a strong reputation as a Base44 partner?">
    We recommend that you publish only the services you are qualified to deliver and communicate openly and professionally with all clients. If a project is not the right fit, you can decline it. Remember to always respond politely to maintain your reputation and refer users to other partners when appropriate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if there is an issue between a client and partner?">
    If you’ve hired a partner and have an issue, for example the work is not delivered on time, or if you have an issue with a client such as no payment, please keep in mind that Base44 Partners are independent third-party providers. We recommend resolving any concerns directly with your partner or client.

    [Contact us](https://docs.base44.com/Community-and-support/Contacting-support) if you want to report a concern. You can also review our [Partners Terms of Service](https://app.base44.com/partners/terms).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update my Base44 partner profile?">
    If you want to update your Base44 partner profile, [contact us](https://docs.base44.com/Community-and-support/Contacting-support) with the details you want to change. Please send us the details you’d like to update and we’ll make the changes for you.

    At the moment, there is no self-service admin panel for partners.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does communication between partners and customers work?">
    Communication between partners and potential customers happens directly via email. We do not have visibility into those conversations and recommend following up directly with the other party if you are experiencing issues.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I work with a Fiverr expert instead of a Base44 partner?">
    Yes. In addition to Base44 Partners, you may see an option to connect with an expert at Fiverr directly from the [Base44 Partners page](https://app.base44.com/partners).

    This experience is powered and provided by Fiverr, not Base44. It is designed for faster, short-term help, such as quick reviews, targeted troubleshooting, or small changes. You can chat instantly or schedule a time soon, and get offers at a fixed hourly rate shown in Fiverr.

    For larger or ongoing projects, complex integrations, or long-term strategic work, Base44 Partners are still the best fit.

    <Note>
      The Fiverr option is not available to everyone yet.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
