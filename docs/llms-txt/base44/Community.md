## FAQs

Click a question below to learn more.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How can I get feedback on my app?">
    Hop into [<u>Discord</u>](https://discord.gg/ThpYPZpVts) and share your app link in the [<u>#🌟showcase</u>](https://discord.com) channel to get feedback from the community.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I submit to Spotlight?">
    **To submit your app to Spotlight:**

    1. Log in to Base44.
    2. Go to the [Spotlight page](https://app.base44.com/spotlight).
    3. Scroll down and click **Submit your app**.
    4. Select the app you want to nominate.
    5. Click **Next**.
    6. Describe what your app does and explain why it should be featured.
    7. (Optional) Upload a screenshot.
    8. Click **Submit**.

    Our team reviews submissions and selects apps based on design, creativity, usefulness, and overall experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I become a Base44 affiliate?">
    You can join the Base44 affiliate program to earn commission for new subscribers. To get started, visit our [**Affiliate Program page**](https://base44.com/affiliates) to learn more and sign up. After joining, you'll receive a unique referral link to share.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
