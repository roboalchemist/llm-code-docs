# Downgrading your enterprise

You currently cannot downgrade an enterprise. However, you can downgrade each organization in the enterprise, except one. See [#removing-org-from-enterprise](https://docs.sonarsource.com/sonarqube-cloud/administering-sonarcloud/adding-organizations-to-your-enterprise#removing-org-from-enterprise "mention").
