<p>Mistral&#x27;s <strong>Magistral</strong> models are specialized reasoning models announced in June 2025. These models excel at multi-step logic, transparent reasoning, and complex problem-solving across multiple languages.</p>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="key-features-of-magistral-models">Key Features of Magistral Models<a href="#key-features-of-magistral-models" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Key Features of Magistral Models" title="Direct link to Key Features of Magistral Models" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>Chain-of-thought reasoning</strong>: Models provide step-by-step reasoning traces before arriving at final answers</li>
<li class=""><strong>Multilingual reasoning</strong>: Native reasoning capabilities across English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Arabic, Russian, Chinese, and more</li>
<li class=""><strong>Transparency</strong>: Traceable thought processes that can be followed and verified</li>
<li class=""><strong>Domain expertise</strong>: Optimized for structured calculations, programmatic logic, decision trees, and rule-based systems</li>
</ul>
