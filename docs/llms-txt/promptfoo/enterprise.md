Enterprise</h1></header>
<p>Promptfoo offers two deployment options to meet your security needs:</p>
<p><strong>Promptfoo Enterprise</strong> is our hosted SaaS solution that lets you securely scan your LLM applications without managing infrastructure.</p>
<p><strong>Promptfoo Enterprise On-Prem</strong> is our on-premises solution that includes a dedicated runner for deployments behind your firewall.</p>
<p>Both solutions offer a suite of tools to help you secure your LLM applications, including:</p>
<ul>
<li class="">Robust RBAC controls to manage multiple users and teams</li>
<li class="">Teams-based configurability for customizing targets, plugins, and scan configurations</li>
<li class="">Detailed reporting and analytics to monitor the security of your LLM applications</li>
<li class="">Remediation suggestions to help you fix vulnerabilities</li>
<li class="">Advanced filtering to find and sort through evals</li>
<li class="">Sharing and exporting functions to integrate Promptfoo with your existing tools</li>
</ul>
<p>Our platform works with any LLM application, agent, or foundation model that is live and ready for inference.</p>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" alt="Promptfoo Dashboard (Enterprise interface shown)" src="/assets/images/promptfoo-dashboard-6ed26392d614661cc84a11d85c865ff5.png" width="3546" height="1816" class="img_SS3x"></p>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="deployment-options">Deployment Options<a href="#deployment-options" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Deployment Options" title="Direct link to Deployment Options" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>We offer two deployment models:</p>
<ul>
<li class="">
<p><strong>Promptfoo Enterprise</strong>: Our fully-managed SaaS solution maintained by Promptfoo, allowing you to get started immediately with no infrastructure requirements.</p>
</li>
<li class="">
<p><strong>Promptfoo Enterprise On-Prem</strong>: Our self-hosted solution that can be deployed on any cloud provider, including AWS, Azure, and GCP. Includes a dedicated runner component for executing scans within your network perimeter.</p>
</li>
</ul>
