<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="setting-up-sso">Setting Up SSO<a href="#setting-up-sso" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Setting Up SSO" title="Direct link to Setting Up SSO" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>Promptfoo supports both basic authentication and SSO through SAML 2.0 and OIDC. To configure SSO with Promptfoo Enterprise, reach out to the support team with your IdP information and the Promptfoo team will configure it. The authentication endpoint is <code>auth.promptfoo.app</code>.</p>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="basic-authentication">Basic Authentication<a href="#basic-authentication" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Basic Authentication" title="Direct link to Basic Authentication" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>Promptfoo supports basic authentication into the application through <code>auth.promptfoo.app</code>. When an organization is created, the global admin will receive an email from Promptfoo to log in. Users, teams, and roles will be created in the Organization Settings of the Promptfoo application, which is detailed further in the <a class="" href="/docs/enterprise/teams/">Teams documentation</a>.</p>
<p>You can also authenticate into the application using a magic link. To do this, navigate to <code>auth.promptfoo.app</code> and click the &quot;Login with a magic link&quot; button. You will receive an email with a link to log in. If you do not receive an email, please be sure to check your spam folder.</p>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="authenticating-into-the-cli">Authenticating Into the CLI<a href="#authenticating-into-the-cli" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Authenticating Into the CLI" title="Direct link to Authenticating Into the CLI" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>You may wish to authenticate into the CLI when using Promptfoo. Follow these steps to connect Promptfoo Enterprise to the CLI.</p>
<ol>
<li class="">
<p>Install the Promptfoo CLI. Read <a class="" href="/docs/getting-started/">getting started</a> for help installing the CLI.</p>
</li>
<li class="">
<p>In the Promptfoo Enterprise app, select the &quot;CLI Login Information&quot; underneath your profile.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" alt="CLI Login Information" src="/assets/images/CLI-login-setting-2006bad88c5723f16dff1752bd9d9776.png" width="874" height="728" class="img_SS3x"></p>
<ol start="3">
<li class="">Copy the first command and run in your CLI. Your CLI will then be authenticated to Promptfoo Enterprise, allowing you to share eval results run locally.</li>
</ol>
<p><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" alt="CLI Login Command" src="/assets/images/CLI-login-key-ed5b9c879aa66aad27a2e035cb69d4e1.png" width="2128" height="1352" class="img_SS3x"></p>
<ol start="4">
<li class="">Once authenticated, you can run <code>promptfoo eval --share</code> or <code>promptfoo share</code> to share eval results to your Promptfoo Enterprise organization.</li>
</ol>

If you were previously an open-source user, you can share your local evals to your Promptfoo Enterprise organization by running <code>promptfoo share</code>.</p></div></div>
<p>Authenticating with your organization&#x27;s account enables <a class="" href="/docs/usage/sharing/#enterprise-sharing">team-based sharing</a>, ensuring your evaluation results are only visible to members of your organization rather than being publicly accessible.</p>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="working-with-multiple-teams">Working with Multiple Teams<a href="#working-with-multiple-teams" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Working with Multiple Teams" title="Direct link to Working with Multiple Teams" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>If your organization has multiple teams, you can manage which team context you&#x27;re operating in:</p>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="viewing-your-teams">Viewing Your Teams<a href="#viewing-your-teams" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Viewing Your Teams" title="Direct link to Viewing Your Teams" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<div class="language-sh codeBlockContainer_mQmQ theme-code-block" style="--prism-color:#393A34;--prism-background-color:#f6f8fa"><div class="codeBlockContent_t_Hd"><pre tabindex="0" class="prism-code language-sh codeBlock_RMoD thin-scrollbar" style="color:#393A34;background-color:#f6f8fa"><code class="codeBlockLines_AclH"><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token comment" style="color:#999988;font-style:italic"># List all teams you have access to</span><span class="token plain"></span><br></span><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token plain">promptfoo auth teams list</span><br></span></code></pre></div></div>
<p>This shows all available teams with a marker (●) next to your current team.</p>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="switching-teams">Switching Teams<a href="#switching-teams" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Switching Teams" title="Direct link to Switching Teams" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<div class="language-sh codeBlockContainer_mQmQ theme-code-block" style="--prism-color:#393A34;--prism-background-color:#f6f8fa"><div class="codeBlockContent_t_Hd"><pre tabindex="0" class="prism-code language-sh codeBlock_RMoD thin-scrollbar" style="color:#393A34;background-color:#f6f8fa"><code class="codeBlockLines_AclH"><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token comment" style="color:#999988;font-style:italic"># Switch to a different team</span><span class="token plain"></span><br></span><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token plain">promptfoo auth teams </span><span class="token builtin class-name">set</span><span class="token plain"> </span><span class="token string" style="color:#e3116c">&quot;Data Science&quot;</span><br></span></code></pre></div></div>
