markdown"><div style="position:relative"><header><h1>Pharmacy Red-Teaming Plugins</h1></header>
<p>The Pharmacy Red-Teaming Plugins are a specialized suite designed for AI systems operating in pharmacy and pharmaceutical contexts. These plugins test critical pharmacy safety functions including drug interaction detection, dosage calculation accuracy, and controlled substance compliance.</p>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="why-pharmacy-red-teaming-matters">Why Pharmacy Red-Teaming Matters<a href="#why-pharmacy-red-teaming-matters" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Why Pharmacy Red-Teaming Matters" title="Direct link to Why Pharmacy Red-Teaming Matters" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>Pharmacy AI systems have unique safety requirements that demand specialized testing:</p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>Patient Safety Impact</strong>: Medication errors are a leading cause of preventable adverse drug events and hospitalizations</li>
<li class=""><strong>Regulatory Consequences</strong>: Non-compliance with DEA regulations can result in pharmacy closure, sanctions, and criminal charges</li>
<li class=""><strong>Calculation Precision</strong>: Dosing errors, particularly in pediatric and renal patients, can be life-threatening</li>
<li class=""><strong>High-Frequency Operations</strong>: Large pharmacy chains like CVS Health dispense millions of prescriptions annually</li>
<li class=""><strong>Complex Drug Knowledge</strong>: Drug interactions, contraindications, and dosing require extensive pharmaceutical knowledge</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="available-pharmacy-plugins">Available Pharmacy Plugins<a href="#available-pharmacy-plugins" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Available Pharmacy Plugins" title="Direct link to Available Pharmacy Plugins" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="drug-interaction-detection">Drug Interaction Detection<a href="#drug-interaction-detection" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Drug Interaction Detection" title="Direct link to Drug Interaction Detection" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<p><strong>Plugin ID:</strong> <code>pharmacy:drug-interaction</code>
<strong>Severity: Critical</strong></p>
<p>Tests whether AI systems properly identify and warn about dangerous drug-drug, drug-food, and drug-supplement interactions. Drug interactions are a leading cause of preventable adverse events.</p>
<p><strong>What it detects:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>Major Drug-Drug Interactions</strong>: Contraindicated combinations like warfarin + NSAIDs, MAOIs + SSRIs</li>
<li class=""><strong>CYP450 Enzyme Interactions</strong>: Drugs affecting metabolism of other medications</li>
<li class=""><strong>QT Prolongation Risks</strong>: Combinations increasing cardiac arrhythmia risk</li>
<li class=""><strong>Serotonin Syndrome</strong>: Multiple serotonergic medications used together</li>
<li class=""><strong>Drug-Food Interactions</strong>: Significant food effects like grapefruit juice with statins</li>
<li class=""><strong>Drug-Supplement Interactions</strong>: Herbal supplements affecting prescriptions (St. John&#x27;s Wort, ginkgo)</li>
<li class=""><strong>Contraindicated Combinations</strong>: Drugs that should never be taken together</li>
<li class=""><strong>Additive Effects</strong>: Medications with compounding side effects</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Example scenarios:</strong></p>

<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="dosage-calculation">Dosage Calculation<a href="#dosage-calculation" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Dosage Calculation" title="Direct link to Dosage Calculation" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<p><strong>Plugin ID:</strong> <code>pharmacy:dosage-calculation</code>
<strong>Severity: Critical</strong></p>
<p>Tests accuracy of pharmaceutical dosage calculations including weight-based dosing, renal adjustments, unit conversions, and IV drip rates. Dosing errors are a major source of medication errors.</p>
<p><strong>What it detects:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>Weight-Based Dosing Errors</strong>: Incorrect mg/kg calculations for pediatric or adult patients</li>
<li class=""><strong>Renal Dose Adjustment Errors</strong>: Improper dose modifications based on CrCl or eGFR</li>
<li class=""><strong>Hepatic Dose Adjustment Errors</strong>: Incorrect adjustments for liver dysfunction</li>
<li class=""><strong>Unit Conversion Errors</strong>: Mistakes converting mg to mL, mcg to mg, units to mL</li>
<li class=""><strong>IV Drip Rate Errors</strong>: Incorrect mL/hr calculations for continuous infusions</li>
<li class=""><strong>Concentration Confusion</strong>: Confusing formulation strengths (100mg/mL vs 100mg/10mL)</li>
<li class=""><strong>BSA Calculation Errors</strong>: Mistakes in body surface area calculations for chemotherapy</li>
<li class=""><strong>Decimal Point Errors</strong>: Misplaced decimals leading to 10-fold or 100-fold errors</li>
<li class=""><strong>Pediatric Dosing Errors</strong>: Exceeding maximum pediatric doses or using adult doses</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Example scenarios:</strong></p>
<div class="language-text codeBlockContainer_mQmQ theme-code-block" style="--prism-color:#393A34;--prism-background-color:#f6f8fa"><div class="codeBlockContent_t_Hd"><pre tabindex="0" class="prism-code language-text codeBlock_RMoD thin-scrollbar" style="color:#393A34;background-color:#f6f8fa"><code class="codeBlockLines_AclH"><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token plain">❌ Calculating pediatric dose using outdated weight measurement</span><br></span><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token plain">❌ Missing renal dose adjustment for patient with impaired kidney function</span><br></span><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token plain">❌ Confusing medication concentrations leading to incorrect volume</span><br></span><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token plain">❌ Using wrong weight (actual vs adjusted) for obese patient dosing</span><br></span><span class="token-line" style="color:#393A34"><span class="token plain">✓ &quot;Current weight measurement required before calculating pediatric dose&quot;</span><br></span></code></pre></div></div>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="controlled-substance-compliance">Controlled Substance Compliance<a href="#controlled-substance-compliance" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Controlled Substance Compliance" title="Direct link to Controlled Substance Compliance" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<p><strong>Plugin ID:</strong> <code>pharmacy:controlled-substance-compliance</code>
<strong>Severity: High</strong></p>
<p>Tests adherence to DEA regulations for controlled substance prescribing, dispensing, and monitoring. Non-compliance can result in DEA sanctions, pharmacy closure, and legal consequences.</p>
<p><strong>What it detects:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>DEA Schedule Violations</strong>: Allowing refills on C-II drugs, exceeding refill limits on C-III/IV</li>
<li class=""><strong>Early Refill Approval</strong>: Dispensing controlled substances before previous supply exhausted</li>
<li class=""><strong>Red Flag Ignoring</strong>: Missing signs of doctor shopping, forged prescriptions, or diversion patterns</li>
<li class=""><strong>Prescription Validity</strong>: Accepting inappropriate prescription types (faxed C-II when not allowed)</li>
<li class=""><strong>PDMP Non-Compliance</strong>: Failing to check prescription drug monitoring programs as required</li>
<li class=""><strong>Transfer Violations</strong>: Allowing transfer of C-II prescriptions or multiple C-III/IV transfers</li>
<li class=""><strong>Quantity Limit Violations</strong>: Exceeding state-mandated days supply limits</li>
<li class=""><strong>Emergency Dispensing</strong>: Improperly authorizing emergency C-II supplies without follow-up</li>
<li class=""><strong>Documentation Failures</strong>: Not maintaining proper controlled substance records</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Example scenarios:</strong></p>
