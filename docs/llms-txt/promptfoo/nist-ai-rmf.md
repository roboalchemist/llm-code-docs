tocCollapsibleButton_iI2p">On this page</button></div><div class="theme-doc-markdown markdown"><div style="position:relative"><header><h1>NIST AI Risk Management Framework</h1></header>
<p>The NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF) is a voluntary framework developed by the U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology to help organizations manage risks associated with artificial intelligence systems. It provides a structured approach to identifying, assessing, and managing AI risks throughout the AI lifecycle.</p>
<p>The framework is organized into four core functions: Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage. Promptfoo&#x27;s red teaming capabilities focus primarily on the <strong>Measure</strong> function, which involves testing and evaluating AI systems against specific risk metrics.</p>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="framework-structure">Framework Structure<a href="#framework-structure" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Framework Structure" title="Direct link to Framework Structure" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>The NIST AI RMF organizes risk measurement into categories:</p>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="measure-1-appropriate-methods-and-metrics-are-identified-and-applied">MEASURE 1: Appropriate methods and metrics are identified and applied<a href="#measure-1-appropriate-methods-and-metrics-are-identified-and-applied" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to MEASURE 1: Appropriate methods and metrics are identified and applied" title="Direct link to MEASURE 1: Appropriate methods and metrics are identified and applied" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>1.1</strong>: Approaches and metrics for measurement of AI risks are selected, implemented, and documented based on appropriate AI RMF profiles, frameworks, and best practices</li>
<li class=""><strong>1.2</strong>: Appropriateness of AI metrics and effectiveness of risk controls are regularly assessed and updated</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="measure-2-ai-systems-are-evaluated-for-trustworthy-characteristics">MEASURE 2: AI systems are evaluated for trustworthy characteristics<a href="#measure-2-ai-systems-are-evaluated-for-trustworthy-characteristics" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to MEASURE 2: AI systems are evaluated for trustworthy characteristics" title="Direct link to MEASURE 2: AI systems are evaluated for trustworthy characteristics" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>2.1</strong>: Test sets, metrics, and details about the tools used during Assessment, Audit, Verification and Validation (AAVV) are documented</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.2</strong>: Evaluations involving human subjects meet applicable requirements and are representative of deployment context</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.3</strong>: AI system performance or assurance criteria are measured qualitatively or quantitatively and demonstrated for conditions similar to deployment</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.4</strong>: The AI system is evaluated regularly for safety risks</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.5</strong>: The AI system to be deployed is demonstrated to be valid and reliable</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.6</strong>: The AI system is evaluated for potential for misuse and abuse</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.7</strong>: AI system security and resilience are evaluated and documented</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.8</strong>: Privacy and data protection practices are evaluated and documented</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.9</strong>: Risk values are assessed and documented</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.10</strong>: Privacy risk of the AI system is examined and documented</li>
<li class=""><strong>2.11</strong>: Fairness and bias are evaluated and results are documented</li>

<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="best-practices-for-nist-ai-rmf-compliance">Best Practices for NIST AI RMF Compliance<a href="#best-practices-for-nist-ai-rmf-compliance" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Best Practices for NIST AI RMF Compliance" title="Direct link to Best Practices for NIST AI RMF Compliance" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>When testing for NIST AI RMF compliance with Promptfoo:</p>
<ol>
<li class=""><strong>Document your testing</strong>: NIST emphasizes documentation of testing methodologies (MEASURE 2.1)</li>
<li class=""><strong>Regular evaluation</strong>: Set up continuous testing to meet the &quot;regularly assessed&quot; requirements</li>
<li class=""><strong>Representative testing</strong>: Ensure test conditions match deployment contexts (MEASURE 2.2)</li>
<li class=""><strong>Risk tracking</strong>: Use Promptfoo&#x27;s reporting features to track identified risks over time (MEASURE 3.1)</li>
<li class=""><strong>Stakeholder feedback</strong>: Combine automated testing with manual review and user feedback (MEASURE 3.3)</li>
<li class=""><strong>Holistic approach</strong>: Test across all four core functions (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage), not just Measure</li>
</ol>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="limitations-of-automated-testing">Limitations of Automated Testing<a href="#limitations-of-automated-testing" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Limitations of Automated Testing" title="Direct link to Limitations of Automated Testing" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>While Promptfoo helps automate many NIST AI RMF measures, some requirements need additional processes:</p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>MEASURE 2.12</strong>: Environmental impact assessment requires infrastructure monitoring</li>
<li class=""><strong>MEASURE 3.3</strong>: Stakeholder feedback processes require organizational procedures</li>
<li class=""><strong>MEASURE 4.3</strong>: Business value assessment requires business context beyond automated testing</li>
</ul>
<p>Automated red teaming should be part of a comprehensive NIST AI RMF compliance program that includes governance, documentation, and stakeholder engagement.</p>
