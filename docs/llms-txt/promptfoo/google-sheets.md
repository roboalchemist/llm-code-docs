<p><strong>Create a new sheet automatically</strong> by omitting the <code>gid</code> parameter. The system will:</p>
<ul>
<li class="">Create a new sheet with a timestamp-based name (e.g., &quot;Sheet1234567890&quot;)</li>
<li class="">Write results to this new sheet</li>
<li class="">Preserve existing sheets and their data</li>
</ul>

<p>This behavior helps prevent accidental data overwrites while keeping your evaluation results organized within the same Google Sheets document.</p>
