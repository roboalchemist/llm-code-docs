</ul>

<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="model-selection-guidelines">Model Selection Guidelines<a href="#model-selection-guidelines" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Model Selection Guidelines" title="Direct link to Model Selection Guidelines" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>Choose models based on your specific needs:</p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>Best Overall</strong>: GPT-4.1 or Claude 4 Opus - Superior coding, instruction following, and long-context understanding</li>
<li class=""><strong>Fast &amp; Cheap</strong>: GPT-4.1-nano - Lowest latency and cost while maintaining strong capabilities</li>
<li class=""><strong>Balanced</strong>: GPT-4.1-mini or Claude 4 Sonnet - Good performance with lower cost than full models</li>
<li class=""><strong>Extended Context</strong>: Llama 4 Scout (10M tokens) for processing entire codebases or multiple documents</li>
<li class=""><strong>Code Generation</strong>: Codestral series for specialized code tasks</li>
<li class=""><strong>Reasoning</strong>: DeepSeek-R1, o-series models, or Grok-4 for complex reasoning tasks</li>
<li class=""><strong>Long Context</strong>: Models with extended context windows for processing large documents</li>
<li class=""><strong>Multimodal</strong>: Vision-capable models for text and image processing, including Llama 4 series</li>
</ul>
<p>Visit the <a href="https://github.com/marketplace/models/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">GitHub Models marketplace</a> to compare model capabilities and pricing.</p>
