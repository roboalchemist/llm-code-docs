class="theme-doc-markdown markdown"><div style="position:relative"><header><h1>Policy Plugin</h1></header>
<p>The Policy red teaming plugin is a customizable tool designed to test whether an AI system adheres to specific policies or guidelines.</p>
<p>This plugin helps ensure that the AI&#x27;s responses align with predefined rules, ethical standards, or operational constraints set by the organization.</p>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="purpose">Purpose<a href="#purpose" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Purpose" title="Direct link to Purpose" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>The Policy plugin helps to:</p>
<ol>
<li class="">Assess the AI&#x27;s ability to follow custom-defined policies or guidelines.</li>
<li class="">Identify instances where the AI might deviate from established rules or standards.</li>
<li class="">Test the AI&#x27;s capacity to maintain compliance with specific requirements across various scenarios.</li>
</ol>

<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="how-it-works">How It Works<a href="#how-it-works" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to How It Works" title="Direct link to How It Works" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>The Policy plugin generates test cases based on the custom policy provided. It creates prompts that:</p>
<ol>
<li class="">Challenge the AI&#x27;s adherence to the specified policy.</li>
<li class="">Present scenarios that might tempt the AI to deviate from the policy.</li>
<li class="">Test the AI&#x27;s understanding and application of the policy across different contexts.</li>
</ol>
<h3 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="prompt-formatting">Prompt Formatting<a href="#prompt-formatting" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Prompt Formatting" title="Direct link to Prompt Formatting" translate="no">​</a></h3>
<p>The Policy plugin automatically selects the appropriate prompt format based on the system type:</p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>Simple format (<code>Prompt:</code>)</strong> - Used for most systems with single-line test cases</li>
<li class=""><strong>Multi-line format (<code>PromptBlock:</code>)</strong> - Used for log analysis systems with structured data</li>
</ul>
<p>For log analysis systems, the plugin generates realistic log entries that contain subtle policy violations, while other systems receive direct conversational prompts.</p>

<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="evaluation-criteria">Evaluation Criteria<a href="#evaluation-criteria" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Evaluation Criteria" title="Direct link to Evaluation Criteria" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>The plugin assesses the AI&#x27;s response based on:</p>
<ol>
<li class="">Policy Adherence: Does the output strictly follow the guidelines set in the custom policy?</li>
<li class="">Consistency: Is the AI consistent in applying the policy across different types of requests?</li>
<li class="">Explanation: Does the AI explain its actions in relation to the policy when refusing a request?</li>
<li class="">Alternatives: When unable to fulfill a request due to policy constraints, does the AI offer policy-compliant alternatives?</li>
<li class="">Boundary Recognition: Does the AI recognize and communicate when a request pushes the boundaries of the policy?</li>
</ol>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="importance-in-gen-ai-red-teaming">Importance in Gen AI Red Teaming<a href="#importance-in-gen-ai-red-teaming" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Importance in Gen AI Red Teaming" title="Direct link to Importance in Gen AI Red Teaming" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>Testing for policy adherence is critical for:</p>
<ul>
<li class="">Ensuring the AI system operates within defined ethical and operational boundaries</li>
<li class="">Maintaining consistency in the AI&#x27;s responses across various scenarios</li>
<li class="">Identifying areas where policy implementation might need refinement or clarification</li>
</ul>
<p>By incorporating the Policy plugin in your LLM red teaming strategy, you can ensure that your AI system consistently adheres to your organization&#x27;s specific guidelines and standards.</p>
