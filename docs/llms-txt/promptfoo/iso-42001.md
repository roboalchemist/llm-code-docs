tocCollapsibleButton_iI2p">On this page</button></div><div class="theme-doc-markdown markdown"><div style="position:relative"><header><h1>ISO 42001</h1></header>
<p>ISO/IEC 42001:2023 is the international standard for AI Management Systems. It provides organizations with a structured approach to managing AI risks, ensuring responsible AI development and deployment. The standard emphasizes governance, risk management, and continuous improvement of AI systems throughout their lifecycle.</p>
<p>The ISO 42001 framework covers seven key risk domains:</p>
<ol>
<li class="">Accountability &amp; Human Oversight</li>
<li class="">Fairness &amp; Bias Prevention</li>
<li class="">Privacy &amp; Data Protection</li>
<li class="">Robustness &amp; Resilience</li>
<li class="">Security &amp; Vulnerability Management</li>
<li class="">Safety &amp; Ethical Use</li>
<li class="">Transparency &amp; Trustworthiness</li>
</ol>
<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="scanning-for-iso-42001-compliance">Scanning for ISO 42001 Compliance<a href="#scanning-for-iso-42001-compliance" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Scanning for ISO 42001 Compliance" title="Direct link to Scanning for ISO 42001 Compliance" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>This guide will walk through how to use Promptfoo&#x27;s features to test for and mitigate ISO 42001 compliance risks.</p>
<p>Promptfoo helps identify vulnerabilities across all seven ISO 42001 risk domains through comprehensive red teaming. The end result is a detailed report card that maps your AI system&#x27;s compliance with ISO 42001 requirements.</p>

<p>ISO 42001 emphasizes transparency in AI operations and prevention of misinformation to maintain user trust and system reliability.</p>
<p>Test for transparency and trustworthiness:</p>
<ul>
<li class=""><strong>Misinformation prevention</strong>: Verify the system doesn&#x27;t generate false or misleading information.</li>
<li class=""><strong>Hallucination detection</strong>: Test for fabricated information presented as factual.</li>
<li class=""><strong>Identity verification</strong>: Ensure the AI doesn&#x27;t impersonate people or organizations.</li>
<li class=""><strong>Claim substantiation</strong>: Check that the system doesn&#x27;t make unverifiable claims.</li>
