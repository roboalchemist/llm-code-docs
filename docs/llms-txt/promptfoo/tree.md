class="theme-doc-markdown markdown"><div style="position:relative"><header><h1>Tree-based Jailbreaks Strategy</h1></header>
<p>The Tree-based Jailbreaks strategy is an advanced technique designed to systematically explore and potentially bypass an AI system&#x27;s constraints by creating a branching structure of single-shot prompts.</p>
<p>It&#x27;s based on the <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.02119" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="">Tree of Attacks</a> paper published by Mehrotra et al in 2023.</p>

<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="how-it-works">How It Works<a href="#how-it-works" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to How It Works" title="Direct link to How It Works" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>The Tree-based Jailbreaks strategy works by:</p>
<ol>
<li class="">Starting with a root prompt that attempts to elicit undesired behavior.</li>
<li class="">Based on the AI&#x27;s response, creating multiple branching follow-up prompts.</li>
<li class="">For each branch, generating new sub-branches based on hypothetical responses.</li>
<li class="">Exploring this tree of possibilities to find the most effective single-shot prompts.</li>
</ol>

<h2 class="anchor anchorTargetStickyNavbar_tleR" id="importance-in-gen-ai-red-teaming">Importance in Gen AI Red Teaming<a href="#importance-in-gen-ai-red-teaming" class="hash-link" aria-label="Direct link to Importance in Gen AI Red Teaming" title="Direct link to Importance in Gen AI Red Teaming" translate="no">​</a></h2>
<p>The tree-based jailbreak strategy allows for a comprehensive exploration of potential vulnerabilities by:</p>
<ol>
<li class="">Testing a wide range of related but distinct prompts.</li>
<li class="">Identifying the most effective paths for bypassing AI safeguards.</li>
<li class="">Simulating diverse attack scenarios without relying on conversational context.</li>
</ol>
<p>This approach can cover a wide range of inputs and identify weaknesses that might not be apparent with manual testing methods.</p>
