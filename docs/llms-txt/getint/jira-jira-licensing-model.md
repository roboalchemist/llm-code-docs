# Jira-Jira Licensing Model

Welcome to the detailed guide on Getint’s **Jira-Jira licensing model**, designed to offer clarity and flexibility for your integration needs.

### **Licensing Options for Jira-Jira Integrations** <a href="#licensing-options-for-jira-jira-integrations" id="licensing-options-for-jira-jira-integrations"></a>

When integrating two Jira instances—whether **Jira Software** or **Jira Service Management**—Getint offers two distinct licensing options to suit your needs:

#### **1. Standard License** <a href="#id-1.-standard-license" id="id-1.-standard-license"></a>

Each Jira instance involved in the integration requires a valid license with the Getint app installed.

**How it Works:**

* Install the Getint app on both Jira instances.
* Manage the integration from a single interface on one of the Jira instances.
* The app installation on the second instance is primarily for licensing and support purposes.

#### **2. Remote License** <a href="#id-2.-remote-license" id="id-2.-remote-license"></a>

Ideal for scenarios where installing the app on both Jira instances is not feasible, such as when integrating with external partners.

**How it Works:**

* Install the app on one Jira instance only.
* Use a **Remote License** for the second Jira instance.
* This option enables seamless integration without requiring the second party to install additional apps.

For pricing details or to purchase a Remote License, contact us at our [Help Center](https://getint.io/help-center).

### **Flexible Licensing for Multi-Instance Integrations** <a href="#flexible-licensing-for-multi-instance-integrations" id="flexible-licensing-for-multi-instance-integrations"></a>

If your organization manages multiple integrations—such as connecting **four or more instances**—our **Flexible License** provides unmatched scalability:

* **What it Covers:**
  * Integrate up to 10 instances, including Jira or other tools.
  * Flexibility to swap integrated tools during the license term.
* **Who is it For:**
  * Managed service providers and enterprises with complex integration needs.

For custom pricing and details, contact us at our [Help Center](https://getint.io/help-center).

### **Why Did We Update the Jira-Jira Licensing Model?** <a href="#why-did-we-update-the-jira-jira-licensing-model" id="why-did-we-update-the-jira-jira-licensing-model"></a>

Our updated licensing model reflects our commitment to fairness and transparency while balancing the needs of customers and operational realities:

* **Previous Model:**
  * Treated all integrations equally, regardless of the size of the organizations involved.
  * Allowed large enterprises to integrate with smaller organizations at the smaller party’s licensing cost, despite requiring enterprise-level support and handling high data volumes.
* **Updated Approach:**
  * Ensures licensing fees reflect the actual resources required to support each integration.
  * Offers flexibility with the **Remote License** for situations where installing apps on both ends isn’t possible.

This change ensures sustainable pricing while maintaining the high standards of service our customers expect.

### **Get Started with Jira-Jira Licensing** <a href="#get-started-with-jira-jira-licensing" id="get-started-with-jira-jira-licensing"></a>

To set up your Jira-Jira integration:

**Choose Your Licensing Option:**

* **Standard License** (app installed on both instances)
* **Remote License** (app installed on one instance only)

Feel free to contact us for assistance. Our team will guide you through the setup process and help you select the best license for your use case.
