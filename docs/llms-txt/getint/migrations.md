# Migrations

### Streamline Your Migration with Getint.io

At Getint.io, we understand the critical importance of transitioning your work smoothly between different platforms. Whether it's moving issues, tasks, or incidents from one tool to another, our platform is designed to facilitate this process efficiently and effectively.

### **Preparing for Migration**

Initiating your migration journey is straightforward with Getint.io. To start, simply contact us at <jacek@getint.io>. Our team is ready to provide you with detailed guidance on executing your migration seamlessly.

### **Pricing Strategy**

Based on our extensive experience, we've found that a three-month preparation period is optimal. This timeframe ensures that your migration is meticulously planned, your team is well-acquainted with the new tool, and thorough testing confirms the successful transfer of your desired data.

Migration preparation might seem straightforward at first glance, but the devil is in the details. The complexities of different tools—with their unique fields, statuses, and user bases—require careful mapping and decision-making about elements like comments, custom fields, and attachments.

Unlike our regular licensing model, migration pricing is not based on the number of Jira users or an annual fee. Instead, we've tailored our pricing to the volume of issues you need to migrate, recognizing the varying scopes of migration projects. For up to 2,000 issues/tasks/incidents, the cost is $1,500. For up to 20,000, it's $3,000, with custom pricing available for larger volumes. This pricing structure applies to a 3-month migration license, accommodating even those needing to migrate upwards of 1 million tasks. [View detailed pricing here](https://getint.io/pricing/).

Upon payment, we'll activate the migration feature, setting you on the path to migrating your historical data. The setup process mirrors that of standard integration but includes specifying a time range for the data you wish to migrate.

### **Migration Assistance**

For clients who prefer not to navigate the migration setup independently, Getint.io offers a consulting service. Our experts can configure the Getint.io tool on your behalf, ensuring a tailored migration process that meets your specific needs.

To proceed, we'll need:

* Admin access to both of the tools you're integrating.
* An Excel file detailing all the types and fields you wish to migrate. [Here is a template to get you started](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/10Wx3ema8GsDcBKFJMnsUujbQzNxFtByPxaaFLDy2lu0/edit?usp=share_link).

The cost of this service is determined by the complexity of your migration project, with a standard rate of $120/hour.

For a hassle-free migration experience that ensures no data is left behind, turn to Getint.io. Our dedicated team is here to support you every step of the way. Contact us to discuss your migration needs and how we can help you achieve a seamless transition.
