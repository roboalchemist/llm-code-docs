# How to Pay for the Apps

For seamless management and integration of your workflows, Getint offers a range of Jira-related applications, including but not limited to:

* [Jira Asana Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223932/getint-jira-asana-integration-sync?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)
* [Jira GitLab Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223999/getint-jira-gitlab-sync-integration?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)
* [Jira Jira Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223930/getint-issue-sync-for-jira-integration?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)
* [Jira ServiceNow Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223935/jira-servicenow-integration-by-getint-io?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)
* [Jira ZenDesk Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223934/getint-zendesk-integration-issue-sync?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)
* [Jira Wrike Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223936/getint-jira-wrike-integration-sync?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)
* [Jira GitHub Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223933/getint-jira-github-sync-integration?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)
* [Jira Azure DevOps Integration](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/apps/1223931/azure-devops-for-jira-integration-sync-issues-to-work-items?hosting=cloud\&tab=overview)

### **Atlassian Marketplace Payments**

By default, all Jira-related integrations are payable via the Atlassian Marketplace after the trial period. To ensure a smooth transition to a paid plan:

* Verify that payment settings are activated within your Atlassian account.
* Visit any of the specific app links above to see if a "BUY" button is available. Clicking it will set you up for purchase confirmation once the trial concludes.

### **Alternative Payment Methods**

Should you require access to paid features ahead of schedule, including migrations, on-premise configurations, custom development, or premium SLA options, we're here to facilitate that. Direct payments can be arranged through:

* **Stripe (Credit Card)**: For quick and secure transactions.
* **Bank Transfer (Invoice)**: We issue an invoice for bank transfers in USD, accommodating those who prefer traditional payment methods.

These alternatives are also the primary routes for processing payments for Non-Jira related integrations, ensuring direct billing from us to you.

### **Non-Jira Related Integrations**

For integrations not involving Jira, we handle the billing process directly with two straightforward payment options:

* **Bank Transfer in USD**: Based on the issued invoice.
* **Stripe**: For expedited and secure credit card transactions.

For more details on payment options or to discuss your specific integration needs, please don't hesitate to reach out to <jacek@getint.io>. We're committed to providing you with the flexibility and support needed to optimize your integration processes efficiently.
