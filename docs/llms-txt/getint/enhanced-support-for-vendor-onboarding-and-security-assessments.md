# Enhanced Support for Vendor Onboarding and Security Assessments

Getint is committed to supporting our clients through the Vendor Onboarding process and comprehensive Security Assessments. We understand these steps are crucial for ensuring compliance and security standards are met, aligning with your organizational requirements.

Given the intricate and time-intensive nature of these processes, Getint offers dedicated participation in Vendor Onboarding and Security Questionnaires for clients with annual licenses of $5,000 or more. For clients with licenses below this threshold, we provide a tailored approach, estimating support costs based on the complexity and time demands of the requested assessments.

Our goal is to facilitate a seamless integration experience, ensuring that Getint not only meets but exceeds your security and compliance expectations.
