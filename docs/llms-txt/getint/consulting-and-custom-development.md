# Consulting and Custom Development

At Getint, we go beyond our standard offerings to provide specialized consulting and custom development services. Our team of experts is equipped to handle everything from script writing to the planning and execution of comprehensive migrations between tools. Whether you're looking to develop a connector not currently supported, request specific features, craft custom scripts for unique actions, or seek full assistance in your migration or integration project, we're here to help.

**Our Custom Services Include:**

* **Custom Development and Script Writing**: Tailored solutions to extend Getint’s capabilities to meet your specific requirements.
* **Consulting Services**: Expert guidance on planning and executing migrations and integrations smoothly and efficiently.

**Pricing Details:**

* Our custom development and consulting services are billed at $120/hour, in addition to our regular license fees.
* To ensure flexibility and accessibility, we offer a minimum package of 5 hours for $600.

These services are particularly beneficial for scenarios requiring advanced customization or direct expertise in navigating complex integration landscapes. Let Getint's team empower your projects with customized solutions designed to fulfill your unique integration and migration needs.
