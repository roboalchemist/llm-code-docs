# Platform Release History

At Getint, we're constantly improving our app to give you a better experience. Our platform connects tools like Jira, GitHub, Azure DevOps, ServiceNow, and others, making it easier to synchronize and manage your projects. Whether you're overseeing a team, transferring data, or connecting multiple systems, Getint simplifies these tasks with its user-friendly design and robust functionality.

### Check out our latest releases on GitHub

[Getint Platform Versions](https://gist.github.com/getintio/4c34c3a23975a41fcd414e93b541be00)

Each update focuses on introducing new features, improving existing ones, and addressing user feedback. Recent updates include merging field options, improved synchronization, and a redesigned workflow builder to support your daily tasks effectively.

Getint also offers the flexibility to customize integrations based on your needs. With options for bi-directional data synchronization, detailed reporting, and advanced filters, the platform gives you the control and tools you need to get the job done.

Thank you for being a part of the Getint. Your feedback helps shape the future of our platform, and we’re excited to keep growing together.

**Your Feedback Matters!** Let us know what you think at our [Support Center](https://getint.io/help-center) or through the feedback option in the app.
