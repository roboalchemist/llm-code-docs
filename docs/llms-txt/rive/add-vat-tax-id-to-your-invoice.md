# Add VAT/Tax ID to Your Invoice

> Need a tax invoice with your VAT or Tax ID for your records? Use our form and we'll email you an updated invoice.

[**Request a Tax Invoice**](https://airtable.com/appUtquW7bOB1IIqA/pagvWDCa4FRE57HFt/form)

You'll need:

* Your original Stripe invoice/receipt number
* Invoice date and amount
* Your company's billing address
* Your VAT/Tax ID number

Your tax invoice will be emailed to you immediately.

***

## For Future Invoices

To have your tax info included automatically on all future receipts, update your billing details in Stripe:

1. Go to [rive.app/account](https://rive.app/account)
2. Click "Manage Billing" to open the Stripe Customer Portal
3. Update your customer information with your Tax ID
4. Save changes

All future invoices will include your tax details automatically.
