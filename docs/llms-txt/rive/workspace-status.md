# Workspace Status

Your workspace status appears under the workspace name in your account settings.

| Status                    | What it means                                                            |
| :------------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Active**                | Paid and working normally                                                |
| **Active (ends \[date])** | Set to cancel at end of billing cycle—you keep access until then         |
| **Suspended**             | Canceled, no longer accessible                                           |
| **Failed Payment**        | Payment failed, workspace canceled—update your payment method to restore |
