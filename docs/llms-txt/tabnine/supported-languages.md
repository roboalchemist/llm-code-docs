# Supported Languages

Tabnine is an AI software development platform with architectural freedom and model flexibility.

The majority of our key capabilities come from chat and agents powered by third-party models.

The LLMs we use cover 600+ languages, libraries, and frameworks.

Customers with private installations can connect and fine-tune their own models.
