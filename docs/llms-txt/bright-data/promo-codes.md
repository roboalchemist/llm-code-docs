# Bright Data Promo Codes

Bright Data offers promo codes to save money on your subscription plans. You can get exclusive discounts on specific products or services by applying a promo code. This guide will show you everything you need to know about the promo codes.

## How to Apply a Promo Code

To apply a promo code to your subscription plan, follow the steps below:

### Step 1. Go to the Billing page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Bright Data website">
    Visit [Bright Data](https://brightdata.com/cp/start) page and sign in to your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the &#x22;Billing&#x22; page">
    Click on the [Billing](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing) page on the left navigation bar.

    <Frame>
            <img src="https://mintcdn.com/brightdata/2RrKkArDJDw55ilE/images/general/account/billing-and-pricing/promo-codes/billing-button.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=2RrKkArDJDw55ilE&q=85&s=6cf16c9c7f268f6c275575f105c27a1e" alt="billing-button" width="1918" height="911" data-path="images/general/account/billing-and-pricing/promo-codes/billing-button.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do the promo codes work with the 'dollar-for-dollar' first deposit matching promo?">
    Yes, you can use both a promo code and the dollar-for-dollar matching promotion together.

    The promo code will be applied directly to your subscription plan, reducing the cost of your plan. Additionally, we will match your first deposit dollar-for-dollar, up to \$500. The matched promotional credits are valid for 90 days. To maintain the bonus, a minimum monthly usage of \$5 is required.

    <Note>
      The matching dollar for dollar credit works for the first deposit only.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
