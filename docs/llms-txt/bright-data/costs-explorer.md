# Keeping track of your usage: Costs Explorer

> We understand the importance of transparency and control over your data usage and expenses. That's why we've introduced the Cost Explorer feature, a powerful tool designed to give you detailed insights into your usage statistics and costs

### Where to Find the Cost Explorer

You can navigate to the costs explorer using [This Link](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing/costs).
Alternatively you can also easily access the Cost Explore feature in the **Billing** tab on the left bar of the control panel. Afterwards look for the dedicated tab clearly marked **"Cost Explorer"**.

### What the Cost Explorer Offers

The Cost Explore feature provides comprehensive usage statistics, which can be broken down by: each product, each zone, charge types etc. for each day or hour you have used our products during the selected time period.
You can also see a breakdown for funds that were used up by your Datasets and Collectors.
The data can be displayed in line chart, bar chart or table.

This allows you to see exactly where your funds are being allocated.

### Real-Time Accuracy

All the information displayed in the Cost Explorer is accurate and updated in real time. This ensures that you always have the most current data at your fingertips.

### Customization Options

The Cost Explorer offers extensive customization options, allowing you to tailor the insights to your specific needs. Whether you want to see a detailed breakdown of your usage or a high-level overview, Cost Explorer has you covered.

### Visual and User-Friendly

The data in the Cost Explorer is displayed in a visual, easily readable format. This makes it simple to understand your usage and costs at a glance. These tables and charts are also available for download as .csv files.

### Answering Your Questions

If you have any questions about your usage or wish to better understand your bill, Cost Explorer is the place to go. It provides all the answers you need, making it an invaluable resource for managing your expenses.
