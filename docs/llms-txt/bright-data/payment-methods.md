# Accepted Payment Methods

> We have an automated billing system that supports a host of payment methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="PayPal">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the billing page">
        [Billing Page](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing) > Click on `Add Payment Method`.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select &#x22;PayPal&#x22; as your payment method.">
        You will then be redirected to PayPal page.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="AliPay">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the billing page">
        [Billing Page](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing) > Click on `Add Payment Method`.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select &#x22;AliPay&#x22; as your payment method.">
        You will then be redirected to AliPay page.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Payoneer">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the billing page">
        [Billing Page](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing) > Click on `Add Payment Method`.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select &#x22;Payoneer&#x22; as your payment method.">
        You will then be redirected to Payoneer page.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Credit Card">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the billing page">
        [Billing Page](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing) > Click on `Add Payment Method`.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select &#x22;Credit Card&#x22; as your payment method.">
        Now you can click the `Add Balance` button and make a payment with your credit card.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Wire transfer">
    <Warning>
      Wire transfer payments are only available after a payment of \$5,000 is made.
    </Warning>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the billing page">
        [Billing Page](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing) > Click on `Add Payment Method`.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select &#x22;Other&#x22; as your payment method." />

      <Step title="Select the &#x22;Wire transfer&#x22; option as your payment method.">
        Now you can click the "Add Balance" button and make a payment with Wire transfer.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Amazon Marketplace">
    You can also buy subscription using Amazon Marketplace.

    To purchase the subscription via Amazon Marketplace, please send your account manager your Amazon marketplace ID (i.e. buy an Amazon Marketplace contract, follow the link, do not signup a new Bright Data account, instead, just send your ID). Your account manager will update the Amazon Marketplace ID on your account.

    <Warning>
      There a few limiation when you purchase a subscription using the Amazon Marketplace

      * You will be allowed to use only products purchased via Amazon marketplace.
      * We do not support paying for Cloud Proxy Manager via Amazon Marketplace.
      * All other payment methods will be removed from your account.
      * Auto-recharge will not be available
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  To ensure your payment goes through smoothly, please disable any ad blockers before proceeding.
</Note>
