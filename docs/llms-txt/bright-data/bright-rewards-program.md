# About the Bright Rewards Program

> This program is designed to reward our loyal customers for their continued usage of our services. Eligible users will receive Bright Points on a monthly basis to purchase Bright Data services with.

## Earn & redeem points

The **Bright Rewards** program allows you to earn **points** based on your monthly spending. The more you use, the more you save!\
Key benefits:

* Earn rewards automatically based on your usage
* Redeem your credits easily when adding funds
* Higher spending unlocks greater rewards

### **How to Redeem Your Bright Rewards**

You have two ways to redeem your Bright Rewards points:\
Go to the [billing section](https://brightdata.com/cp/billing/overview) in the control panel, then:

* Click the "Redeem" button in the Bright Rewards section.

Or

* Use the "Add Funds" button to redeem your points.
