# Referral Program

> Bright Data’s referral program offers a simple way to earn by recommending our platform to others. Whether you're introducing a colleague or suggesting Bright Data to a business, you can receive up to 50% of the revenue from each customer you bring in. This guide breaks down how the program works, how it differs from the affiliate program, and how you can start earning today.

Bright Data's referral program offers a straightforward way to earn rewards by introducing others to our platform. Here's how it works:

1. **Share Your Referral Link**: After joining the referral program, you'll receive a unique referral link. Share this link with individuals or businesses that could benefit from Bright Data's services.

2. **Earn Commissions**: When someone signs up through your link and subscribes to a paid plan, you earn a commission. Specifically, you'll receive a 50% revenue share, up to \$2,500 per customer.

3. **Receive Account Credits**: Alternatively, you can opt to receive credits to your Bright Data account, with an additional 5% bonus when selecting this option.

**Frequently Asked Questions**

* **How does the Bright Data referral program work?**
  Our referral program allows you to earn by sharing Bright Data with others. Share your unique referral link; when someone you refer signs up and spends \$100, you’ll earn a \$100 credit. Additionally, you can earn a 50% revenue share, up to \$2,500 per customer.

* **Where can I find my referral code?**
  After signing up for the referral program, your unique referral code will be available in your account dashboard. You can copy and share it with potential Bright Data users.

* **How do I earn money with the referral program?**
  You earn in two ways:

  * A \$100 credit when a referred customer spends \$100.

  * A 50% revenue share, up to \$2,500 per customer, as they continue to use Bright Data.

* **Can I track my referral earnings?**
  Yes, you can monitor your referral progress and earnings through your Bright Data account dashboard.

For more information or to join the referral program, visit our [referral page](https://brightdata.com/referral/).

**Referral Program vs. Affiliate Program – What’s the Difference?**
If you’re wondering whether this is the same as Bright Data’s affiliate program – it’s not. Here’s the key distinction:

* **Referral Program**: Ideal for existing users who want to recommend Bright Data to colleagues, friends, or businesses. You earn by directly inviting others to sign up.

* **Affiliate Program**: A broader approach suited for marketers, bloggers, and influencers who promote Bright Data to larger audiences through content, ads, or websites.

If you’re interested in the affiliate program, [click here to learn more](https://brightdata.com/affiliate).
