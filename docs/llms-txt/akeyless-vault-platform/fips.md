# FIPS Certification

Federal Information Processing Standards (FIPS) Certification

Akeyless Platform uses a certified cryptographic module (when [FIPS mode](https://docs.akeyless.io/docs/fips-mode) is enabled on the Gateway), according to National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) FIPS 140-2 through the Cryptographic Module Validation Program (CMVP).
