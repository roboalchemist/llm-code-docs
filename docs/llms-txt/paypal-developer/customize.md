# Customize Subscriptions

The following table lists all the ways you can customize the Subscriptions API.

| Capabilities | What you get |
| --- | --- |
| [Pricing plans](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/pricing-plans/) | Set up a fixed price or quantity-based (user or seat) plan. You can also configure enhanced pricing models by setting up volume based and tiered-based plans. |
| [Billing cycles](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/billing-cycles/) | Set the frequency that the user is charged. Choose the frequency of the billing cycle and if the billing continues for a fixed time period or until the user cancels the subscription. |
| [Pause or resume a subscription](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/pause-resume/) | Pause or soft-cancel a customerâ€™s subscription, and easily resume or reactivate it at any time. |
| [Upgrade or downgrade a subscription](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/revise-subscriptions/) | Give your customers the flexibility to change their subscription plan. |
| [Change subscription quantity](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/change-quantity/) | Give your customers the flexibility to change the number of units for seat-based or quantity-based subscriptions. |
| [Start a subscription on a future date](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/future-date/) | Allow customers to start their subscription on a future date. |
| [Offer a trial period](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/trial-period/) | Offer a trial period to let subscribers try your product at a free or discounted price before regular billing cycles start. |
| [Charge a setup fee](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/one-time-fee/) | Charge the subscriber a one-time setup fee before the subscription begins, typically for items that aren't part of the regular subscription billing like setup or onboarding. |
| [Payment failures and recovering balances](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/payment-failure-retry/) | PayPal attempts to capture failed payments automatically, and you can also set failure thresholds that suspend unpaid subscriptions. |
| [Multiple subscription buttons](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/multiple-buttons-website/) | Offer more than one type of subscription on a single page of your website. |
| [Update plan pricing](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/update-plan-pricing/) | Modify subscription pricing for all subscribers at once. |
| [Sell on social media](/docs/business/subscriptions/customize/sell-on-social/) | Accept subscriptions on social media platforms. |
