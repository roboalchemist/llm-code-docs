# 3rd-party plugins, add-ons, and extensions

If your business uses one of the following systems, you can connect PayPal or Braintree payments to your system with a no-code plugin. If you have questions, [email us](mailto:braintreepaypalplugins@paypal.com).

With this plugin, you can:

- Accept credit cards, PayPal, Venmo, Bancontact, EPS, giropay, iDEAL, Klarna, MyBank, and P24.

- Offer credit options such as Buy Now, Pay Later 1.

You must have a Salesforce account.

## **Hyperwallet Mirakl Connector**

The Hyperwallet Mirakl Connector (HMC) is a self-hosted service that links a Mirakl marketplace solution and the Hyperwallet payout platform.

With this connector, you can:

- Payout to sellers and operators.
- Replicate seller and payee accounts.
- Carry out KYC and payee verification.
- Update Hyperwallet statuses in Mirakl.
- Monitor email notifications for statuses and errors.

You must have a Mirakl instance.

[Go to GitHub repository](https://github.com/paypal/mirakl-hyperwallet-connector)
