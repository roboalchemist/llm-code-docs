# Search transactions on the reports page

You can log into your PayPal business account and use the Reports page to search for payouts transactions.

## Transaction finder

To search for payouts transactions:

1. Log in to your [PayPal business account](https://www.paypal.com).
2. Under Activity, select **All Reports**.
3. Select **Activity download**, and then click the magnifying glass/search icon.
4. Enter an optional keyword for the payment and set the following:
   - Under Transaction type, select **Mass payments**.
   - Under Date range, choose the desired time frame.
   - Under Currency, choose the desired currency.
5. **Click Advanced options** to further reduce your search criteria, otherwise click **Search**.
6. You can now view the search criteria and click **Download report**.

The Search Transaction Results list displays this information for transactions that match your search criteria:

- Date
- Type
- Name
- Subject
- Gross Amount
- Fees
- Net Amount

Within this list, you can perform these actions:

- Sort the transactions by **Name** or **Gross Amount**.
- Click **Print** to print a copy.
- Click **Download** and select a file type to save the list to a file.

## Activities download report

You can create and download detailed reports of all your transaction activity for any time period in the last three years.

To create the Activities download report:

1. Log in to your [PayPal business account](https://www.paypal.com).
2. Under Activity, select **All Reports**.
3. Select **Activity download**.
4. Specify search criteria and click **Create Report**.
