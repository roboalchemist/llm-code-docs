# PayPal Developer Community Champions

## A community of experts and advocates

PayPal Champions is a global community of experts who enjoy creating and sharing their technical knowledge with others.

![championPicture](https://www.paypalobjects.com/ppdevdocs/PPChamoions/ChampionsHero.png)

## What is a PayPal Champion?

A PayPal Champion is an expert who goes above and beyond to advocate for PayPal. As PayPal ambassadors, Champions provide content and thought leadership across communities and solutions. They help influence and mentor others to make the most of their PayPal experience. They are selected for their advocacy based on a number of factors, including:

- Creating content, such as blogs, videos, and white papers
- Advocating on social media
- Engaging in communities and forums
- Speaking engagements and more!

![What is a PayPal Champion?](https://www.paypalobjects.com/ppdevdocs/PPChamoions/ChampionsPato.png)

## Features

PayPal Champions are eligible for rewards and recognition, including:

- Online profiles in our Champions gallery
- Access to networking and engagement opportunities
- Invitations to PayPal events, including speaking and meetup opportunities
- Custom swag that identifies them as part of the Champions program
- Expert support/collaboration from our product and engineering teams
- Timely access to new products and features
