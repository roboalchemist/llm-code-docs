# Commerce Platforms

## Note:
Pay Later messaging requires a PayPal Checkout integration.

- To integrate PayPal Checkout, see our [documentation](/studio/checkout/standard/getstarted) or [video tutorial](/video/watch/?videoId=MBfJEUGNNs0).
- If you've already integrated PayPal Checkout, you can integrate Pay Later messaging.

## Add the button and messages for Pay Later offers. We dynamically show your customers the best offer for each purchase. Offers are included with PayPal Checkout, so there are no additional costs to you unless you are in the US.
