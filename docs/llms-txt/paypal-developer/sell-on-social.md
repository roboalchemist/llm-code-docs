# Sell on social media

You can accept subscriptions on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter by generating a weblink and sharing it with your customers. This is helpful if you donâ€™t have a website and want to accept subscription payments from your customers.

## Know before you begin

You'll need a [PayPal business account](https://www.paypal.com/bizsignup).

## Get subscription link

1. From your business account dashboard, go to [Manage subscriptions](https://www.paypal.com/billing).
2. Choose or create a subscription plan, and copy the link.
   
   **Note:** Subscription links are valid for 6 months.
   
3. Share the link on social media, email, or SMS.

**Step result**: You can view and manage subscriptions from the subscriptions dashboard.
