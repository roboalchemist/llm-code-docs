# Exercise 11.5: Updating a published skill to a new version

Once you publish a skill, you can make changes and upgrade your company skill store with the latest changes that you have made to your skill.  Your skill can be upgraded by as shown below:

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/tIS-Kli2vQLzGJ7Ixoaj4YEvb8RZRVDyI17Jj4OZHnS5OgjV0bFY57Uu99djrjGzu495XT9C-EZXO-OjFVY8g1NIpBY310b29Spi--KOs8FOFqHdUnfD28EyOf_gYqejSxF1gQti)

Click republish to the skill store, update the changes, and also change the version number to help users understand that you have changed/ updated your skill. &#x20;

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/Pmpj2n3TQuG892sJLD2Fo9pMX9g2O1pi3zTrM-l_f1SndllcXQZ0TVvF-xzza6kJ7v0NgKseW-GFPUI_shCk25bY15GFsXefPAHCSbcJXLWlhJsf_BpfkDZZ1qVmZYUO-AAOJ2f3)

Once you have made all the changes, you will be asked if you want to update your skill. Click Yes

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/tbmOp0x41FngHH3cedhEYypEWpu0u2C8E5O_uHd-q89StnErhZeG-UOtu6iW1ouy1jHLqExx2r6P4-d1xClgSklic0pgnz0zmR21QjRfJjXrvV1beFK29QkHHPsuOQFeSXsW3xA8)

Your changes will now be available in the company skill store.\ <br>

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/nUwQQwLYyh0vT17a-WWtqzvj2n8E9Ihd3A-thI1nwtbWSEJ6m6wlEBSz63gj5nAs68yStwI9kx3SKArS7kkUADwrw0-wdc-FHe24XCnQirpYuB346yEjfdlqznFbX2e85oZK-BmL)
