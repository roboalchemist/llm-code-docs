# Actions

The `Action` column on the page lists all ingested documents or articles and allows you to perform the following actions:

* **View:** Open and review the ingested article or document.
* **Edit:** Modify the attributes and preview URL assigned to the article or document.
* **Delete:** Remove an individual article or document from the job.
* **Reingest:** Reingest an article or document that was previously ingested or encountered an error during ingestion.
