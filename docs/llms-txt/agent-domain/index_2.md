<p class="leading-7 mb-4">Agents are shipping fast. They handle support, schedule meetings, negotiate deals, and coordinate across systems. But the ecosystem is fragmented — no shared naming, no discovery standard, no community governance.</p>
<p class="leading-7 mb-4">That&#x27;s a problem. Without common infrastructure, one platform will own the layer between agents and the internet. When that happens, everyone else plays by their rules.</p>
<p class="leading-7 mb-4"><strong>We&#x27;re building the alternative.</strong> An open, community-governed foundation for agent identity, discovery, and trust.</p>
