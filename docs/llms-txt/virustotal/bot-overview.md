# VT Bot

# Overview

***

If you ever need help with VirusTotal Intelligence or our documentation, you can always use our VT Bot.
VT Bot is a helpful AI assistant that can answer your questions and help you find the information you need.
To access the bot, click on the chat bubble in the bottom right corner of any VirusTotal page.

![VT Bot Bubble](https://storage.googleapis.com/vtdocresources/guides/vt-bot/img/vt-bot-bubble.png)

You can ask questions about anything related to VirusTotal Intelligence or our documentation, and it will provide you with helpful information and resources.

![VT Bot Answer](https://storage.googleapis.com/vtdocresources/guides/vt-bot/img/vt-bot-answer.png)

You can also click on some of the convenience shortcuts and optionally provide feedback about the answer if you wish to help us improve the bot.

![VT Bot Dialog](https://storage.googleapis.com/vtdocresources/guides/vt-bot/img/vt-bot-widget.png)

# Limitations

***

The bot's primary focus is to answer your questions based on the knowledge within our documentation. While it can be seen as an enhanced search engine using natural language, it is important to note that its knowledge is limited to the information contained within our documents. Therefore, it may not have access to general knowledge or understand the broader context of your current query or the specific page you are viewing.
