# Contributors

VirusTotal is a free service developed by a team of devoted engineers who are independent of any ICT security entity. VirusTotal is an information aggregator: the data we present is the combined output of different antivirus products, file and website characterization tools, website scanning engines and datasets, and user contributions.

This page acknowledges all companies and individuals that have integrated a product, tool or resource with VirusTotal, or who have contributed in other ways. VirusTotal is not tied to any of these companies or individuals in any way, so the results provided are not subject to bias.

VirusTotal is not responsible for false positives generated by any of the resources it uses. False positive issues should be addressed directly with the company or individual behind the product under consideration. In this article you can find a [list of false positive contacts](https://virustotal.readme.io/docs/false-positive-contacts).
