One of the search modifiers available in [VirusTotal Intelligence](https://virustotal.readme.io/docs/file-search-modifiers) is **behaviour\_tags** or **behaviour\_tag** . This modifier will search for files tagged with the literal provided due to their behaviour while being executed in our sandboxes.
