# Get a Provider Account

Developers and service providers who intend to publish their API products on the Platform require an API provider account.

Sign up for the API7 Provider Portal and get a 30-day free trial of the Essential Plan. During these 30 days, you can choose to renew the Essential Plan or upgrade to a higher version of the plan at any time.

This tutorial will guide you through the process of setting up your provider account, and by following the instructions provided you will be able to begin your journey.

## Prerequisite(s)[â](#prerequisites "Direct link to Prerequisite(s)")

1. An active email address
2. A password between 8 and 16 characters that contains at least three of the following characters: lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, or special characters

## Sign Up for an Account[â](#sign-up-for-an-account "Direct link to Sign Up for an Account")

1. Go to the login page and click **Sign Up**.

![login](https://static.api7.ai/uploads/2023/08/28/3GQEtI34_iShot_2023-08-28_16.44.10.png)

2. Fill in your name, email and password in the form. All fields are required. Check the box to agree to the agreement and click **Sign Up**.

![sign-up](https://static.api7.ai/uploads/2023/08/28/VvzyrjY0_sign-up.png)

3. Check the verification email in your mailbox, click **Verify Your Email** to verify your email.

![verify-email](https://static.api7.ai/uploads/2023/08/28/ht0aphwt_verify-email.png)

4. After successful email verification, your 30 days Essential Plan free trial starts now. Enter your account and password on the login page to sign in.
