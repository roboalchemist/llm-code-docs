# Renew License

API7.ai provides a [30-day free trial license](https://api7.ai/try?product=enterprise) for API7 Enterprise. The license allows you to configure features, capacity limits, and support terms for the API management platform.

Licenses have an expiration date based on the terms of the purchase. Ensure to renew licenses before expiry to avoid service disruptions and loss of access to features. The API7 Enterprise Dashboard will show a renewal notice as the expiration date approaches.

[Contact API7.ai experts](https://api7.ai/contact) to renew your license.

To upload a new license, follow these steps:

1. Select **Organization** from the top navigation bar, and then select **License**.
2. Click the **Edit** icon.
3. Select the new license that you want to upload and then click **Upload**.
4. Confirm the new expiry date and then click **Update**.
