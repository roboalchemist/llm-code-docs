# Session Management

1. [Launch Configurations](https://docs.warp.dev/terminal/sessions/launch-configurations) supports saving a configuration of windows, tabs, and panes to open later.
2. [Session Navigation](https://docs.warp.dev/terminal/sessions/session-navigation) enables you to easily navigate to any session in Warp.
3. [Session Restoration](https://docs.warp.dev/terminal/sessions/session-restoration) automatically restores the window and tabs from your previous session.
