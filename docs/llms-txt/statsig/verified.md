# Verified Metrics

> Mark trusted, curated metrics as verified to help teams identify reliable metrics for experiments.

<Info>This is an Enterprise feature, please reach out to our support team, your sales contact, or via our [Slack community](https://statsig.com/slack) if you want this enabled.</Info>

Verified metrics are a way to identify metrics that are curated by your company and known to be trustworthy. When users set up experiments and search for metrics, they'll see a verified icon next to verified metrics.
