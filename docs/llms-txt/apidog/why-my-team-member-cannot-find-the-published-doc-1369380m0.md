# Why my team member cannot find the published doc?

#### Q: My team member says the “Share Docs” section is empty, even though I’ve published a document. Why is this happening?

**A:** In Apidog, only team members with Admin permissions on a project can view published documents under Share Docs.

If a member is assigned as Editor or Read-only, the published document will not be visible to them — the section will appear empty.

To allow them to see the published document, you’ll need to change their role to Admin in the project settings. After the change of role has made, ask the user to refresh Apidog and can find the published doc under Share Docs.

<TipInfo>Be mindful when granting Admin permissions, as it also provides full control over the project’s settings and resources.</TipInfo>

If the guided steps does not resolve your issue please reach out to [Apidog Support Center](https://docs.apidog.com/apidog-support-center-748035m0.md)
