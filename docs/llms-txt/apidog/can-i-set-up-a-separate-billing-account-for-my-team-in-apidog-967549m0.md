# Can I set up a separate billing account for my team in Apidog?

### Q1: Can I create a separate billing account for my team in Apidog?
A: Currently, Apidog does not support separate billing accounts. All billing is handled through the account that manages the team or organization.


### Q2: How can someone else in my team generate an invoice ?
A: Your developer colleague can follow these steps to generate an invoice and share it with you for payment:

1. Log in to their Apidog account and select the relevant Team/Organization.

![Z11VbjSkPoTDyvxZ9ejcTY3BnXb.png](https://api.apidog.com/api/v1/projects/544525/resources/354134/image-preview)

2. Go to the Plans section and click Upgrade.

3. In the subscription plans section, click Click to upgrade.

4. Choose the desired plan, duration, and number of seats (e.g., 7).

5. Click Get Invoice to generate a Stripe invoice.

Share the invoice with you for online payment using your company credit card.

### Q3: Can I pay for the plan using a company credit card even if someone else generates the invoice?
A: Yes! Once your colleague shares the Stripe invoice with you, you can pay it directly using your company credit card.

#### Q4: Who can I contact if we run into any issues during the process?
A: You can contact the Apidog support team at any time—we’re happy to help guide you or your team through the setup.[Apidog Support Center](https://docs.apidog.com/apidog-support-center-748035m0.md)
