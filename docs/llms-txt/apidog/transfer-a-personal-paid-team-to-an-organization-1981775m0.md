# Transfer a Personal Paid Team to an Organization

If you accidentally created a **paid team under your personal account** and want to move it to an **Organization**, you can do so using the **Transfer In** feature.

This process will transfer **both the team and its subscription** to the target organization.



### How to Transfer a Personal Team to an Organization

1. Make sure you are the **Owner** of the target Organization.
2. Go to **Organization → Teams**.
3. Click **Transfer In** in the top-right corner.
4. Select the personal team you want to transfer.
5. Confirm the transfer.

Once completed, the team and its paid plan will belong to the Organization.



### Important Notes Before Transferring

⚠️ **Only one subscription is allowed per Organization**

* An Organization **cannot have multiple subscriptions at the same time**.
* If the Organization already has:

  * a trial subscription, or
  * another active paid subscription
    you must cancel or remove it **before** performing the transfer.

If multiple subscriptions exist, the transfer may fail with errors such as **“Server error”** or **transfer not allowed**.



### Transfer Failed? Try These Steps

If the transfer fails even though the setup looks correct:

* Refresh the page and try again
* Wait a few minutes and retry (backend changes may take time to take effect)
* Try performing the transfer using the **Apidog Web App**
* Ensure you are logged in as the **Organization Owner**

If the issue persists, please contact support with:

* Team ID
* Organization ID
* Screenshots of the error message



### Common Scenarios

**Q: I created a team as a personal team by mistake. Can I move it to my organization?**
Yes. Use **Transfer In** to move the team and its subscription to the Organization.

**Q: Will the paid plan be transferred as well?**
Yes. The subscription is transferred automatically together with the team.

**Q: Why does the transfer fail even though I followed the steps?**
Most failures are caused by the Organization already having another subscription (trial or paid).
