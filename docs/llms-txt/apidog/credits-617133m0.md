# Credits

Apidog Credits are a reward system that allows you to earn credits through invitations and use them to reduce subscription costs.

## Earning Credits Through Invitations

Invite new users to register and receive **$10 Credit** per successful registration. There is no limit to the number of credits you can earn.

### Method 1: Team or Project Invitation

Invite new users to join your team or project. When they successfully register, you receive a $10 Credit.

### Method 2: Referral Invitations

Referral invitations are independent of your team or project. Invited users simply need to register to earn you credits.

**How to send referral invitations:**
1. Navigate to **Account Settings** → **Referrals and Credits**
2. Share your referral link with potential users

## Credit Management

### Credit Rules

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Usage** | Credits can only be used to upgrade team plans (not withdrawable) |
| **Transfer** | Credits can be contributed to teams but cannot be transferred back to personal accounts |
| **Expiration** | Credits expire at the end of the third calendar year after acquisition (applies to both personal and team credits) |
| **History** | View credit acquisition and spending history in **Account Settings** → **Referrals and Credits** |

## Using Credits

### Contributing Credits to Teams

<Background>
![image.png](https://api.apidog.com/api/v1/projects/544525/resources/372033/image-preview)
</Background>
Team members can contribute their personal credits to the team, allowing administrators to use them as deductions during payment.

### Paying with Credits
Team managers can prioritize the use of credits when paying for subscription plans. After the 14-day trial ends, payment is automatically processed and any existing credits in your team are automatically deducted. No additional action is needed.

:::info[]
Currently, using credits for organization subscription is not supported.
:::
