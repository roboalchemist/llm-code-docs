# Moving Paid Teams to Organizations

Moving a paid team into an organization requires careful consideration of billing and subscription management. This guide answers common questions about the process.

## Moving Paid Teams to Paid Organizations

### Can I move a paid team into a paid organization?

No, paid teams cannot be directly moved into a paid organization due to billing conflicts. You must downgrade the team to the free plan first.

### How do I move my paid team into a paid organization?

**Process:**
1. **Downgrade the team to the free plan** (requires canceling the current subscription)
2. Contact Apidog support to request downgrade and prorated refund for unused time
3. Once downgraded, move the team into the organization

### What information do I need to provide to support?

When contacting support, provide:
- **Team ID** (e.g., `123456`)
- **Organization ID** (e.g., `123456`)
- **Preferred resolution**: Downgrade + Refund or billing consolidation

### Will I get a refund if I downgrade?

Yes, depending on the billing cycle and terms, Apidog will issue a refund based on unused time.

### What happens to my data if I downgrade?

When you downgrade or unsubscribe from a paid plan:
- **Data remains intact**: All existing data (APIs, projects, etc.) is preserved
- **Feature restrictions**: Some features may become restricted based on your new plan

### Should I backup my data before downgrading?

It's recommended to:
- Check your current usage
- Back up critical data if you're unsure about the new plan's restrictions

## Moving Paid Teams to Free Organizations

### Can I transfer a paid team to a free organization?

Yes, you can transfer **one** paid team to a free organization. The organization's plan status will be updated according to the transferred team's plan.

### What role do I need to transfer a paid team?

You must be the **owner of both the team and the organization** to perform the transfer operation.

## Self-Service vs. Support Assistance

### Can I do this myself, or do I need support?

| Scenario | Self-Service | Support Required |
|----------|--------------|------------------|
| Transfer free team to paid organization | ✅ Yes (if you're the owner of both) | ❌ No |
| Transfer paid team to free organization | ✅ Yes (if you're the owner of both) | ❌ No |
| Downgrade paid team | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |

**For downgrades requiring manual handling:**  
Email Apidog Support with the required details at the [Apidog Support Center](https://docs.apidog.com/apidog-support-center-748035m0.md).
