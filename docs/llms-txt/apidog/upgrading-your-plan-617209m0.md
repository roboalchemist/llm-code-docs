# Upgrading Your Plan

Team administrators can upgrade their plan to access advanced features and capacity. When you upgrade, you automatically get a **14-day free trial** of premium features—no separate trial activation required. You only need to provide payment information to start the trial; billing begins after the 14-day period ends.

## Prerequisites

- Team administrator role
- Access to the Apidog application

## Upgrading Your Plan with 14-Day Free Trial

When you upgrade to a paid plan, you receive a 14-day free trial period. Your billing starts after the trial ends, so you can explore premium features risk-free.

### Step 1: Access the Plans Page

Click **Upgrade** in the top-right corner to navigate to the Plans page, where you can view current capacity usage.

:::tip[]
Team members can contribute their personal credits to the team to reduce plan billing costs.
:::

<Background>
![Upgrade Plan Step 1.png](https://api.apidog.com/api/v1/projects/544525/resources/370900/image-preview)
</Background>

### Step 2: Select Your Plan

Click **Upgrade** or **Manage Plan** to view and select your desired plan.

<Background>
![Upgrade Plan Step 1.5.png](https://api.apidog.com/api/v1/projects/544525/resources/370901/image-preview)
</Background>

### Step 3: Provide Payment Information

After selecting your plan, click **Start 14-day free trial** or **Upgrade** to proceed to the Stripe payment page.

<Background>
![Upgrade Plan Step 2.jpeg](https://api.apidog.com/api/v1/projects/544525/resources/370908/image-preview)
</Background>

Fill in your credit card information on the secure payment page. Verify that all payment details are correct before proceeding.

:::important[]
No charge will be made during the 14-day free trial. Your card will only be charged after the trial period ends.
:::

### Step 4: Confirmation

After providing your payment information:
- You'll be redirected to the Plans page
- The page will display your new plan's capacity
- Your 14-day free trial begins immediately
- An email notification with the subject **"Subscription Successful"** will be sent to the team administrator


:::tip[]
Team admins can cancel the subscription anytime during the 14-day free trial. Canceling during the trial period means you keep access until the trial ends, but won't be charged.
:::
