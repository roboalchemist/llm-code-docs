# Comments

In Apidog, every endpoint supports comments, allowing team members to discuss and collaborate directly on the endpoint documentation.

## Opening Comments

To view or add comments, simply open any endpoint and click the **"Comments"** icon in the upper right corner. The comment panel will slide out from the side of the page, making it easy to check existing discussions or post new feedback.

## Adding Comments & @ Mentions

In comments, you can use `@` to mention project members. Once posted, the mentioned members will receive a notification, including **email** and **inbox**.

<Background>
<img style="width: 460px;" src="https://api.apidog.com/api/v1/projects/544525/resources/361449/image-preview" />
</Background>

:::important
Email and inbox notifications need to be enabled in **Settings** > **Notifications** (top right corner). Otherwise, members won't receive related alerts.
:::

## Image Upload Support

You can insert images directly in comments to provide additional details, such as request examples, screenshots, or error logs.

**How to upload:** Click the image icon in the **comments** input box and select a local image, or simply copy and paste the image into the input box. Images can be clicked to enlarge.

## Comment Filtering

When there are many comments, use the filter feature to quickly find what you need:

- **Open**: Shows only comments that have not yet been addressed.
- **Resolved**: Shows comments marked as resolved, making it easy to review past discussions.
