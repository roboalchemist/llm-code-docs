# July 31, 2025 Notice

### **Model Decommissioning Notice**

To optimize resources and enhance the overall user experience, Novita will decommission the **Stable Diffusion 3  API** on **August 15, 2025 (UTC 0:00).**

The API will be inaccessible after this date. Please update your applications before then to prevent service interruptions.
