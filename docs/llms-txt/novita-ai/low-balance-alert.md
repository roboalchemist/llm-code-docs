# Low Balance Alert

To prevent service interruption due to insufficient balance, you can enable a low balance alert.

Once set, if your account balance drops below your designated threshold, the system will notify you via **Email** or **Inbox** to ensure continuous service.

<Note>
  The alert will be sent at most once per day (from 00:00 to 24:00 UTC+0), and notifications will stop once your balance rises above the set threshold.
</Note>

## How to Set Up

1. Go to the [Low Balance Alert page](https://novita.ai/billing/balance-warning) and click "Add Alerts".

2. In the pop-up dialog, enter your desired warning amount, enable the "Active" status, and click "Confirm".

* The warning amount must be between \$1 and \$1,000.
* The interval between amounts with an active warning status must be at least \$5.
