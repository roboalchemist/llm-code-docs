# April 04, 2025 Product Updates

## **New Features**

* **Added Support for Hugging Face Login**

  Users can now sign in using their Hugging Face account. Social login now supports Hugging Face in addition to Google and GitHub.

  Updated time: April 02, 2025
* **Added Qwen2.5 VL 72B Instruct to the Model Library**

  Qwen2.5 VL 72B Instruct is the latest high-performance multimodal large language model launched by Novita.

  Featuring enhanced visual comprehension capabilities, it also demonstrates strong performance in complex reasoning and decision-making tasks.

  Updated time: March 31, 2025
* **Added Gemma-3-27b-it to the Model Library**

  Gemma-3-27b-it is the latest high-performance multimodal large language model launched by Novita.

  Built on Gemini 2.0 architecture, it possesses cross-modal reasoning capabilities across text, images, and short videos.

  Updated time: March 31, 2025
