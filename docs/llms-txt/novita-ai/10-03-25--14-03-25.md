# March 14, 2025 Product Updates

## **New Features**

* **Embedding Model-BAAI: BGE-M3 is Added to Model Library**

  Novita supports the `baai/bge-m3` text embedding model. This model is capable of simultaneously achieving three core functionalities: dense retrieval, multi-vector retrieval, and sparse retrieval. Designed to be compatible with over 100 languages, it supports a variety of input formats, ranging from short sentences to long texts of up to 8,192 tokens.

  For detailed usage instructions, please refer to the API documentation: [Model-BAAI: BGE-M3](https://novita.ai/docs/api-reference/model-apis-llm-create-embeddings)

  Updated time: March 13, 2025
* **Login Security: Limit on Incorrect Password Attempts**

  To enhance account security, a login attempt limit has been implemented: Maximum 5 incorrect passwords within 1 hour.

  Updated time: March 11, 2025

## **Improvements**

* **Pricing Update for Wan 2.1 T2V Video Model**

  Optimized pricing strategy for video generation models: The pricing for all resolutions is now standardized at \$0.08 per second.

  Updated time: March 10, 2025
