# March 26, 2025 Notice

## Platform Service Adjustment Notice

### 1. Model Decommissioning Notice

To continuously enhance platform service efficiency, focus on core product capability upgrades, and deliver more advanced, high-quality, and standardized services, Novita platform will decommission certain large language models (LLMs) on April 3, 2025(UTC 0:00). The list of models scheduled for removal is as follows:

* teknium/openhermes-2.5-mistral-7b
* qwen/qwen-2-7b-instruct
* nousresearch/nous-hermes-llama2-13b
* qwen/qwen-2-vl-72b-instruct
* openchat/openchat-7b
