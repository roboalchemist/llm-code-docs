# June 20, 2025 Product Updates

## **Model APIs Updates**

### **New Features**

* **MiniMax-Hailuo-02 Now Available on Novita**

  Novita now supports MiniMax’s latest high-performance video generation model — **Hailuo 02**.

  Hailuo 02 delivers industry-leading instruction-following capabilities and excels at generating videos in complex physical scenarios, including high-intensity stunt simulations. It supports ultra-high-definition video generation, with:

  * Up to **6 seconds** at **768P or 1080P**
  * Up to **10 seconds** at **768P**

  Explore more creative possibilities with one of the most advanced video models available.

  Get started now: [**MinMax-Hailuo-02**](https://novita.ai/models/image#minimax-hailuo-02)
