# Payment Methods

Novita AI uses **Stripe** for all payment processing.

<Tip>All your payment information is securely stored by Stripe, and we <u>do not store</u> any payment information.</Tip>

You can manually top up a specified amount of credit, which must be greater than \$10. Additionally, you can enable the [Automatic Top-Up](/guides/auto-top-up), which will automatically add credit if your balance falls below a specified threshold.
