# Nov 3, 2025 Product Updates

**Purpose**

Storage-related costs for Agent Sandbox have continued to increase, and the platform can no longer maintain free storage usage.To ensure sustainable operations and more efficient resource allocation, the Agent Sandbox Storage Billing feature has been launched.

**Billing Scope**

* Storage space used by Sandbox Templates
* Files and data retained when a Sandbox is paused

**Free Period**

A half-month free period is provided until November 17, 2025 (UTC).

During this period, users will not be charged and can check their usage at:

[https://novita.ai/sandbox-console/usage](https://novita.ai/sandbox-console/usage)

**Next Steps**

After the free period, storage billing will take effect. Users will be notified in advance via in-app messages or email.
