# LobeChat

> Integrate Novita AI with LobeChat to build advanced AI apps with multimodal capabilities, enhanced privacy, and customizable features.

With the **Novita AI & LobeChat integration**, you gain seamless access to Novita AI’s comprehensive suite of LLM models, including Llama, DeepSeek, and more. This integration enables you to create and deploy advanced AI applications tailored to your needs. Whether it’s customer service, content creation, or personal assistance, LobeChat, and Novita AI provide the tools to build intelligent, dynamic, and privacy-focused AI solutions.

This guide will walk you through integrating Novita AI’s LLMs with the LobeChat platform, enabling you to deploy AI applications with advanced capabilities like multimodal interaction, context-aware reasoning, and extensible plugins.

## **What Is LobeChat?**

LobeChat is an open-source, modern UI/Framework designed for ChatGPT and other LLMs. It simplifies the development of AI chat applications by supporting speech synthesis, multi-modal inputs, and an extensible plugin ecosystem. With LobeChat, you can deploy your private chat application using models like OpenAI’s ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Novita AI’s LLMs in just one click.

## **Prerequisites**

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

* **Novita AI LLM API Key**:
  * Visit [Novita AI’s website](https://novita.ai/) and create an account.
  * After logging in, go to the [**Key Management**](https://novita.ai/settings/key-management) page to generate your **API Key**. This key is required to connect Novita AI’s models to LobeChat.

* **LobeChat Account**
  * Sign up for a LobeChat account at [LobeChat](https://lobechat.com).

### **Step 1: Connect Novita AI to LobeChat**

To connect Novita AI’s models with LobeChat:

* Log in to your **LobeChat account** and access the [**Settings**](https://lobechat.com/settings/common).
* Navigate to the **AI Service Provider** tab.
* Scroll down to find the **Novita AI** section and expand it.
* Paste your **Novita AI API Key** into the provided field and click **Save**.

### **Step 2:** Select a Novita AI Model

Once the API Key is configured, you can select a Novita AI LLM model to power your AI application:
