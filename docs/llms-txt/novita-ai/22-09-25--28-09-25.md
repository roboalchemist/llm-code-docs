  Qwen3-VL—the most powerful vision-language model in the Qwen series to date. Novita offers world-first support!

  Updated time: September 24, 2025
* **qwen/qwen3-vl-235b-a22b-instruct Model Release**

  Qwen3-VL—the most powerful vision-language model in the Qwen series to date. Novita offers world-first support!

  Updated time: September 24, 2025
* **minimax-hailuo-02 First-last Frame**

  First-Last Frame is a video generation technology introduced by Hailuo AI. By specifying the start and end images (the first and last frames), it automatically generates dynamic transition videos in between. The technology supports complex instruction execution and delivers cinematic-quality effects, making it suitable for applications such as e-commerce advertising, one-click outfit changes, and more.

  Updated time: September 26, 2025
* **Seedance V1 Lite/Pro**

  Seedance 1.0 is the latest video generation foundational model launched by ByteDance's Doubao AI team, featuring both lite and pro versions. Its capabilities and pricing are fully aligned with the official website.
