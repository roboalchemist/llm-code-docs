# December 19, 2024

The **Team** feature is now available.

**With Team, you can:**

* Convert your personal account into a team account or join an existing team to collaborate with others;
* Invite other users to join your team, and share the team's resources with them;
* Manage your members' permissions to the team's resources by assigning different roles;
* Analyze usage data for each team member.

For more information, please visit: [Team Documentation](/guides/team).
