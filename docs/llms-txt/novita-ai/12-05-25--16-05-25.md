# May 16, 2025 Product Updates

## **Model APIs Updates**

### **Improvements**

* **Hunyuan Video Fast Now 67% Cheaper**

  We’ve significantly reduced the pricing for the Hunyuan Video Fast model — **now only one-third of the original price**. Enjoy high-quality video generation at a much lower cost.

  Updated time: May 13, 2025

***

## **GPUs Updates**

### **New Features**

* **Compute Capacity Expanded to Cover All Major Continents**

  Our global compute footprint continues to grow. Leveraging a distributed resource pool, we now offer GPU services across all major continents—empowering customers worldwide.

  **New Regions:**

  1. **Middle East:** United Arab Emirates (AS-AE-01)
  2. **Europe:** United Kingdom (EU-GB-01)
  3. **South Asia:** India (AS-IN-01)
  4. **South America:** Brazil (SA-BR-01)

  Updated time: May 13, 2025

### **Improvements**

* **Explore & Instances Interface Enhancements**

  We’ve refined both the Explore and Instances pages to surface more information and streamline interactions:

  1. Display architecture names for H100, H200, and A100 GPU card types
  2. On the Explore page, display the maximum GPUs supported per server as "\[card count] max" and streamline the per-instance GPU selector.
  3. On the Instances page, display the number of GPUs configured for each instance

  Updated time: May 15, 2025
