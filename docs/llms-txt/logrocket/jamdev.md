# Jam.dev

Integrating LogRocket with [Jam](https://jam.dev/)

Create complete bug reports with LogRocket session data directly from your web app, simply by recording your screen with Jam's browser extension. Jam auto-includes all the technical context developers need to debug.

<Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/271e978-LogRocket_docs_lead_image.png" />

Every bug report you create with Jam automatically includes the LogRocket session recording. You'll find the link to the LogRocket session in the info panel in Jam logs.

<Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/762e871-LogRocket_docs_integration_highlight.png" />

You don't have to connect anything to set started with the Jam + LogRocket integration. The URL to the LogRocket session recording is automatically included every time you report a bug with Jam.

Here's how to log bugs with Jam:

1. **Install the Jam extension.** Get the browser extension at [jam.dev](http://jam.dev) . Pin the extension to your browser for easy access.

   <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/6fd40fe-Step_1_GIF.gif" />
2. **Click on Jam when you need to report a bug.** Choose your screen recording method -- screenshot, video, or instant replay (the last 30 seconds of what just happened). Draft your bug report, and hit "Create"!

   <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/da218f4-Step_2_Take_2.gif" />
3. **Share the Jam with developers.** Your newly created Jam will open automatically in a new tab. To share your Jam, copy the link and send it to your teammates.

   <Image align="center" src="https://files.readme.io/515268e-Step_3_img.png" />
