# Comparisons

LogRocket provides a comprehensive understanding of the user experience in your websites and native mobile applications. With a single line of code, we help you surface user struggle, optimize KPIs like conversion and adoption, and solve user-reported issues.

User experiences have become the latest differentiator for buyers - did you know that 80% of customers think the experience a business provides is as important as its goods and services?

And while there's plenty of testing you can do before a release, your product can (and will) do unexpected things when run in the wild, and users are often slow to provide feedback. Instead, they just leave.

There's no tool quite like LogRocket on the market when it comes to understanding and improving digital experiences. But parts of LogRocket overlap with a handful of established categories:

* [Session Replay & Digital Experience Analytics](https://docs.logrocket.com/docs/session-replay-digital-experience-analytics)
* [Product Analytics](https://docs.logrocket.com/docs/product-analytics)
* [Exception, Error & Issue Tracking](https://docs.logrocket.com/docs/exception-error-issue-tracking)
* [Application Performance Monitoring (APM)](https://docs.logrocket.com/docs/application-performance-monitoring-apm)
* [Mobile App Analytics](https://docs.logrocket.com/docs/mobile-app-analytics)
