# Plans Page

A breakdown of the information that can be found on your Plans page!

The Plans page can be found under Settings > Plans or by <Anchor label="following this link" target="_blank" href="https://app.logrocket.com/r/settings/plans">following this link</Anchor>

Your Plan details:

* **Web Session Quota:** the number of web sessions you can capture each month
* **Mobile Session Quota:** the number of mobile application sessions you can capture each month
* **Session Retention:** how long your session replays will be kept
* **Analytics Retention:** how long your session data will be kept
* **User Seats:** how many team members you can invite to join your LogRocket org
