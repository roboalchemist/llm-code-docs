# Enable Emailed Invoice Receipts

Automatically email successful invoice receipts to your Billing Email on file

* Visit Settings > Plans > "View Invoices" to toggle Invoice Receipts on/off
* Only users with **"Admin" permissions** are able to access the "View Invoices" link to enable this setting, or update the Billing Email field.
* After a successful payment is made, an invoice receipt will be emailed to the Billing Email on file
* Billing Email is specified at the bottom of the Plans page
* A list of your LogRocket Admins can be found on the Team Members page
