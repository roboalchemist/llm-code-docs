# Classic apps deprecation timeline adjustment

November 7, 2024

We want to update you about our [previous announcement](/changelog/2024-09-legacy-custom-bots-classic-apps-deprecation) regarding support for classic apps. After much consideration and feedback, we have decided to push back the deprecation date for classic apps to **March 31, 2026**.
