# Retiring of api.slack.com timeline adjustment

June 5, 2025

We want to update you about our [previous announcement](https://docs.slack.dev/changelog/2025/03/27/api-site-sunsetting) regarding the retirement of our old documentation experience on api.slack.com. We have moved the date from the end of **June 2025** to the end of **August 2025**.
