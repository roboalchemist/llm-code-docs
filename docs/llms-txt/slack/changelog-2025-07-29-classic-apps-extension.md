# Classic apps deprecation timeline adjustment

July 29, 2025

We want to update you about our [previous announcement](/changelog/2024-09-legacy-custom-bots-classic-apps-deprecation) regarding support for classic apps. After much consideration and feedback, we have decided to push back the deprecation date to **November 16, 2026**.
