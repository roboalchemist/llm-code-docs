# [TabBar](https://bryntum.com/docs/gantt/api/Core/widget/TabBar)

A special toolbar used by [TabPanel](https://bryntum.com/docs/gantt/api/#Core/widget/TabPanel) to present [tabs](https://bryntum.com/docs/gantt/api/#Core/widget/Tab) for the container's items.

The [items](https://bryntum.com/docs/gantt/api/#Core/widget/Container#config-items) of a tab bar are typically managed by the tab panel, however, items can be added that do not correspond to items in the tab panel. The [weight](https://bryntum.com/docs/gantt/api/#Core/widget/Widget#config-weight) config of each tab defaults to 0 or the weight of its corresponding item.
