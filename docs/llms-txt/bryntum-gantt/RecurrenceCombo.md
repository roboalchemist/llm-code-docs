# [RecurrenceCombo](https://bryntum.com/docs/gantt/api/Scheduler/view/recurrence/field/RecurrenceCombo)

A combobox field for selecting a recurrence pattern: `Daily`, `Weekly`, `Monthly` or `Yearly` if the recurrence has no other non-default settings, or `Custom...` if the recurrence has custom setting applied.
