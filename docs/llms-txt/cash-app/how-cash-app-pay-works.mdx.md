# How Cash App Pay Works

Cash App currently supports three main use cases:

* Mobile-Ecommerce
* Web-Ecommerce
* POS (Point-of-Sale)

<img src="https://files.buildwithfern.com/cash-app.docs.buildwithfern.com/2026-02-13T14:24:26.350Z/cash-api-docs/assets/images/how-cash-app-pay-works.png" alt="How Cash App Pay Works" noZoom />

<br />

# Mobile-Ecommerce

The Customer visits a mobile ecommerce app(or site) and uses their Cash App to pay for goods and services.

<img src="https://files.buildwithfern.com/cash-app.docs.buildwithfern.com/2026-02-13T14:24:26.350Z/cash-api-docs/assets/images/mobile-ecom-flow.png" alt="Ecommerce web workflow: choose a product, checkout, add shipping address, select paymnet method, link Cash App Pay, complete purchase" noZoom />

<br />

<br />

# Web-Ecommerce

The Customer visits a web-based ecommerce site and pays for items by scanning the QR code generated at checkout using Cash App on their phone.

<img src="https://files.buildwithfern.com/cash-app.docs.buildwithfern.com/2026-02-13T14:24:26.350Z/cash-api-docs/assets/images/ecom-web-flow.png" alt="Ecommerce web workflow: choose a product, checkout, scan a QR code, link Cash App Pay, complete purchase" noZoom />

<br />

<br />

# POS

Customer goes to a merchant and uses a Point-of-Sale (POS) device, for example, like the Square POS device.

<img src="https://files.buildwithfern.com/cash-app.docs.buildwithfern.com/2026-02-13T14:24:26.350Z/cash-api-docs/assets/images/pos-flow.png" alt="POS workflow: choose a product, checkout, scan a QR code, link Cash App Pay, complete purchase" noZoom />
