# Carrier partner list

## Learn which carriers work with us to deliver the goods to your customers.

When sending shipping information, you have to let us know the carrier that manages your deliveries. For more details about sending the carrier information, see the sections Adding shipping information and [Delivery tracking](https://docs.klarna.com/payments/after-payments/order-management/more-actions/delivery-tracking/).
