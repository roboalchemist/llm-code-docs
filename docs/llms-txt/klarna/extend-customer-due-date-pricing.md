# Extend payment date pricing

## Here you can find the fees for extending the payment due date, according to the purchase's country.

When you want to give your customers more time to pay for their purchase, you have to [extend the order's due date](https://docs.klarna.com/payments/after-payments/order-management/more-actions/extend-customer-due-date-pricing.md). The order extension increases the credit costs, so we charge a fee. The following table shows the countries where extending the customerâ€™s payment date is available and its corresponding fee. The options presented here might vary.
