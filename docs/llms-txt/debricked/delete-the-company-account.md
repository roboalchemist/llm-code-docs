# Delete company account

In order to delete your company account:

<figure><img src="https://1696666310-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FXrChfOBHOF0MXs8qC14m%2Fuploads%2F4juslJsPZJIoiMuc9hbv%2FScreenshot%202024-12-19%20111402.png?alt=media&#x26;token=a736b2bb-d553-4cf1-bb98-106c31447295" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Click **Admin tools** on the left side menu.
2. Type your password.
3. Go to the **Company settings** tab.
4. Click **Delete account** and confirm.

<figure><img src="https://1696666310-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FXrChfOBHOF0MXs8qC14m%2Fuploads%2FoAvDHNPbQx6pqxo4y0sL%2Fimage%20(1).png?alt=media&#x26;token=ec77048d-2ba5-4676-9cf1-a7aacd4e2c3f" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="warning" %}
**This will delete your company data, including all users connected to this account.**
{% endhint %}
