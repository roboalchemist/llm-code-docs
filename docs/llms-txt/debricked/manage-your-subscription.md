# Manage your subscription

### Cancel your subscription

If you are not satisfied with the current premium offering, you can [contact us](https://docs.debricked.com/overview/help).

To cancel your premium subscription:

1. Go to **Billing** on the left side menu.
2. Click your subscription.
3. Click **Cancel subscription**.
4. Enter the reason (if applicable) and click **Confirm cancelation**.

### Reactivate your account

If you previously canceled a subscription, you can reactivate it with just a few clicks.\
To do so:

1. Go to **Billing** on the left side menu.
2. Click your subscription.
3. Click **Reactivate subscription.**
