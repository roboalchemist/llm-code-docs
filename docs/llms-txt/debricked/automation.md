# Automation

In order to efficiently prevent vulnerabilities and unwanted licenses from entering your codebase, it is recommended to set up automation rules. The rules can be enforced in your pipelines, failing them or showing warnings, or set to notify relevant people, minimizing the number of vulnerabilities brought into your codebase.

You can see an overview of all automations in your organization by clicking the **Automations** on the left side menu. Here, you can [create new automation rules](https://docs.debricked.com/product/automation/create-an-automation-rule), as well as [edit or disable/delete](https://docs.debricked.com/product/automation/edit-an-automation-rule) the existing rules.
