# Celebrating the 2025 EMEA GitLab Partner Award Winners
<h2 id="recognising-excellence-gitlabs-2025-emea-partner-award-winners">Recognising Excellence: GitLab&rsquo;s 2025 EMEA Partner Award Winners</h2>
<p>Each year, GitLab celebrates its top-performing partners across the EMEA region, who demonstrate excellence in DevOps innovation, client impact, and collaborative partnership. The <strong>2025 EMEA GitLab Partner Awards</strong> reflect the commitment of these organisations to not only adopt but also maximise the value of the GitLab DevSecOps platform for their clients.</p>
<p>This year’s winners embody the spirit of transformation, guiding companies through their DevOps journeys, streamlining development lifecycles, and ensuring robust security and compliance. GitLab partners are key to driving growth, enabling digital transformation, and delivering true business agility.</p>
<p>The awards span four main categories:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Partner of the Year</strong>: Rewarding top performance overall.</li>
<li><strong>Commercial Partner of the Year</strong>: Recognising excellence in serving fast-growing and mid-market businesses.</li>
<li><strong>Rising Star</strong>: Honouring rapid growth and emerging market success.</li>
<li><strong>Technology Partner of the Year</strong>: Celebrating innovation and integration excellence.</li>
</ul>
<p>Each winner stood out by building deep GitLab expertise, showcasing customer success stories, and creating tailored solutions to meet unique regional and industry needs. Their dedication enhances the reach and effectiveness of GitLab’s all-in-one platform across multiple sectors.</p>
