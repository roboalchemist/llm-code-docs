# Solana Activation

> Activate Solana NFTs, and Tokens

### Add an Activation

After selecting `Solana` from the dropdown in the [`🔥 Manage Activations`](/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/manage-activations) menu, a new embed will appear:

### Remove an Activation

To remove a collection, go to the **Collection Setting Menu** and click the `🗑️ Remove A Collection` option

<Frame caption="Click on Remove an Activation">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_5c03a1.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN&q=85&s=3de02d0aa6da0765c9aec2c1eaebf2f0" width="991" height="870" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_5c03a1.png" />
</Frame>

Now select the collection you would like to remove from the dropdown menu

<Frame caption="Select the collection you want to remove">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_c3fb75.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN&q=85&s=2c7bd9f554a414fe09eafdb0b18dcd38" width="816" height="166" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_c3fb75.png" />
</Frame>

A new embed will appear, click on `🗑 Confirm Removal` to remove the collection. **This cannot be undone**!

<Frame caption="Click on Confirm Removal">
