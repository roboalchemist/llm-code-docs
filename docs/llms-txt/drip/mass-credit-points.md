# Mass Credit Points

> Mass Credit NFT's or users

The **Mass Credit** feature allows you to credit points to users or NFTs

## Credit Users

### Credit Specific Users

To credit specific users, use the `Credit users` button. Once you clicked the button, you will get a menu where you can select all the users you want to credit.

<Frame caption="Select The Users You Want To Credit">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/9R8wvri2DbuZkKko/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_89885c.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=9R8wvri2DbuZkKko&q=85&s=059bdcd5b7c450ab37949f22c7690adc" width="784" height="350" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_89885c.png" />
</Frame>

Once you have selected the users you want to credit, you will be presented with a form where you must enter the number of points you want to award to all selected members.

<Frame caption="Enter The Amount Of Points You Want To Credit">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/9R8wvri2DbuZkKko/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_8791d9.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=9R8wvri2DbuZkKko&q=85&s=0d890646adbf488f08c5269ca2cb24c9" width="872" height="562" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_8791d9.png" />
</Frame>

### Credit All Users

To credit all users, use the `Credit all users` button. Once you clicked the button, you will be presented with a form where you must enter the number of points you want to award to all members. Once you click `Submit`, it will credit the Points.

<Frame caption="Enter The Amount Of Points You Want To Credit">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/9R8wvri2DbuZkKko/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_14d0d3.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=9R8wvri2DbuZkKko&q=85&s=81fc21aee7d94352724aa41fd3f867fb" width="872" height="564" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_14d0d3.png" />
</Frame>

## Credit NFTs

### Credit Specific NFTs

To credit specific NFTs, use the `Credit list of NFTs` button. Once you have clicked on the button, you will get a form where you have to enter the number of points you want to credit and all the ids of the NFTs, separated by a comma.

<Frame caption="Enter The Amount Of Points You Want To Credit And The NFTs">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/9R8wvri2DbuZkKko/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_fd45a4.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=9R8wvri2DbuZkKko&q=85&s=27f7e1830c88b4eb54322f697dcaf983" width="870" height="720" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_fd45a4.png" />
</Frame>

### Credit All NFTs

To credit all NFTs, use the `Credit all NFTs` button. Once you have clicked the button, you will be presented with a form where you must enter the number of points you want to award to all NFTs. Once you click `Submit`, it will credit the Points.

<Frame caption="Enter The Amount Of Points You Want To Credit">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/9R8wvri2DbuZkKko/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_3ac493.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=9R8wvri2DbuZkKko&q=85&s=9072365249be05cd9f4cc2612d1ddecd" width="872" height="566" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/points-settings/img_3ac493.png" />
</Frame>

<Info>You can also use negative numbers to remove points from users</Info>
