# Insta-Quest

> Automate your quests (Pro Feature)

After selecting `⚡Insta-Quest` from the dropdown menu in [Quest Settings](/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings), a new embed will appear:

<Frame caption="Insta-Quest Menu">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/KyAgUkWQS8xerylE/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_fe4a2f.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=KyAgUkWQS8xerylE&q=85&s=16619810da845ed3f73fe50ed9d40fd3" width="498" height="251" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_fe4a2f.png" />
</Frame>

Click on `📍 Add Account`**. This will open a popup which you can input the Twitter account handle in.**

<Frame caption="Enter Twitter Account Handle">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/KyAgUkWQS8xerylE/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_fe3803.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=KyAgUkWQS8xerylE&q=85&s=9ea5147556cb9e64d356b0afb527e9ef" width="440" height="282" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_fe3803.png" />
</Frame>

Once you have the Twitter account added you will be greeted by a new embed.

<Frame caption="Insta-Quest Settings">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/9R8wvri2DbuZkKko/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_0e5ecc.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=9R8wvri2DbuZkKko&q=85&s=f3303b054f4b91bbce76495928985bd8" width="502" height="395" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_0e5ecc.png" />
</Frame>

Click on the `🔍 Set Filter` button to add specific words that will allow the bot to use Twitter posts as Insta-Quests. A form will appear for you to enter words to allow DRIP to pick up .

<Frame caption="Filter Quests to Specific Words">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/KyAgUkWQS8xerylE/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_e5f7bc.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=KyAgUkWQS8xerylE&q=85&s=b97320c1d42531aa96ff0f9f973d7143" width="440" height="282" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/quest-settings/img_e5f7bc.png" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Simply selecting a Channel and Template without adding a filter will post
  every Twitter post from your account as a quest.
</Warning>
