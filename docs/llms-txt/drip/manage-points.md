# Manage Points

> Lookup information about members in your community

Drip Rewards keeps various information about members of your community that you can access by clicking the `🔍 Manage Points` button.

<Frame caption="Click on the Manage Points button">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/ZY7HlxlZapkMV-Kp/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/img_11ad84.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=ZY7HlxlZapkMV-Kp&q=85&s=aa74bda15c3401e75926e4f8c18ed974" width="505" height="340" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/img_11ad84.png" />
</Frame>

After pressing the `🔍 Manage Points` button a new embed will appear

<Frame caption="Manage Points Embed">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/ZY7HlxlZapkMV-Kp/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/img_aa3e50.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=ZY7HlxlZapkMV-Kp&q=85&s=8d85af67e37abfb19e6b3273bb231e86" width="500" height="240" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/img_aa3e50.png" />
</Frame>

Choose a member you would like to lookup by selecting their username from the dropdown menu

<Frame caption="Select a username from the dropdown menu">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/ZY7HlxlZapkMV-Kp/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/img_2da083.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=ZY7HlxlZapkMV-Kp&q=85&s=88d6f16220247749fc81674356cb8f31" width="468" height="233" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/img_2da083.png" />
</Frame>

After selecting a member, information kept by Drip Rewards will appear in a new embed. You can add/remove points from this embed and select a new member to view as you wish
