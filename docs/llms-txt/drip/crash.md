# Crash

> A strategic game where players bet on a multiplying value aiming to cash-out before the multiplier crashes

<Warning>
  *We strictly prohibit the usage of casino features if your Points can be
  purchased or sold for real-world value.*
</Warning>

### Creating a new Crash Game

To start a Crash game, go to the `🎲 Casino` channel and click the `Start` button. Now click `🚀 New Crash Game` from the dropdown menu

### Starting a Crash Game

To begin a new round of Crash, click on the `Start Round` button.

<Frame caption="Click on Start Round">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/4PSGtW5kH_NJ_rrk/images/drip-features/games/img_c890c3.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=4PSGtW5kH_NJ_rrk&q=85&s=899ccf5e07e1f1f41c4d191ba929d5c3" width="658" height="160" data-path="images/drip-features/games/img_c890c3.jpg" />
</Frame>

Choose the amount of points you would like to bet in the game.

<Frame caption="Choose your bet amount">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/4PSGtW5kH_NJ_rrk/images/drip-features/games/img_82905c.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=4PSGtW5kH_NJ_rrk&q=85&s=b73c051dd6ea2c6b84ff61f2f0e888cf" width="990" height="578" data-path="images/drip-features/games/img_82905c.jpg" />
</Frame>

The Game Master can start the game once all players have selected a bet amount.

The embed seen below will be sent in the thread and the multiplier GIF in the top right corner will begin to count upwards.

To cash out, click the `Cash Out` button before the multiplier crashes.

<Frame caption="Click Cash Out before the Crash">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/4PSGtW5kH_NJ_rrk/images/drip-features/games/img_6a2838.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=4PSGtW5kH_NJ_rrk&q=85&s=cfdc4b31caa49488f15cf111cea33728" width="986" height="526" data-path="images/drip-features/games/img_6a2838.jpg" />
</Frame>

A message will appear showing you your winnings when you cash out
