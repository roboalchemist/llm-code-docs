# Overview

> Create a Quest that requires members to perform an action of your choosing

### Creating a Custom Quest

To create a Custom Quest, click on `✨ Custom Quests` from the dropdown menu

<Frame caption="Click on Custom Quests">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk/images/drip-features/quests/img_dde851.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk&q=85&s=96ed265af2c0f411290859f0e0a8fc6d" width="400" height="190" data-path="images/drip-features/quests/img_dde851.png" />
</Frame>

A form will appear for you to enter information about the custom quest.

<Frame caption="Custom Quest Setup Form">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk/images/drip-features/quests/img_90f07d.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk&q=85&s=4341de7ad7700d13d538ee983d0062d4" width="872" height="1126" data-path="images/drip-features/quests/img_90f07d.jpg" />
</Frame>

This information will now appear in the Quest embed

<Frame caption="Updated Quest Embed">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk/images/drip-features/quests/img_0b8d61.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk&q=85&s=97b02dd89da6e99be7025e7a3661c625" width="988" height="660" data-path="images/drip-features/quests/img_0b8d61.jpg" />
</Frame>

After adding requirements to the Custom Quest, you can modify different components:

* ✨ Reward&#x73;**:** Modify the total budget and amount of points per claim. You can also select a role to reward upon completion of the quest

<Info>
  Custom Quests have the ability to give "Custom Rewards"

    <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk/images/drip-features/quests/img_645a1a.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk&q=85&s=11cfbef28f9b2154b262aca26cca1c59" alt="" width="814" height="188" data-path="images/drip-features/quests/img_645a1a.jpg" />
</Info>

* ✨ Reward&#x73;**:** Modify the total budget and amount of points per claim. You can also choose a role to reward, or apply a multiplier for future points earned

* ⏱ **Time:** Change the expiry limit of the Quest (leave blank for no expiry)

* 🔔 Role&#x73;**:** Select roles to mention in the Quest

* ✏️ **Customize:** Customize how the Quest looks

* 📄 **Templates:** Create or Select a Quest template

<Frame caption="Modify Quest Components">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk/images/drip-features/quests/img_7c5db0.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=j6RMTMdOfXJbm5Jk&q=85&s=effe90396c8df8e5954769638306973f" width="444" height="79" data-path="images/drip-features/quests/img_7c5db0.png" />
</Frame>

Click the publish button to send the Quest when finished. A thread will also be created for the creator of the Quest to [verify entries](/drip-features/quests/custom-quests/verify-entries).
