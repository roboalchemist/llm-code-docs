# NFT Sales Tracker

> Set up a sales tracker for your NFT Collections so you never miss a sale again!

You can access the **NFT Sales Tracker** settings by clicking the `🤑 NFT Sales Tracker` option in the [Web3 Settings](/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings) menu.

When you click on the option, you will see the following menu:

## Add New Tracker

To add a new tracker, click the `New Tracker` button. This will open up a new menu where you configure your sales tracker.

<Frame caption="Add Sales Tracker Menu">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/fZiObPJYfg75-m3M/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_27d741.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=fZiObPJYfg75-m3M&q=85&s=5b6095de3dd8ca5b4b1e82fbb86d26d4" width="998" height="698" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_27d741.png" />
</Frame>

1. Click the `Choose Collection` button, you will see a dropdown menu appear. Select the collection you want to set up the Sales Tracker for.

<Frame caption="Choose the Collection">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/fZiObPJYfg75-m3M/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_522718.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=fZiObPJYfg75-m3M&q=85&s=1b3e9109a153213a8f0ba76789b1146e" width="792" height="188" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_522718.png" />
</Frame>

1. Now click the `Choose Channel` button, you will see a dropdown menu appear. Select the channel you want the sales to be posted.

<Frame caption="Choose the Channel">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_e67a70.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN&q=85&s=977a412c77a6ca14132a4b5febf9ff99" width="792" height="524" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_e67a70.png" />
</Frame>

1. Once it is configured as desired, click the `✅ Save Tracker` button to save the Sales Tracker and now you will see the confirmation message:

<Frame caption="Confirmation Message">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/fZiObPJYfg75-m3M/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_58d205.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=fZiObPJYfg75-m3M&q=85&s=ccefb13b5457e61f1b0417a116d7e4bb" width="990" height="798" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_58d205.png" />
</Frame>

## Edit Tracker

To add a new tracker, click the `Manage Tracker` button. This will open a new menu where you can see all active sales trackers and a dropdown menu where you can select them.

1. To edit a Sales Tracker, pick the Sales Tracker you want to edit in the drop-down menu.

<Frame caption="Choose the Collection You Want To Edit">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_d58f36.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN&q=85&s=7fc30ae0301b8f4af3686b8ef213f76c" width="996" height="714" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_d58f36.png" />
</Frame>

1. Now you will get some option for you to edit. Pick the option you want to edit.

<Frame caption="Pick the Option">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_c71607.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN&q=85&s=62f337031b5b064ea52183e49f340d87" width="1168" height="654" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_c71607.png" />
</Frame>

**2.1** **Edit Channel**

Click `📝 Edit Channel` to edit the channel to which sales are sent. You will get the option to choose a new channel. Pick the channel in the drop-down menu.

<Frame caption="Pick The Channel In The Drop-down Menu">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/fZiObPJYfg75-m3M/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_552499.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=fZiObPJYfg75-m3M&q=85&s=e5fb3244254cd7211590d809f472d529" width="1172" height="670" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_552499.png" />
</Frame>

**2.2 Toggle Linked Only**

Click `🔗Toggle Linked Only`to send only sales from linked accounts in the server.

## **Remove Tracker**

To remove a tracker, use the `Manage Trackers` button, **select** the sales tracker you want to delete and click the `🗑️ Delete Tracker` button to delete the tracker.

<Frame caption="Removed Tracker">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN/images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_99db23.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=kUGFp_25nD2Z7HmN&q=85&s=c9f9efdfa67dca951a8d5a89dc6fffcd" width="990" height="658" data-path="images/admin-settings/admin-dashboard/admin-settings/web3-settings/img_99db23.png" />
