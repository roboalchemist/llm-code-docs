# Browse NFTs

> View NFTs you hold from the server's activated collections.

### Browse NFTs

Go to your [Dashboard](/drip-features/user-dashboard) and click the `🔎 Browse NFTs` button.

<Frame caption="Pair NFT Button">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/93FznksNZY37vUgd/images/drip-features/user-dashboard/img_8f3b66.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=93FznksNZY37vUgd&q=85&s=0c69a183eea4d4fd6cb6ec797a43a500" width="285" height="130" data-path="images/drip-features/user-dashboard/img_8f3b66.png" />
</Frame>

Choose an activated collection you want to browse.

<Frame caption="Active Collections Select">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/93FznksNZY37vUgd/images/drip-features/user-dashboard/img_a655b0.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=93FznksNZY37vUgd&q=85&s=aa8052c3c314d83c8d60ca291ecf37af" width="420" height="167" data-path="images/drip-features/user-dashboard/img_a655b0.png" />
</Frame>

Once you make a selection, all owned NFTs in that collection are displayed from your connected wallets. When the collection type is a `🔥 ``Generator`, it will also display the points balance the NFT currently holds.

<Frame caption="List NFTs Collection">
  <img src="https://mintcdn.com/driprewards/93FznksNZY37vUgd/images/drip-features/user-dashboard/img_2ece9d.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=93FznksNZY37vUgd&q=85&s=18a142ca4231b5c3cc4f1a0aa9683978" width="401" height="324" data-path="images/drip-features/user-dashboard/img_2ece9d.png" />
</Frame>

If you click on an NFT, you will get a display similar to the one below depending on the type of NFT effect:
