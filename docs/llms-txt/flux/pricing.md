# Pricing

## How much does Flux cost?

For the latests plans available, take a look at our [pricing page](https://www.flux.ai/p/pricing). 

## How do I cancel my plan?

You can manage your subscription (including cancelling), from the Plans and Payments menu in the top right, then click on "Manage Plan".

## I am a student. How do I access the educational plan?

Students get free access to the educational plan. To access the benefit, you need to sign up using your student account. 

- If your student account contains ".edu" (for example [johndoe@stanford.edu](mailto:johndoe@stanford.edu)), you'll automatically get access to the student plan. **You only need to create an account with Flux using that email address.**
- If you're a student but don't have access to a ".edu" account, please [fill out this form](https://fluxai.typeform.com/to/XRKbXYE8).
