# First Time Designer

**You’re in the right place if:**

- You’re new to PCB design or CAD tools
- You’ve never manufactured a PCB before
- You prefer step-by-step guidance and clear explanations

**Your focus** should be on learning the basics, designing small circuits, and exploring foundational concepts through guided tutorials.

### 📘 Tutorials

- **Your First PCB in Flux**
    - [A step-by-step walkthrough](https://docs.flux.ai/flux/Introduction/flux-walkthrough-project) that takes you from zero to a completed PCB. We introduce concepts like placing components, wiring signals, and exporting for manufacture—no experience required.
