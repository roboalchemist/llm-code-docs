# General FAQs

### Does Flux have an offline version?

Flux is a cloud-based solution and does not offer an offline version.

### How do I change Flux to Light Mode?

Sorry, Flux's UI doesn't have a light mode.

### How do I manage or update my profile settings?

To update your profile, visit your [Flux profile](https://www.flux.ai/signup) and click **Edit Profile** to change details like your display name, bio, and social media handles.

### How do I change my password?

Log out and use the **Forgot Password** link at [login page.](https://www.flux.ai/login) If you signed up with Google or GitHub, change your password through those services.
