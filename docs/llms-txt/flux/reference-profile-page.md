# The Flux Profile Page

The profile page serves as your personal dashboard in Flux, providing a centralized location to manage all your PCB design projects and showcase your work to the community.

## Accessing Your Profile Page

There are two ways to access your profile page:

1. **From the top menu**: Click on your avatar in the top-right corner of any Flux page, then select "Profile"
2. **Direct URL**: Navigate to `flux.ai/[your-username]`

## Key Features

### Project Management

Your profile page displays all your projects, organized in a grid layout with thumbnails for easy visual recognition. Each project card shows:

- Project name
- Last modified date
- Visibility status (public or private)
- Thumbnail preview of the design

### Filtering and Searching

Efficiently find specific projects using the built-in filtering and search capabilities:

- **Filter by status**: View all projects, only public projects, or only private projects
- **Filter by type**: Filter by schematic-only, PCB-only, or complete projects
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find projects by name or description

### Portfolio Showcase

Your public profile serves as your portfolio in the Flux community:

- Share your `flux.ai/[your-username]` URL to showcase your public projects
- Control which projects appear in your portfolio by adjusting their visibility settings
- Highlight your best work by pinning important projects to the top of your profile

## Managing Project Visibility

Control who can see your projects directly from the profile page:

1. Hover over a project card
2. Click the visibility icon (eye symbol)
3. Select "Public" or "Private" from the dropdown menu

## Organizing Projects

Keep your workspace tidy with these organization features:

- **Sort projects**: Arrange by last modified date, creation date, or alphabetically
- **Archive projects**: Move completed or inactive projects to the archive section
- **Delete projects**: Permanently remove projects you no longer need

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Missing Projects

- **Issue**: Projects don't appear on your profile page
- **Solution**: Check if you're signed in with the correct account or if the projects were archived

### Visibility Changes Not Taking Effect

- **Issue**: Projects remain private despite changing visibility settings
- **Solution**: Refresh the page or check if you have the necessary permissions to change visibility

### Profile URL Not Working

- **Issue**: Your profile URL returns an error
- **Solution**: Verify your username is correct and that you have at least one public project
