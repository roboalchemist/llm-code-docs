## Slack Community

The best place for support using Flux is our [Slack community](https://www.flux.ai/p/slack-community). You'll be able to ask questions directly to the Flux team and meet other engineers, designers, and hardware enthusiasts using Flux.

We're a remote company with team members in most time zones, and all of us are present in Slack. **This makes it the fastest way to get your questions answered, as it's very likely that someone from Flux will be online.**

### Getting the Most from the Slack Community

1. **Use the appropriate channels**: Post your questions in the relevant channel for faster responses
2. **Share project links**: When asking for help with a specific project, include a link to it
3. **Be specific about your issue**: Clearly describe what you're trying to accomplish and what's not working
4. **Include screenshots**: Visual references can help others understand your problem more quickly
5. **Check pinned messages**: Many channels have important information pinned that might answer your question

## Found a Bug?

If you happen to find any unexpected behavior while using Flux, please report it in our [issue-tracking tool](https://fluxai.canny.io/). Someone from the engineering team will take care of it and let you know when it's fixed.

There's also a "Feature request" section where you can help shape the direction of Flux by letting us know what you'd like to see in Flux.

### How to Submit an Effective Bug Report

When reporting a bug, please include:

1. **Steps to reproduce**: Detailed steps that consistently trigger the issue
2. **Expected behavior**: What you expected to happen
3. **Actual behavior**: What actually happened
4. **Browser and OS information**: Your browser version and operating system
5. **Screenshots or recordings**: Visual evidence of the issue when possible
6. **Project link**: A link to the project where the issue occurred (if applicable)

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Account Access Problems
- Reset your password through the login page
- Clear your browser cookies and cache
- Ensure you're using the email address associated with your Flux account

### Design Import/Export Issues
- Check that your files are in a supported format
- For large designs, try importing in smaller sections
- Verify that all required files are included in your import package

### Performance Concerns
- Close unnecessary browser tabs to free up memory
- For complex designs, consider breaking them into modules
- Use the object tree to hide layers or components not currently being worked on
