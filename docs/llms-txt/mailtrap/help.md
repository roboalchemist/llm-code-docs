## Quick Solutions

### Can't send emails?

1. Verify your [API token](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/setup/api-tokens) is correct
2. Check your [sending domain](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/setup/sending-domain) is verified
3. Review your [sending limits](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/setup/sending-limits)
4. Confirm your [integration](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/setup/api-integration) is properly configured

### Emails not reaching inbox?

1. Check [email logs](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/analytics/logs) for delivery status
2. Review [bounce categorization](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/deliverability/bounce-categorization) for issues
3. Verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records
4. Check if recipients are on [suppressions list](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/deliverability/suppressions-list)

### Integration issues?

1. Review the [API integration guide](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/setup/api-integration)
2. Check the [SMTP integration guide](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-api-smtp/setup/smtp-integration)
3. Test with [Email Sandbox](https://docs.mailtrap.io/email-sandbox/overview) first
4. Use our [API reference](https://api-docs.mailtrap.io/) for detailed endpoints
