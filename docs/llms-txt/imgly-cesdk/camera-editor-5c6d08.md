## What is the Camera and Editor Solution?

This prebuilt solution combines a customizable Flutter camera module with CE.SDK’s powerful creative editing engine. Capture high-quality photos or videos directly in your app, then hand them off to a rich editor supporting filters, cropping, adjustments, text overlays, stickers, and more.

Built on top of the Creative Engine that powers CE.SDK, it offers real-time previews, fast rendering, and native performance on both Android and iOS.

## Key Features

- **Flutter Camera Module**\
  Capture photos and videos with control over resolution, aspect ratio, flash, and camera switching—all from your Flutter app.

- **Integrated Media Editor**\
  Let users enhance their media with filters, crop and rotate, fine-tune adjustments like brightness and contrast, or add text, stickers, and overlays.

- **Template Support**\
  Load dynamic templates with placeholders—perfect for branded experiences or user-generated content.

- **Cross-Platform Support**\
  Runs natively on both Android and iOS, ensuring consistent behavior across devices.

- **Optimized for Flutter**\
  Designed with mobile performance in mind—native rendering, low memory overhead, and minimal integration effort.

## Why Use This Solution?

Whether you're building a social platform, marketplace, or storytelling tool, this Flutter-first solution lets you launch faster with built-in camera and editor functionality. Skip the complexity of custom media tools and focus on delivering unique experiences with a proven SDK under the hood.

This solution is ideal for:

- Flutter developers building apps with user-generated content
- Teams needing high-performance camera and editing features
- Mobile-first products looking for rapid time-to-market with minimal setup
