# Integrations

<HTMLBlock>
  {`
  <div class="cs-headline">At Cloudsmith, our goal is to make it as easy as possible for you to integrate the different elements of your software supply chain.</div>
  `}
</HTMLBlock>

Cloudsmith comes with an awesome [CLI](https://help.cloudsmith.io/docs/cli) (Command Line Interface) tool, a discoverable first-class [API](https://api.cloudsmith.io), and [bindings](https://help.cloudsmith.io/docs/api-bindings) allowing you to publish and download your packages and containers using practically any leading DevOps tool/service.

> 📘 Service Accounts
>
> For CI/CD, we would usually recommend that you create a Service Account in your organization. See [Service Accounts](https://help.cloudsmith.io/docs/bot-service-accounts) for more details.
