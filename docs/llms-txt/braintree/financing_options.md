        This feature is only available to merchants domiciled in Brazil.
    </span></div></div></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>
    Consumers using credit cards in Brazil are accustomed to seeing installment options to pay for
    some or all of the items in their shopping cart. Now, Braintree will support Billing Agreement
    with Installments in Brazil. Merchants will integrate using the Braintree GraphQL API to offer
    </span><a href="/braintree/articles/guides/payment-methods/paypal/overview"><span>Pay with PayPal</span></a><span> to their

        From the merchant checkout page the consumer clicks the </span><code><span>Pay with PayPal</span></code><span> button and
        is directed to Hermes flow where the consumer will set up the billing agreement and be able to
        select/change their funding instrument.
    </span></li><li><span>

        Braintree GraphQL API) to get installment options.
    </span></li><li><span>
        Consumer will be redirected back to the merchant checkout page and will see their billing
        agreement and the option to select installments on the checkout page.
    </span></li><li><span>
        Consumer will then be able to Checkout with their billing agreement and a selected installment
        option.
