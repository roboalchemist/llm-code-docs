How does this all work?\n\n\n\nSingle Sign-On (SSO) is a technology that allows users to access multiple applications with a single login. Instead of entering credentials for each individual app, users authenticate once and gain seamless access to all their assigned SaaS applications.SSO is sort of like a bar. In a bar, customers only show ID once to order drinks, instead of re-verifying for each round. Similarly, with SSO, users verify their identity once and then gain access to multiple services without repeated logins.\n\n\n\nThe basic steps of a SSO/SAML Login flow are as such:\n\n\n- **User attempts to access a service** (like Salesforce).\n\n\n- The **service provider redirects** the user to the identity provider (like Okta, Entra, OneLogin etc.).\n\n\n- The **identity provider authenticates** the user (usually with credentials or MFA).\n\n\n- Upon successful login, the identity provider generates a **SAML assertion** (an XML-based document).\n\n\n- The SAML assertion is passed back to the **service provider**, verifying that the user is authenticated.\n\n\n- The **service provider grants access** to the user without requiring a separate login.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n## What does SSO/SAML at Braintree look like?\n\nBraintree supports SAML 2.0-based Single Sign-On (SSO), allowing users to log in through your organization's identity provider.\n\n\n- Users are created, edited, suspended, andmanaged in the **Braintree Control Panel**\n\n\n- Users **log in via your IdP** -\u0026gt; No password on our site.\n\n\n- Users suspended in the IdP will be denied access to Braintree but will still appear active in the Control Panel unless manually suspended or deleted\n\n\n- We support SP-Initiated SSO, or when users begin login at Braintree's site via [https://id.braintreegateway.com/sso/sessions/new](https://id.braintreegateway.com/sso/sessions/new) in production or [https://id.sandbox.braintreegateway.com/sso/sessions/new](https://id.sandbox.braintreegateway.com/sso/sessions/new) in sandbox.\n\n\n- \n\n\n\nWe also support IdP-Initiated SSO, where users begin login from their IdP dashboard.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n## Required Setup Values\n\n| Field | Example | Notes |\n| --- | --- | --- |\n| **Merchant ID** | 1ab2cdefghij4kl5 | Found in the Control Panel URL after/merchants/ |\n| **Email Domain(s)** | @yourcompany.com | List all domains used for user login.
