class="main-article-content"><div class="ecmRichText" style="margin-bottom:0px"><h1><span>Creating Applications for Merchant Accounts</span></h1><p class="markdown-p"><div class="custom-block notification notification--availability"><div class="custom-block-heading">Availability</div><div class="custom-block-body"><span>
        The account onboarding API is only available for select merchants at this time.
    </span></div></div></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>
    If your business has hundreds of billers, franchisees, or sub-merchants, this API streamlines the
    process of submitting their information for underwriting. Once a biller is approved, you can
    process payment in its name and create payout calls to move funds.
</span></p><h2 id="requirements" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Requirements</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#requirements" aria-label="Requirements"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>
    In order to use this API, you must have completed the initial application process for Partners and
    have been set up by your PayPal Account Manager, with login credentials to the Braintree control
    panel. You'll also need to work with your Account Manager to configure and set up all the features
    you require.
</span></p><h2 id="creating-an-application" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Creating an Application</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#creating-an-application" aria-label="Creating an Application"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>
    To create an account application, you will specify all relevant business information for
    underwriting and configuration options for each payment method you will accept. A few input fields
    define the structure of a </span><code><span>createBusinessAccount</span></code><span> mutation.
</span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>
    Under </span><code><span>createBusinessAccount</span></code><span> you will find some settings for the account, such as the
    supported </span><code><span>countryCode</span></code><span>, </span><code><span>externalId</span></code><span>, etc. In addition, the
    </span><code><span>financialInstruments</span></code><span> and </span><code><span>business</span></code><span> inputs are all nested under it.
</span></p><p class="markdown-p"><code><span>business</span></code><span> represents the legal entity of your biller and its business details.
    </span><code><span>business.stakeholders</span></code><span> represents personas who are owners/office bearers of the
    business, and optional </span><code><span>business.pointsOfContact</span></code><span> are additional people who should be
    involved in the vetting and review process other than stakeholders.
</span></p><p class="markdown-p"><code><span>financialInstruments</span></code><span> represents the payout method for the merchant account.</span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>
