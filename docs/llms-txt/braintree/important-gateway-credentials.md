  purposes, and are often confused with one another.
</span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>

  of the </span><a href="/braintree/articles/control-panel/important-gateway-credentials#api-credentials"><span>four API credentials</span></a><span>. This value is required for certain

  shopping carts.
</span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>
  Your </span><strong><span>merchant account ID</span></strong><span> is a unique identifier for a specific merchant account
  in your gateway. It is used to specify which merchant account to use when creating a transaction,
  creating a subscription, verifying a payment method, or generating a client token.
