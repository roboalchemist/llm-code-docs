class="main-article-content"><div class="ecmRichText" style="margin-bottom:0px"><section><h1><span>Venmo</span></h1><p class="markdown-p"><span>
        Grow your business by tapping into the Venmo consumer base when you offer Venmo as a payment
        option at checkout. Your customers will be able to use their Venmo accounts to make purchases,
        offering more convenience and flexibility. In addition, customers can can connect their Venmo
        wallet to your mobile app, mobile website, or desktop website to seamlessly pay for all future
        transactions via Venmo without having to reauthorize in the Venmo app (also known as
        </span><a href="/braintree/articles/guides/payment-methods/venmo#vaulting"><span>Vaulting</span></a><span>).
    </span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>
        Paying with Venmo also gives customers the flexibility to fund transactions using their Venmo
        balance or any of their saved payment methods, while you enjoy your same low processing rate for
        all those transactions.
    </span></p><h2 id="availability" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Availability</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#availability" aria-label="Availability"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>
        The current release of Venmo's payment product for merchants is only available for certain
        business models.
    </span></p><h5 id="use-cases-that-are-not-supported-include" class="test_h5 markdown-h5"><span>Use cases that are not supported include:</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#use-cases-that-are-not-supported-include" aria-label="Use cases that are not supported include:"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h5><ul class="list list--disc"><li><span>Selling goods or services in person</span></li><li><span>
            Receiving payment for goods or services through the Venmo app
            </span><ul class="list list--disc"><li><span>e.g., a mechanic or a plumber wants to charge a client after providing a service</span></li></ul></li><li><span>Facilitating peer-to-peer transactions between two Venmo users</span></li></ul><p class="markdown-p"><span>
        You can accept Venmo as a payment method if none of the above apply, and you meet all of the
        following criteria:
    </span></p><ul class="list list--disc"><li><span>You process (or plan to process) transactions using a US business entity</span></li><li><span>
            You use (or plan to use) one of our compatible SDKs:
            </span><ul class="list list--disc"><li><a href="/braintree/docs/guides/client-sdk/setup/android/v5"><span>Android v5 SDK</span></a></li><li><a href="/braintree/docs/guides/client-sdk/setup/ios/v6"><span>iOS v6 SDK</span></a></li><li><a href="/braintree/docs/guides/client-sdk/setup/javascript/v3"><span>JavaScript v3 SDK</span></a></li></ul></li></ul><p class="markdown-p"><a href="/braintree/articles/guides/payment-methods/venmo#setup"><span>See our setup instructions</span></a><span>
        for details on how to begin processing payments with Venmo and check out the
        </span><a href="/braintree/docs/guides/venmo/configuration"><span>developer docs</span></a><span> for information on how to
        add Venmo as a payment method to your integration.
    </span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>If your use case is not yet supported, please check back for updates on a wider release!</span></p><h2 id="customer-availability" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Customer availability</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#customer-availability" aria-label="Customer availability"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>Venmo payments are supported on the following Venmo app versions:</span></p><ul class="list list--disc"><li><span>9.1.0 or later for iOS apps</span></li><li><span>9.13.0 or later for Android apps</span></li><li><span>7.5.0 or later for iOS/Android mobile web browsers</span></li><li><span>8.12.0 or later for desktop web browsers</span></li></ul><p class="markdown-p"><span>To check the Venmo app version:</span></p><ol class="list list--num"><li><span>Log into the Venmo app</span></li><li><span>Click your profile icon on the bottom right corner</span></li><li><span>Click the </span><strong><span>Settings</span></strong><span> icon (top right corner)</span></li><li><span>Scroll to the very end of the Settings page to see the Venmo app version</span></li></ol><p class="markdown-p"><span>Minimum OS Requirements for mobile devices:</span></p><ul class="list list--disc"><li><span>Android 6.0 or later</span></li><li><span>iOS 12.0 or later</span></li></ul><p class="markdown-p"><span>The version number will be displayed at the bottom of the Settings page.</span></p><h2 id="processing" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Processing</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#processing" aria-label="Processing"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>

    </span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>

    </span></p><p class="markdown-p"><a href="/braintree/help/Reconciling"><span>Contact us</span></a><span> for more details on your available

    </span></p><h3 id="refunds-and-voids" class="test_h3 markdown-h3"><span>Refunds and voids</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#refunds-and-voids" aria-label="Refunds and voids"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h3><p class="markdown-p"><span>

    </span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>Venmo requires that refunds are issued within 180 days of the initial sale.</span></p><h2 id="vaulting" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Vaulting</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#vaulting" aria-label="Vaulting"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>Venmo payment methods can be used for vaulting.</span></p><p class="markdown-p"><span>
        For a more seamless customer experience, you can store the payment method token on your servers
        (i.e. vaulting). This allows you to process future transactions without having to send the
        customer to the Venmo app, also known as an app switch.
    </span></p><h2 id="setup" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Setup</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#setup" aria-label="Setup"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>

    </span></p><h3 id="testing" class="test_h3 markdown-h3"><span>Testing</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#testing" aria-label="Testing"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h3><p class="markdown-p"><span>

    </span></p><ol class="list list--num"><li><span>
            Log into the </span><a href="https://sandbox.braintreegateway.com/login"><span>sandbox Control Panel</span></a></li><li><span>Click on the gear icon in the top right corner</span></li><li><span>Click </span><strong><span>Account Settings</span></strong><span> from the drop-down menu</span></li><li><span>Scroll to the </span><strong><span>Payment Methods</span></strong><span> section</span></li><li><span>Next to </span><strong><span>Venmo</span></strong><span>, click the </span><strong><span>Options</span></strong><span> link</span></li><li><span>Click the </span><strong><span>Accept</span></strong><span> button to accept Venmo's terms of service</span></li></ol><p class="markdown-p"><span>
