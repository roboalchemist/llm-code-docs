class="main-article-content"><div class="ecmRichText" style="margin-bottom:0px"><section><h1><span>Account Updater</span></h1><p class="markdown-p"><span>
  Account Updater is a feature that automatically requests updates for vaulted payment methods in
  the event that a customer's vaulted card expires or is replaced – helping you avoid failed
  transactions or gaps in services provided to your customers.
</span></p><h2 id="fees" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Fees</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#fees" aria-label="Fees"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>
  Pricing for Account Updater varies depending on your pricing model.
  </span><a href="/braintree/help/feeHelp"><span>Contact us</span></a><span> for more information on fees.
</span></p><h2 id="compatibility" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Compatibility</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#compatibility" aria-label="Compatibility"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>
  Account Updater is available to merchants who use Braintree Direct and are either based in the US
  or transact primarily with US customers. </span><a href="/braintree/help"><span>Contact us</span></a><span> to see if
  Account Updater is a good fit for your business.
</span></p><h3 id="customer-compatibility" class="test_h3 markdown-h3"><span>Customer compatibility</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#customer-compatibility" aria-label="Customer compatibility"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h3><p class="markdown-p"><span>
  Whether a card can be updated using Account Updater is dependent on the customer’s issuing bank
  and that bank's participation in this feature. As a result, even if a customer's card type is
  compatible, it may not be eligible for automatic updates if the issuing bank does not participate.
  Supported card types are:
</span></p><ul class="list list--disc"><li><span>Visa</span></li><li><span>Mastercard</span></li><li><span>Discover</span></li></ul><div role="alert" class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__1QhIq ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert--note__gRM6J"><div class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__header__T2Wkg"><style data-emotion="css 4757ty-svg-size_xs">.css-4757ty-svg-size_xs{speak:none;display:inline-block;aspect-ratio:1;width:1.125rem;height:1.125rem;}.css-4757ty-svg-size_xs img,.css-4757ty-svg-size_xs svg{width:100%;height:100%;display:block;}</style><span class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__icon__puACD css-4757ty-svg-size_xs" data-ppui-info="icons_8.21.2" role="img" aria-label="note" data-ppui="true"><svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" fill="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="1em" height="1em" data-ppui="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M21 12a9 9 0 1 1-18 0 9 9 0 0 1 18 0zm-9.662 5h1.32c.18 0 .276-.096.276-.276v-5.925c0-.18-.096-.276-.276-.276h-1.32c-.18 0-.276.096-.276.276v5.925c0 .18.096.276.276.276zM12 9a1 1 0 1 0 0-2 1 1 0 0 0 0 2z" clip-rule="evenodd" data-ppui="true"></path></svg></span><span class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__title__FOcbD">Note</span><span class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__content__2-Ddi"><p class="markdown-p"><span>Prepaid cards and cards processed through Apple Pay or Google Pay are not supported.</span></p></span></div></div><h2 id="setup" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>Setup</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#setup" aria-label="Setup"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>
  Account Updater is not enabled on your account by default. We’ll work with you directly to make
  sure this feature is configured in a way that works best for your business.
  </span><a href="/braintree/help"><span>Contact us to get started.</span></a></p><h2 id="how-it-works" class="test_h2 markdown-h2"><span>How it works</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#how-it-works" aria-label="How it works"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h2><p class="markdown-p"><span>
  When Account Updater is first enabled, we automatically send all vaulted Visa, Mastercard, and
  Discover credit card information to the card-issuing banks to request updates in batches of
  600,000. If the issuing bank has opted to participate, they will respond with the new card number
  and/or expiration date – allowing us to update the vaulted card.
</span></p><div role="alert" class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__1QhIq ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert--note__gRM6J"><div class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__header__T2Wkg"><style data-emotion="css 4757ty-svg-size_xs">.css-4757ty-svg-size_xs{speak:none;display:inline-block;aspect-ratio:1;width:1.125rem;height:1.125rem;}.css-4757ty-svg-size_xs img,.css-4757ty-svg-size_xs svg{width:100%;height:100%;display:block;}</style><span class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__icon__puACD css-4757ty-svg-size_xs" data-ppui-info="icons_8.21.2" role="img" aria-label="note" data-ppui="true"><svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" fill="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="1em" height="1em" data-ppui="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M21 12a9 9 0 1 1-18 0 9 9 0 0 1 18 0zm-9.662 5h1.32c.18 0 .276-.096.276-.276v-5.925c0-.18-.096-.276-.276-.276h-1.32c-.18 0-.276.096-.276.276v5.925c0 .18.096.276.276.276zM12 9a1 1 0 1 0 0-2 1 1 0 0 0 0 2z" clip-rule="evenodd" data-ppui="true"></path></svg></span><span class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__title__FOcbD">Note</span><span class="ContextualAlert-module_pp-alert__content__2-Ddi"><p class="markdown-p"><span>

    due within 2 weeks
  </span></li><li><span>Card has been flagged for </span><a href="/braintree/articles/guides/account-updater#next-day-card-refresh"><span>Next Day Card Refresh</span></a></li><li><span>
    Relevant transaction activity suggests an update may be needed;
    </span><a href="/braintree/articles/guides/account-updater#transaction-activity-criteria"><span>learn more</span></a></li></ul><h3 id="transaction-activity-criteria" class="test_h3 markdown-h3"><span>Transaction activity criteria</span><a class="icon icon--link hash-link" href="#transaction-activity-criteria" aria-label="Transaction activity criteria"><img src="https://www.paypalobjects.com/btdevdoc/bundles/4.3.24/_assets/anchor-PKYVW2VT.svg" alt="AnchorIcon"/></a></h3><p class="markdown-p"><span>Account Updater will request an update for a vaulted card that meets the following criteria:</span></p><ul class="list list--disc"><li><span>Has been declined by the processor in the last 30 days</span></li><li><span>
    Has been processed successfully within the last 13 months, but has not had any successful
