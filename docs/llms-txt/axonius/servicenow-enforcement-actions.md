# ServiceNow Related Enforcement Actions

Axonius integrates with ServiceNow to help reconcile and maintain your CMDB while streamlining incident management. Through Enforcement Center actions, Axonius can create and update devices in ServiceNow, ensuring accurate and current asset records. It can also generate ServiceNow incidents based on device or user data from Axonius, enabling timely manual intervention when needed, such as addressing device issues or updating user accounts.
