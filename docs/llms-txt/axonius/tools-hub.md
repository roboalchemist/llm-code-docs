# Tools Hub

The **Tools Hub** is your centralized and intuitive toolbox for accessing all Axonius Identities tools and simulators. This dedicated space consolidates essential tools, including [**Role Mining**](/docs/using-the-role-mining-simulator),[**Entitlement Consolidation**](/docs/entitlement-consolidation-simulator), and [**Rule Simulator**](/docs/rules-simulator).

It's designed to help you investigate complex access scenarios, identify birthright access, pinpoint outlier entitlements and excessive access, and gain deep insights into your identity landscape. Leveraging our AI/ML engine for powerful recommendations, the Tools Hub streamlines administrative access and significantly enhances operational efficiency.
