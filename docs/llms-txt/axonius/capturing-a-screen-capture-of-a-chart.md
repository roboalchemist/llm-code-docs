# Capturing a Screen Capture of a Chart

You can download a screen capture of some charts.

1. From the chart's 3-dot menu, select **Capture Chart**.

   <Image align="center" border={false} width="400px" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/img/platform/dashboards/ChartCaptureChartMenu.png" />
2. A screen capture of the chart is downloaded to your Downloads folder.

   <Image align="center" border={false} width="400px" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/img/platform/dashboards/ChartCaptureDownloadMenu.png" />
