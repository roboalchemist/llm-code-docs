# Identity & Access Workspace

The Identity & Access workspaces helps you secure your entire user population, including human and non-human identities. Manage the complete Identity Lifecycle (JML), establish and enforce access policies, and proactively reduce risk to enhance your overall Identity Security Posture.

See the following for more about Identities:

* [Use Cases for Identities](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/use-cases-for-identities)
* [Identities Resources](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/identities-resources)
* [Identities Dashboards](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/identities-dashboards)
* [Identities Data Model - Basic Concepts](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/identities-data-model-basic-concepts)
* [Identities Glossary](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/identities-glossary)

The Identity & Access workspace **Home** page includes charts and KPIs of special interest regarding identity management. The Home page is a read-only page and is divided into sections that give you immediate insight into your identities environment.

* **Identities Overview** - This section offers key metrics and visualizations related to your organization's identity base, providing a high-level view of identity distribution, trends, and potential risks.
