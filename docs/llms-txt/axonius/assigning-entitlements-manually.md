# Assigning Entitlements Manually

You can manually assign entitlements when an individual identity needs the entitlement and the processes and modules such as Rules, Campaign, Workflow, and Enforcement Actions are too complex, sensitive, and time-consuming.

**To assign an entitlement manually:**

1. From the Managed Identities page, hover over an identity and click **Manage Entitlements**.
   ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/Images/ManuallyAssignEntitlement.png)

2. From the **Adapter Connection** list, select the adapter connection that the entitlement is fetched from. Once the adapter connection is selected, the available entitlements are populated into the Entitlement list.

3. From the **Entitlement** list, select the entitlement to assign.

4. Under **Reason**, enter the reason for granting the entitlement.

5. Click **Save**.
