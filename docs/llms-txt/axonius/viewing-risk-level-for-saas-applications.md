# Viewing Risk Level for SaaS Applications

SaaS applications can have a [risk level](/docs/application-risk-score) associated with them this level can be displayed in the Asset Graph. The level of risk is identified by an icon on the node. The risk levels are:

| Icon                                                                                                                                                            | Risk Level  |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------- |
| <Image alt="AssetGraph-RiskLow.png" width="40px" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/Images/AssetGraph-RiskLow.png" />   | Low risk    |
| <Image alt="AssetGraph-RiskMed.png" width="40px" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/Images/AssetGraph-RiskMed.png" />   | Medium risk |
| <Image alt="AssetGraph-RiskHigh.png" width="40px" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/Images/AssetGraph-RiskHigh.png" /> | High risk   |

This is an example and does not indicate any intrinsic risk for any specific SaaS application.
![AssetGraph-SaaSApps-RiskIndicator.png](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/Images/AssetGraph-SaaSApps-RiskIndicator.png)

You can view or hide the risk indicator from the Layers panel.
