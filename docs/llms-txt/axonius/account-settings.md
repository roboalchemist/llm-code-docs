# Account Settings

From your account settings, you can do the following:

* **Change password** - From the **Password** tab, you can change your login password.

<Image alt="ChangeUserNAme.png" width="400px" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/Images/ChangeUserNAme.png" />

<Callout icon="📘" theme="info">
  Note

  This tab is not available when logging in with Single-SignOn providers.
</Callout>

* **Rotate the API Key** - See [Axonius REST API](/docs/axonius-rest-api) to learn more about accessing the API.

**To change your user password**

1. From the bottom of the left navigation panel, click the avatar.

![UserAccountMenu.png](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Axonius/ax-docs-pub/refs/heads/main/Images/UserAccountMenu.png)

2. From the menu that opens, click **User Settings**. This opens the **My Account** page.
3. To change your login password: From the **Password** tab, type your **Current password**, **New password**, and **Confirm new password**. Then click **Save**.
