# Cyber Assets

Ensure every asset across every system is known, compliant, and protected using Axonius Cyber Assets. Axonius continuously collects, normalizes, and enriches your cyber asset data to give you a complete and trusted view of your entire technology footprint and attack surface. Using Cyber Assets, step up from a static asset inventory to dynamic asset intelligence with the following capabilities:

* [Agent Coverage](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/agent-coverage-hub) - An out-of-the-box solution that delivers immediate visibility into your agent deployment status across different security tools.
* [Device Inventory Classification Overview](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/device-inventory-classification-overview) - A centralized classification page with a structured methodology for managing and classifying your device inventory.
* [Windows Patch Tuesday Workspace](https://docs.axonius.com/axonius-help-docs/docs/windows-patch-tuesday-workspace) - a centralized page monitoring the patch currency of all your Windows clients and servers, and identifying lagging devices, critical vulnerabilities, and unsupported systems.
