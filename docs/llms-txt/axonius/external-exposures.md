# External Exposures

External Exposures is a key tool for monitoring your network to check whether its components are exposed to the public internet. Axonius determines this by checking if a resource's main IP address, or the IP address after being processed by a firewall or load balancer, is a public one. This workspace helps you understand your network's topology and prevent potential attack paths to your organization.
