# Google Meet

> 🚧 Note:
>
> This Google Meet integration can automate the creation of Google Meetings but cannot invite the FireHydrant AI copilot to join the meeting and scribe. To make use of AI features and for future scheduling capabilities, please use the [Google Calendar](https://docs.firehydrant.com/docs/google-calendar-integration) integration.

The Google Meet integration enables you to create Google Meets when incidents are created automatically.

## Prerequisites

* You'll need <Glossary>Owner</Glossary> permissions in FireHydrant to configure integrations
* You'll need a **generic service account**/**user** with administrative privileges
  * FireHydrant recommends using a generic Google service account or user rather than an individual named user - this prevents problems if the named employee were to depart the organization

## Installing the Google Meet integration

<Image alt="The Google Meet tile in the integrations list" align="center" width="650px" src="https://files.readme.io/bccb489-Screenshot_2024-01-09_at_1.42.18_PM.png">
  The Google Meet tile in the integrations list
</Image>

1. Go to FireHydrant's [Integrations page](https://app.firehydrant.io/organizations/integrations) and search for the **Google Meet** app. Click the '+'.
2. Click **Authorize Application** on the next page and follow through the on-screen prompts. You're all set! Google relies on internal authorization to route you to the authenticated meeting room.
