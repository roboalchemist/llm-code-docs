# WhatsApp Business Account Verification

> Verify your WhatsApp Business Account is linked with Plivo

Once you’ve linked your WABA account with Plivo, it will be displayed on the Plivo console.

<Frame>
    <img src="https://mintcdn.com/plivo/9TcugqK5W7G3A-xp/images/verify-onboarding.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=9TcugqK5W7G3A-xp&q=85&s=c6fa5a33b7853097b7f91daba0a92349" alt="" width="962" height="300" data-path="images/verify-onboarding.jpg" />
</Frame>

Verify successful onboarding by navigating to your [Meta Business Account](https://business.facebook.com/settings/whatsapp-business-accounts/) and selecting your Meta account. Under *Business Settings*, click on *WhatsApp Accounts*. Go to the *Partners* tab. You should see Plivo on your Partners list.

<Frame>
    <img src="https://mintcdn.com/plivo/9TcugqK5W7G3A-xp/images/verify.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=9TcugqK5W7G3A-xp&q=85&s=acd9cd327aa18b8be31963d657e88fd4" alt="" width="982" height="350" data-path="images/verify.jpg" />
</Frame>

Next, click on the *Settings* tab. You should see ‘PLIVO INC’ listed under payment method.

<Frame>
    <img src="https://mintcdn.com/plivo/9TcugqK5W7G3A-xp/images/verify2.jpg?fit=max&auto=format&n=9TcugqK5W7G3A-xp&q=85&s=b658786b613910f82865c83236e57771" alt="" width="652" height="350" data-path="images/verify2.jpg" />
</Frame>

If you click [WhatsApp Manager](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/phone-numbers/), the number that was onboarded should appear as `connected`. Read more about [managing WhatsApp phone numbers](/messaging/concepts/whatsapp/manage-whatsapp-phonenumbers/).

You can now create WhatsApp templates. Once you already have approved templates, you can [start messaging](/messaging/use-cases/whatsapp/getting-started/send-whatsapp-template/node/).
