# Rent India Numbers

> Requirements and process for renting phone numbers in India

Renting phone numbers in India requires KYC compliance and is available only to India-registered businesses.

<Warning>
  **INR Account Required:** Indian phone numbers are only available to accounts with INR currency. If your account is in USD, you cannot rent Indian numbers. Account currency cannot be changed after creation — you'll need to create a new INR account.
</Warning>

***

## Requirements

| Requirement           | Details                          |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **Account currency**  | Must be INR (Indian Rupees)      |
| Business registration | India-registered businesses only |
| KYC compliance        | Required before renting numbers  |
| Payment               | Indian credit/debit card only    |

***

## KYC Documents Required

| Document                               | Description                                                                 |
| -------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Certificate of Incorporation (COI)** | Proof of business registration from the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) |
| **GST Certificate**                    | Tax registration                                                            |

<Warning>
  **Both documents are mandatory.** Your application will be rejected if either document is missing.
</Warning>

### Certificate of Incorporation Requirements

Your COI must meet these specific requirements:

| Requirement           | Details                                                       |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Issuing Authority** | Must be issued by the **Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA)** |
| **CIN Number**        | Must include the **Corporate Identification Number (CIN)**    |
| **Company Name**      | Must match exactly with the name on your GST certificate      |

<Note>
  **Common rejection reasons:**

  * Submitting GST certificate only (COI is also required)
  * COI not issued by MCA
  * COI missing CIN number
  * Company name mismatch between COI and GST certificate
</Note>

No other documents are accepted.

***

## Submit Compliance Application

1. Navigate to [Compliance Application](https://cx.plivo.com/phone-numbers?tab=compliance)
2. Click **Create Application**
3. Select **India** and click **Continue**
4. Upload COI and GST Certificate
5. Select business type (Direct Brand or Reseller)
6. Submit

Review time: \~15 minutes (up to 1 business day)

***

## Business Types

| Type             | Definition                             | Compliance Requirement                |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Direct Brand** | Place calls for your own business      | One compliance application            |
| **Reseller**     | Offer communication services to others | Separate application per end customer |

***

## Application Status

| Status   | Meaning                   | Next Steps                      |
| -------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Approved | KYC verified              | Rent numbers                    |
| Rejected | Documents need correction | Check rejection email, resubmit |

***

## Rent Numbers After Approval

### Direct Brands

1. Go to **Buy Phone Numbers**
2. Select India
3. Compliance application links automatically

### Resellers

1. Ensure end-customer compliance is approved
2. During purchase, select the appropriate compliance application
3. Application must be approved beforehand

***

## Update Compliance on Existing Numbers

**Option 1:**

1. Go to **Active Numbers**
2. Select numbers
3. Click **Choose Action > Update Compliance**
4. Select compliance application
5. Save

**Option 2:**

1. Click on specific number
2. Select compliance from dropdown
