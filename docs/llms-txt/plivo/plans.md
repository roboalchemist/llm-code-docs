## Professional Plan

The Professional plan is ideal for startups, developers, and businesses getting started with communications APIs.

### What's Included

**Channels:**

* Voice (PSTN) — Make and receive phone calls
* SMS & RCS — Send text messages globally
* WhatsApp Chat & Call — WhatsApp Business messaging

**AI Agents:**

* Voice Agent — AI-powered phone conversations
* Messaging Agent — Automated SMS/WhatsApp responses
* Chat Agent — Website chat automation
* Verify Agent — Phone number verification

**Features:**

* 2 requests per second (RPS)
* 50 concurrent calls
* Coverage in United States and India
* Self-serve onboarding
* Standard support

### Limits

| Region             | Monthly Usage Limit |
| ------------------ | ------------------- |
| US + Rest of World | \$2,500             |
| India              | ₹2,00,000           |

To exceed these limits, upgrade to Enterprise.

***

## Enterprise Plan

The Enterprise plan is designed for high-volume businesses requiring custom configurations, compliance certifications, and dedicated support.

### What's Included

Everything in Professional, plus:

**Extended Coverage:**

* [Global coverage](https://www.plivo.com/virtual-phone-numbers/coverage/)

**Custom Configuration:**

* Custom RPS and call concurrency limits
* Shortcodes and custom sender IDs
* Volume-based pricing discounts

**Compliance & Security:**

* BAA (Business Associate Agreement) for HIPAA
* HIPAA compliance support
* SSO (Single Sign-On) integration

**Premium Support:**

* Dedicated Slack channel for onboarding
* Priority support

### Pricing

| Region             | Starting Price |
| ------------------ | -------------- |
| US + Rest of World | \$1,000/month  |
| India              | ₹50,000/month  |

Contact [Plivo Sales](https://www.plivo.com/contact/sales/) for custom pricing based on your volume and requirements.

## How to Upgrade

### From Professional to Enterprise

1. Contact [Plivo Sales](https://www.plivo.com/contact/sales/)
2. Discuss your volume and requirements
3. Receive a custom quote
4. Sign agreement and get onboarded via Slack

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I start with Professional and upgrade later?

Yes. Most customers start with the Professional plan and upgrade to Enterprise as their usage grows or when they need enterprise features like compliance certifications.

### What happens if I exceed the Professional plan limits?

Your account will be temporarily limited. Contact [Plivo Sales](https://www.plivo.com/contact/sales/) to upgrade to Enterprise for unlimited usage.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes. The Professional plan is free to start. You only pay for what you use with no minimum commitment.

### Do Enterprise contracts have a minimum term?

Contact [Plivo Sales](https://www.plivo.com/contact/sales/) to discuss contract terms.

### Can I get volume discounts on the Professional plan?

Volume discounts are only available on the Enterprise plan. If you have high volume, Enterprise may be more cost-effective.
