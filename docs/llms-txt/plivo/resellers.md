# Resellers

> WhatsApp reseller requirements and Meta Tech Provider registration

Meta prohibits unauthorized reselling of WhatsApp Business APIs. Independent Software Vendors (ISVs) interested in reselling WhatsApp Services must register as a ‘[Tech Provider](https://business.whatsapp.com/partners/become-a-partner?fbclid=IwAR2SdT7oRMC15hYYRrTcajyrbROdYSEEV_2F_ZUjBMrUppDe1Lc2Gvkc7q8)’ and comply with the conditions specified in the [Tech provider terms](https://www.facebook.com/legal/BM-tech-provider-terms). We encourage you to read through these and reach out for any clarifications.
