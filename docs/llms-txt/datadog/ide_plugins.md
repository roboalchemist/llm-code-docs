## Overview{% #overview %}

[Code Security](https://docs.datadoghq.com/security/code_security/) integrates directly with integrated development environment (IDE) tools to provide real-time feedback on the security and quality of your code. IDE integrations are supported for SAST, SCA, and IAST.

- [JetBrains IDEs: IntelliJ IDEA, GoLand, PyCharm, WebStorm, and PhpStorm](https://docs.datadoghq.com/developers/ide_plugins/idea/#static-analysis)
- [Visual Studio Code](https://docs.datadoghq.com/developers/ide_plugins/vscode/#static-analysis)
