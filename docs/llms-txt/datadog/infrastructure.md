## Overview{% #overview %}

{% video
   url="https://datadog-docs.imgix.net/images/infrastructure/Hostmap-compressed.mp4" /%}

Infrastructure monitoring includes core Datadog features that visualize, monitor, and measure the performance of your hosts, containers, and processes.

## Components{% #components %}

- [Infrastructure List- See a list of all your hosts monitored by Datadog.](https://docs.datadoghq.com/infrastructure/list)
- [Host and Container Maps- Visualize your hosts together on one screen with customized groupings, filters, and metrics made comprehensible by color and shape.](https://docs.datadoghq.com/infrastructure/hostmap)
- [Containers View- Monitor the containers across your environment with real-time visibility.](https://docs.datadoghq.com/infrastructure/containers)
- [Processes View- Monitor your processes with real-time visibility of the most granular elements in a deployment.](https://docs.datadoghq.com/infrastructure/process)
