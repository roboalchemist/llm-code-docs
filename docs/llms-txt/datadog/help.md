Our friendly, knowledgeable solutions engineers are here to help!

You can contact our Support Team by visiting Datadog's Support Website. (all sites).For details on how to log into the Support Website, see Access Your Support Ticket.

Chat with the Datadog Support Team Monday through Friday from 10:00 to 19:00 ET (Eastern Time) using in-app chat. See Reaching out on chat for best practices.
