# Sharing

## Overview{% #overview %}

Shared visualizations allow you to display metric, trace, and log visualizations outside of Datadog. Share visualizations to enhance decision-making and problem-solving processes with team members.

- [**Shared dashboards**: Generate a public link for users to access](https://docs.datadoghq.com/dashboards/sharing/shared_dashboards)
- [**Share graphs**: Generate an embed code](https://docs.datadoghq.com/dashboards/sharing/graphs)
- [**Scheduled reports**: Set a schedule to send email reports](https://docs.datadoghq.com/dashboards/sharing/scheduled_reports)

## View all public dashboards and graphs{% #view-all-public-dashboards-and-graphs %}

To see the complete list of publicly shared dashboards, navigate to [**Organization Settings > Public Sharing > Shared Dashboards**](https://app.datadoghq.com/organization-settings/public-sharing/shared-dashboards). Click the **Shared Graphs** tab to view publicly shared graphs from your organization. To revoke access, see the [dashboard](https://docs.datadoghq.com/dashboards/sharing/shared_dashboards/) and [graph](https://docs.datadoghq.com/dashboards/sharing/graphs/#revoke) documentation.
