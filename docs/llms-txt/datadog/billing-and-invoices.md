# Billing and invoices

As an Account Owner, you can make changes to your subscription and payment methods at any time.

{% alert level="info" %}
Subscriptions created through the AWS Marketplace are managed through your Marketplace Account.
{% /alert %}

## View, print, and configure receipts{% #view-print-and-configure-receipts %}

1. Navigate to **User** > **Subscription settings**.
1. To view, download, or print receipts for past payments, click **Billing history**.
1. To configure receipts to be emailed to you each month, or include additional billing information, such as your company address or VAT ID, click **Receipt settings**.

## Update payment details{% #update-payment-details %}

1. Navigate to **User** > **Subscription settings**.
1. Click **Edit card**.
1. Enter your new card details, then click **Update payment info**.

## Switch to annual payment{% #switch-to-annual-payment %}

You can switch to paying your subscription annually at any time.

1. Navigate to **User** > **Subscription settings**.
1. Click **Change plan**, then toggle the switch from **Monthly** to **Annual**.
1. Click **Continue**, then click **Confirm**.

{% alert level="info" %}
Any already paid for subscription period will be fully prorated, and the remaining credit will be automatically applied towards the next billing cycle.
{% /alert %}
