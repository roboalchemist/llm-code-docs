## Frequently asked questions

### Do you support multi-processor use cases?

Yes. If you already have a payments processor, you don't need to switch your current processing provider(s). You can add PayPal as a processor for all or part of your processing volume.

### I already have your checkout solutions. What's the benefit of using your payment processing and additional services?

PayPal is a full-stack provider of end-to-end payment solutions for global businesses. From core processing to value-added services like optimization, orchestration, fraud protection, and payouts. All of these are available on a single platform with PayPal Open, the one platform for all business.

### How can you help me reduce my payments processing-related costs?

Our comprehensive suite of cost optimization solutions and services such as Smart Routing are designed to reduce costs related to your payment processing.

### How can you help me improve my authorization rates?

Our optimization solutions, like Network Tokenization, Smart Retries, and Direct Connect, offer a variety of ways to help you meet your authorization goals.

### How can you protect my business from fraud?

Our AI-based fraud detection tools help protect your business from existing and evolving threats from fraudsters. We also offer Chargeback Protection and Dispute Management solutions.
