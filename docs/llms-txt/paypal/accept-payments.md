### Frequently asked questions

#### How can I accept payments?

Small- and medium-size businesses can start by setting up a [PayPal Business account](https://www.paypal.com/us/bizsignup/entry?product=ppcp). You'll just need to confirm a little info about your business and link a bank account to start accepting the most popular payment methods.

For larger businesses, give us a call at [1-844-385-9959](tel:18443859959) to get started.

#### What type of businesses can use PayPal for accepting payments?

We have payment solutions for online, in-person, and on-the-go businesses of all sizes. Reach out to one of our [account specialists](https://www.paypal.com/us/business/contact-sales) if you have any questions about how our payment solutions can benefit your business and your customers.

#### What payment methods can I accept with PayPal?

You can accept PayPal, Venmo (in the US), card payments, digital wallets, and installment payments. [See our full list of payment methods](https://www.paypal.com/us/business/accept-payments/payment-methods).

#### Do I need a website to accept PayPal?

No, you don't need a website to accept PayPal. Use [Pay Links and Buttons](https://www.paypal.com/us/business/accept-payments/payment-links) to create a link, button, or QR code for a specific product or service that you can share with customers in an email, social media post, or text. Or share your unique PayPal QR code to help customers make payments to your business.

#### How much are PayPal’s transaction fees?

Fees vary by transaction type. [Get more details about our fees](https://www.paypal.com/us/business/fees).

#### What currencies do you accept?

We are available in more than 200 global markets and support 130+ send currencies. Currency conversion fees may apply. [See our full list of currencies](https://developer.paypal.com/docs/reports/reference/paypal-supported-currencies/).

#### What type of customer support do you offer?

Small- and medium-size businesses have access to online support, a dedicated call center, and a resolution center with their Business account. [Visit our Help Center](https://www.paypal.com/us/cshelp/business) for more information.

Larger businesses using our enterprise solutions can contact their account manager or reach out for support in our [developer portal](https://developer.paypal.com/braintree/help).

#### Are your solutions PCI compliant?

We are fully PCI compliant, so the processing, storing, and transmitting of card data for your transactions will meet the PCI compliance standards. We also offer optional services that can help further reduce the compliance burden for your business. [Visit our Security Center](https://www.paypal.com/security/learn-about-paypal-secure-technology) for more information about our security technology.
