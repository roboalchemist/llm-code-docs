#### What is a crypto wallet?

Crypto wallets act as a digital storage solution for users to hold and manage their cryptocurrencies.

#### How do I use PayPal to buy and sell crypto through my external wallet?

1. Log in to your crypto wallet.
2. Tap Buy or Sell, then select ‘PayPal.’
3. Enter the USD amount worth of crypto you want to buy, or crypto quantity you want to sell. You’ll be redirected to PayPal to log in.
4. Review the transaction details and PayPal will manage the rest.

#### Tips to avoid crypto scams

1. PayPal will never ask for personal information such as passwords and security codes.
2. Secure your digital wallets to keep others from gaining access and stealing your crypto.
3. Make sure the website you are visiting is legitimate — watch out for typos, and misspelled words.

If you receive a suspicious message, forward it to [phishing@paypal.com](mailto:phishing@paypal.com), then delete it.
