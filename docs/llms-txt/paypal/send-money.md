## Frequently asked questions

### How do I send money online from my desktop computer?

1. Log in to PayPal
2. Log in to your account with PayPal.
3. Choose “Send & Request.”
4. Enter your recipient’s name, PayPal username, email, or mobile number. Choose “Next.”
5. Enter the amount, choose the currency, and add a note if you’d like. Select “Continue.”
6. Choose whether you’re “Paying for an item or Service” or “Sending to a friend.”
7. Select how you want to pay and choose “Next.”
8. Review the information and select “Send Payment Now.”

### How do I send money on the PayPal app?

1. Open the app and log in to your account
2. Choose 'Send and Request'
3. Enter your recipients name, PayPal username, email, or mobile number.
4. Enter the amount, choose the currency, and add a note if you wish. Select 'Send'
5. Choose payment type between 'Friends and Family' or 'Goods and Services'. Choose "Next"
6. Review the details and select 'Send'

### Can I send money as a gift?

Yes you can. Here’s how.

1. Choose “Send & Request.”
2. Select “Send.”
3. Enter your recipient’s name, PayPal username, email, or mobile number. Choose “Next.”
4. Enter the amount you want to gift.
5. Select “Change” next to “paying for an item or service.”
6. Choose the present icon. Select a card theme and add a personal note.
7. Select “Next.” Then choose “Continue” to confirm your transaction.

### Is there a fee to send money with PayPal?

It’s free to send money domestically to friends and family using your PayPal balance or bank account. Money sent from a credit or debit card will incur a 2.9% transaction fee plus a $0.30 flat fee.

International fees apply for money sent abroad. [Learn more about our fees here](https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/paypal-fees).
