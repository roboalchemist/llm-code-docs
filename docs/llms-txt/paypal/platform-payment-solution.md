## Frequently asked questions

### What is the cost of PayPal platform solutions?

Platform transaction rates and fees are negotiable across any of our integration models. Contact Sales to discuss the right pricing for your business.

### Where can I review technical documentation?

Technical documentation for our PayPal Hosted integration can be reviewed in our [developer docs](https://developer.paypal.com/docs/multiparty/). Fill out the form above to review documentation on our embedded solution.

### What support does PayPal offer platforms?

Partnering with PayPal opens the door to accelerated growth, optimized revenue, enhanced customer experiences, and access to new business opportunities. Our [PayPal Partners Program](https://www.paypal.com/us/enterprise/become-a-partner) offers:

- Onboarding and integration specialists as well as ongoing support
- Comprehensive technical documentation, access to APIs, SDKs, and sandbox testing
- Technical training and education sessions led by PayPal training experts
- Co-marketing opportunities with PayPal and access to a robust library of marketing toolkits with customizable marketing assets

### Are there any startup or monthly fees?

There are no upfront or monthly fees associated with our Platform integrations.

### Does the platform integration have mobile compatibility?

All of our integration models are optimized to function seamlessly across desktop and mobile (iOS and Android).

### Are all of your integrations future proofed?

PayPal keeps our integrations up to date and current with the latest trends and developments.
