## Frequently asked questions

### Can I donate money to charities through PayPal?

Yes, you can donate to charities, causes, and more through PayPal.

### Can I accept donations using a personal PayPal account?

Yes, donations may be accepted using a PayPal personal account. After taking the first step of starting a cause, we’ve got steps to get you on your way to becoming a confirmed PayPal charity. [Get started now](https://www.paypal.com/signin?returnUri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.paypal.com%2Fcharities&state=%2Fdashboard).

### Does PayPal charge fees to charities?

Qualified charities can receive a discounted rate. [Find out if you qualify](https://www.paypal.com/charities?_ga=2.74284562.141690627.1670871416-425547105.1668612986).

### Can PayPal be used for donations?

Yes, you can use PayPal to give and accept donations via the app or online.

### How do I accept donations with PayPal?

To accept donations with PayPal, set up a [Donate button](https://www.paypal.com/buttons/) and integrate it to your site. You can also receive donations through the [Venmo app](https://www.paypal.com/us/cshelp/article/enable-your-charity-to-receive-donations-via-the-venmo-app-help888).

### How much does PayPal charge for donations?

Charities that have been confirmed with PayPal get a processing rate of 1.99% + $0.49 per donation. Charities that aren’t confirmed with PayPal get a rate of is 2.89% + $0.49 per transaction. [Visit our pricing page](https://www.paypal.com/webapps/mpp/paypal-fees) for more information.

### How do I find a charity on PayPal?

You can find a confirmed charity on our [Donation Hub](https://www.paypal.com/fundraiser/hub).
