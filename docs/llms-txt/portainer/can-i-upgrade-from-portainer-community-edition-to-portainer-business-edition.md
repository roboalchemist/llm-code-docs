# Can I upgrade from Portainer Community Edition to Portainer Business Edition?

Yes, you can! The upgrade process will carry your existing configuration over to Portainer Business. See our [platform-specific upgrade instructions](https://docs.portainer.io/start/upgrade/tobe) for more information.
