# Policy Based Management

{% hint style="info" %}
This section is only available in Portainer Business Edition.
{% endhint %}

Within the Policy Based Management section you can configure policies for your environments.

If the Policy Based Management feature has not been enabled, clicking on items in this section will take you to the [Settings](https://docs.portainer.io/sts/admin/settings/general#additional-functionality) page where you can enable the feature.&#x20;
