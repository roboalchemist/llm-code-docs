# Licenses

Licensing in Portainer Business Edition is based on the [number of nodes](https://docs.portainer.io/faqs/licensing/what-is-a-node-for-licensing-purposes) you are managing.

<figure><img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/xdTQRpMuktD2l0URtOJO/blobs/9h5h6TyBRdZl2ek06qNU/2.32-licenses-info.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If you wish to renew your existing license you can do so by clicking the **Renew license** button.

You can also view a list of each license applied to your Portainer installation.

<figure><img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/xdTQRpMuktD2l0URtOJO/blobs/9GNJjuxQ4aVSwFKhH08p/2.32-licenses-list.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If you want to add more nodes to your environment than your license allows, you can [buy more nodes](https://docs.portainer.io/faqs/licensing/how-do-i-add-more-nodes-to-my-license).

## Add a new license

Once you have obtained your new license key, to add it to Portainer click **Add license**, enter your license key then click **Submit**.

<figure><img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/xdTQRpMuktD2l0URtOJO/blobs/GyjYrYstf0uhzoSdHJy0/2.20-licenses-add.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Remove a license

To remove a license (for example when you want to remove an expired license), tick the box next to the license and click **Remove**. You will be asked to confirm the removal.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Ensure you have enough licenses to cover the number of nodes you are using before committing to the removal. For more on how nodes are calculated, refer to [this article](https://docs.portainer.io/faqs/licensing/what-is-a-node-for-licensing-purposes).
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="https://content.gitbook.com/content/xdTQRpMuktD2l0URtOJO/blobs/xQdNHD1nyAMW06DsKdzg/2.19-licenses-remove.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To confirm, click **Remove**.
