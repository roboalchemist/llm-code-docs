# Walkthrough video

This walkthrough takes you through the full onboarding flow to deploy Stable Diffusion XL in your own cloud through Mystic.

Mystic's Bring Your Own Cloud (BYOC) allows you to run your ML models directly in a VPC on your own cloud to get GPUs at cost.

In a few simple steps, our platform authenticates with your cloud provider and instantly creates a cluster to deploy and run your ML models. You can think of Mystic’s BYOC platform as optimised ML infra with a serverless experience, but less pricey than an engineer managing it.
