# Introduction

The [**Plugin License Portal**](https://account.tokens.studio/) is where you manage your subscription, seats, and billing information.\
It is available for all organizations on the Starter Plus plan (formerly Tokens Studio for Figma Pro), and can be accessed by organization admins.

From the portal, you can:

* View your current subscription and plan details
* Add or remove seats for your team
* Assign or revoke access for team members
* Update payment methods and view invoices
* Change or upgrade your plan
