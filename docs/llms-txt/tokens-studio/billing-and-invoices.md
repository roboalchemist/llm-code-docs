# Billing & Invoices

The **Billing** section of the Account Portal gives you full control over your payment details and history.

***

#### Viewing invoices

* Go to the **Edit Billing** link in the Account Portal.
* You’ll see a list of past invoices, including amount, date, and payment status.
* Click on any invoice to download a PDF copy for your records.

***

#### Updating your payment method

1. Go to the **Edit Billing** tab, select **Update payment method**.
2. Enter new payment details (credit card, PayPal, etc. depending on support).
3. Save your changes — the new method will be used for all future charges.

***

#### Failed payments

* If a payment fails, you’ll receive an email notification.
* The system will automatically retry the payment.
* If repeated attempts fail, your subscription may be paused until the issue is resolved.

***

#### Billing cycles

* Your billing cycle is visible in the **Dashboard**.
* Any changes to seats or plan upgrades are reflected in the next invoice.
* Yearly subscribers will see a single invoice per year; monthly subscribers see one per month.

***

> 💡 *Tip: Always keep your billing details up to date to avoid interruptions in service.*
