# Overview of the Portal Dashboard

When you log into the Account Portal, you’ll see a dashboard that gives you a quick overview of your organization’s subscription.

#### What you’ll find on the dashboard

* **Current Plan** – shows which subscription tier your organization is on.
* **Seats** – how many seats are included in your plan, how many are currently in use, and how many are available.
* **Billing Summary** – your next billing date, payment method, and recent invoices.
* **Organization Settings** – options to manage team members, roles, and permissions.

#### Why it’s useful

The dashboard acts as your starting point. From here you can decide whether you need to:

* Add seats if your team is growing.
* Remove or reassign seats if people have left.
* Update payment information before the next billing cycle.

> 💡 *Tip: Keep an eye on the seat usage panel. If all seats are filled, new team members won’t be able to access until you add more.*
