# Ask AI (in Findings)

**Ask AI** is available directly inside the Findings view. It provides quick explanations and remediation guidance for an individual finding based on its context in your environment.

***

## What Ask AI Does

Ask AI helps you understand a specific finding by generating:

* A clear explanation of the issue
* The potential impact
* Practical remediation steps
* Additional context from Jit’s knowledge graph

This is useful for triage, developer support, and quick validation.

***

## How It Works

When you click **Ask AI**, Jit automatically provides the AI with:

* The finding details
* Severity and category
* The affected resource
* Relevant relationships from the knowledge graph

The response is tailored to your environment—not a generic security description.

***

## Example Questions

You can ask things like:

* *“Explain this finding.”*
* *“Why does it matter?”*
* *“How do I fix it?”*
* *“Could this be a false positive?”*

***

## Ask AI vs. Agentic Chat

* **Ask AI**: Focused on a *single finding* for explanation or remediation.
* **Agentic Chat**: Used for broader analysis, dashboards, and creating agents.

***

## Summary

Ask AI provides fast, contextual support for understanding and remediating individual findings.\
For broader questions or automated workflows, use **Agentic Chat** or create an agent.
