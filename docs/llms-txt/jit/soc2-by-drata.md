# SOC2 by Drata

# Overview

The **SOC2 by Drata** plan enables automated evidence collection for key technical SOC2 controls, enhancing your vulnerability management and Secure Development Lifecycle (SDLC) capabilities.

Once integrated, Jit will periodically sync relevant control evidence to your Drata account.
