# Integrating with Third-Party Products and Services

In addition to your source-code management platform, Jit enables you to integrate with a variety of third-party products and services— including notification platforms and cloud services.

> 📘 Note
>
> SCM integration (which is required for basic Jit functionality) is described in Getting Started with Jit.
