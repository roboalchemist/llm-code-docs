# Import teams

Allows manual management of your teams by importing data that contains team information, rather than syncing with external sources like GitHub.
 This operation performs the following actions: 
1. Deletes all existing teams and their associated resources and members.
2. Stops the auto sync from external sources like `GitHub`. 
3. Creates teams along with their associated resources and members, or adds new members/resources to existing teams from the uploaded file.

**Requires the following permission:**
`jit.teams.write`
