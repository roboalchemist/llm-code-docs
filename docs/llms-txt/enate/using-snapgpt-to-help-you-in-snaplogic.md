# Using SnapGPT to help you in SnapLogic

A very useful and powerful tool that you have at your disposal in SnapLogic is their own in-built [SnapGPT](https://docs.snaplogic.com/snapgpt/snapgpt-about.html).&#x20;

<figure><img src="https://3859925423-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MWYnDNwe3Cuo4zlGbs5-887967055%2Fuploads%2FPfsfWEWBYFQANCK8wnuv%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=147a70c1-fa50-4080-b6e6-ba35817ec295" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can have SnapGPT carry out essential any and all tasks in SnapLogic. You can ask it to create or configure a specific Snap for you, identify why your pipeline may not be working or even create a pipeline from scratch for you.

You can also have SnapGPT carryout more contextual skills such as generating expressions or SQLs. SnapGPT can also answer any questions you have about using SnapLogic and retrieve any useful SnapLogic documentation.
