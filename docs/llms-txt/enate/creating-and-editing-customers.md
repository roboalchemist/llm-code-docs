# Creating and Editing Customers

## Creating Customers <a href="#a-creating-customers" id="a-creating-customers"></a>

To add Customer companies, click on the '+' icon at the top of the Service Matrix and select 'Customer'.

![](https://3859925423-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-legacy-files/o/assets%2F-MWYnDNwe3Cuo4zlGbs5%2F-MWoypV3sAJh_u70lHP7%2F-MWoywzTs-Eh6t8nZHAr%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=3e0335e6-7487-4f5a-b4ec-ace1c5e30861)

Fill in the customer settings in the resulting popup.

## **Subsequent Editing** <a href="#c-subsequent-editing" id="c-subsequent-editing"></a>

To edit a Customer’s settings, click on the ellipses beside the Customer name and select the Edit link. Make your desired changes in the resulting popup and click OK to save changes.

![](https://3859925423-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-legacy-files/o/assets%2F-MWYnDNwe3Cuo4zlGbs5%2F-MWoypV3sAJh_u70lHP7%2F-MWozA3EbH8m4oG__jly%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=eb6f0152-28d6-442b-af57-0c2c6383aab2)

You can see what edits have been made to the customer and when, as well as when the customer was created, and even when a customer was deleted by clicking on the Show Activity button in the Edit screen.

![](https://3859925423-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-legacy-files/o/assets%2F-MWYnDNwe3Cuo4zlGbs5%2F-MWoypV3sAJh_u70lHP7%2F-MWozGjVg_a8ahg0g8__%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=6d911579-eaac-4008-98f9-f2baf49c6389)

## Cloning a Customer

You are also able to clone a customer by clicking on the Clone button in the Edit screen. This will clone all of the customer's settings apart from its name. You are able to make changes to any of the settings once the customer has been cloned.

## Deleting a Customer

To delete a customer, click on the ellipses beside the Customer, hover over the delete link and click on 'Customer'.

![](https://3859925423-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-legacy-files/o/assets%2F-MWYnDNwe3Cuo4zlGbs5%2F-MWoypV3sAJh_u70lHP7%2F-MWozCmyk2X3Wu51LLOQ%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=cafde716-8e30-49ae-af35-3b41b87e4723)

If you delete a Customer which has linked [Email Routes](https://docs.enate.net/enate-help/builder/builder-2021.1/email-mailbox-configuration/email-routes), you will be notified of this and will need to update the respective Email Routes in order to stop them from creating more work for this Customer.
