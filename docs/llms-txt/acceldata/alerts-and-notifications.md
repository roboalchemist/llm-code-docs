# Alerts & Notifications

ADOC makes it easy to stay on top of the health and performance of your data by providing **alerts and notifications**. Instead of manually checking dashboards, you can configure ADOC to notify you whenever important events occur—such as data quality issues, compute inefficiencies, or policy violations.

Alerts ensure that teams are **proactively informed** about problems before they affect downstream processes or business outcomes. Notifications can be tailored by severity, delivery channel, or feature area (for example, reliability or compute) so the right people receive the right information at the right time.

By using alerts and notifications, organizations can reduce response time, minimize disruptions, and maintain confidence in the reliability and efficiency of their data ecosystem.
