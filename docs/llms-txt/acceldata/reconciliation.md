# Reconciliation

Reconciliation policies help you compare data between two tables to ensure that they remain in sync — a critical step for financial, operational, or regulatory use cases.

Use this policy when you want to:

- Reconcile data between a **source** and **target** table (e.g., staging vs. production, upstream vs. downstream).
- Detect missing or mismatched records early in your pipelines.
- Guarantee end-to-end data integrity across systems.

These APIs allow you to integrate reconciliation directly into your data quality monitoring pipelines.

- [Create and List Policies](https://docs.acceldata.io/acceldata-data-observability-cloud/api/create-and-list-reconciliation-policies)
- [Manage Reconciliation Policy by ID](https://docs.acceldata.io/acceldata-data-observability-cloud/api/manage-reconciliation-policy-by-id)
- [Manage Reconciliation Policy by Name](https://docs.acceldata.io/acceldata-data-observability-cloud/api/manage-reconciliation-policy-by-name)
- [Policy Executions and Results](https://docs.acceldata.io/acceldata-data-observability-cloud/api/reconciliation-policy-executions---results)
