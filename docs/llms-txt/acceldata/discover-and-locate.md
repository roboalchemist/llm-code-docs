# Discover and Locate

The first step in working with assets in ADOC is to **find the right datasets across all your connected sources**. The Discover and Search APIs let you locate assets by ID, name, or UID, or fetch lists of assets with filters for pagination, profiled assets, or watched assets. This gives you a single, unified way to explore data across warehouses, lakes, and streaming systems without needing to know exact locations in advance.
