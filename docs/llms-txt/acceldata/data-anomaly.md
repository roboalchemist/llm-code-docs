# Data Anomaly

A **Data Anomaly Policy** automatically detects unexpected or abnormal behavior in your data profiles over time.
 This includes anomalies in:

- Record counts
- Value distributions
- Null patterns
- Outliers
- Statistical deviations from historical baselines

This policy helps you identify **sudden issues in dataset behavior** before they cascade into downstream failures or impact business dashboards.
