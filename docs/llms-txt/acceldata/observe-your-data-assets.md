# Observe Your Data Assets

## Visualize Your Data Quality Summary

To view your first data quality report, navigate to  **Reliability** -&gt;  **Reporting** -&gt; **New Report** on the top right corner of the page. Click **Save** from the top of the page, providing a report name.

![](https://uploads.developerhub.io/prod/Yoq2/a170nj7cyc2oib4gr878eqw9xmwp91fzo0ok509one9mqka44dp06pdxx5xmhhat.png)

## Visualize Your Policies

The **Policy Summary** page provides a graphical representation of the information about all your policies.

To view the **Policy Summary** page, navigate to **Reliability** in the left navigation menu, click **Policy Summary** under **Reports** section.

![](https://uploads.developerhub.io/prod/Yoq2/jllsgh1cgnvwqwbx0is8dyy3l1f1bzlqvxrc2v8qa46nnc9s4aq6iubkk8bstx3k.png)

## Discover Your Data Assets

The **Discover Assets** page in ADOC helps you to explore, monitor, and manage all your data assets.

To view the **Discover Assets** page, navigate to **Reliability** in the left navigation menu, click  **Discover Assets.** 

![](https://uploads.developerhub.io/prod/Yoq2/29cl5hijf1cdmtpiwicvoz4v11dau7o0gpwn3gt8ntmr86holqjluztpy23yh9ij.png)
