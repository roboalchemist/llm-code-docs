# Manage Policies

ADOC goes beyond simply applying and monitoring policies. It also provides tools to **manage, organize, and reuse policies efficiently** across teams and environments.

These capabilities reduce duplication of effort, improve governance, and make it easier for business users to maintain large sets of rules.

Key features include:

- [Import and Export Policies](https://docs.acceldata.io/acceldata-data-observability-cloud/documentation/import-and-export-policies)**:** Share or reuse policies across tenants and environments.
- [Policy Groups](https://docs.acceldata.io/acceldata-data-observability-cloud/documentation/policy-groups)**:** Bundle related policies together for easier organization and management.
- [User Defined Templates](https://docs.acceldata.io/acceldata-data-observability-cloud/documentation/user-defined-templates)**:** Define custom reusable functions (business rules or transformations) once and apply them across multiple policies.
- Deletion of Policies: To delete a policy, click the **Delete**  icon in the Policies table. A confirmation dialog box appears where you can optionally add a reason for the deletion for audit purposes.
