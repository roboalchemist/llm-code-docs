# Referral Program

> Invite friends to Base44 and earn bonus credits together.

## Inviting friends to Base44 and earning credits

Share your referral link to invite friends to Base44. They get 10 bonus credits when they sign up, and you earn 10 credits once they publish their first app.

You can invite up to 5 friends who are eligible for referral credits.

**To share your referral link:**

1. Click the **Gift** icon at the top right of your workspace.
2. Click **Copy** to copy your referral link.
3. Share the link with your friends.

<Note>
  **Notes:**

  * Only new Base44 accounts are eligible for referral credits, and you can earn referral credits for up to 5 friends in total.
  * Check your friends' email addresses carefully before they sign up with your link. Referral emails and credits cannot be edited or reset later, and the Base44 team cannot change or reissue referral links.
</Note>

## FAQs

Click a question below to learn more about the referral program.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many credits does my friend get when joining with my link?">
    They receive **10 bonus credits** immediately after signing up with your referral link, as long as they are creating a new Base44 account and you have not reached the 5 referral limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When do I receive my credits?">
    You earn **10 credits** once your friend publishes their first app on Base44.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many people can I invite?">
    You can invite up to 5 friends with your referral link. After 5 successful referrals, your link no longer gives bonus credits to new signups.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who qualifies as a new user?">
    Anyone who has never created a Base44 account before is considered new and is eligible for referral credits, as long as they sign up through a live referral link that has not reached the 5 referral limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do referral credits renew each month?">
    No, referral credits do not renew each month. At your monthly renewal date, your message credits reset to the standard credits that come with the plan you are on, regardless of any additional credits you received through referrals.

    For example, if your plan includes 25 monthly message credits and you earn 10 extra credits from referrals (for a total of 35 credits), your balance resets to 25 credits at the start of the next renewal period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I reset my referral link or fix a typo?">
    No. Once your referral link has been used or has reached the 5 referral limit, it cannot be reset or changed.

    If a friend signs up with the wrong email address or without using your link, referral credits cannot be added or moved later, and the Base44 team cannot adjust these referrals manually.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
