# Pricing

> Backend service pricing and credit usage

## Backend service pricing

The Base44 backend service is currently in **beta** and **free to use**. There is no separate backend subscription or additional charges for using backend features.

### How it works

Backend operations use your workspace's **integration credits**, which are included in all Base44 plans. If you need higher credit limits or additional features, you can upgrade to one of the regular Base44 plans.
